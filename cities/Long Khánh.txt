'''Long Khánh''' is a [[Provincial city (Vietnam)|city]] of [[Đồng Nai Province]] in the [[Southeast (Vietnam)|Southeast]] region of [[Vietnam]]. It covers an area of  194&nbsp;km² and had a population of 151,467 in 2019.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Vietnam: Administrative Division (Provinces and Districts) - Population Statistics, Charts and Map |url=https://www.citypopulation.de/en/vietnam/admin/ |access-date=2024-02-07 |website=www.citypopulation.de}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|publisher=Tổng Cục Thống kê Việt Nam (General Statistics Office of Vietnam)|title=Đơn vị hành chính, mã 732 (English: administrative units, code 732)|url=https://www.gso.gov.vn/phuong-phap-luan-thong-ke/danh-muc/don-vi-hanh-chinh/}}</ref>

Long Khánh city's predecessor was Long Khánh [[District-level town (Vietnam)|town]], which founded in 2003. It was recognized as a city in 2019.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Việt Nam News|title=Đồng Nai Province has new city of Long Khánh|url=https://vietnamnews.vn/society/520833/dong-nai-province-has-new-city-of-long-khanh.html}}</ref>

==Geography==
Long Khánh is located entirely in Đồng Nai territory. It shares borders with:<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Long Khánh city official website|title=Giới thiệu Thành phố Long Khánh (English: About Long Khánh city)|url=http://longkhanh.dongnai.gov.vn/Pages/Gioithieu_chitiet.aspx?CatID=2}}</ref>
*[[Xuân Lộc district]] to the east
*[[Thống Nhất district]] to the west
*[[Cẩm Mỹ district]] to the south
*[[Xuân Lộc district]], [[Thống Nhất district]] and [[Định Quán district]] to the North

Long Khánh has 15 divisions (11 wards and 4 commune), include: 

*Ward: Xuân An, Xuân Bình, Xuân Hòa, Xuân Trung, Xuân Thanh, Phú Bình, Bảo Vinh, Suối Tre, Xuân Lập, Bàu Sen, Xuân Tân
*Commune: Bảo Quang, Hàng Gòn, Bình Lộc, Bàu Trâm.

==Sights==
*Saint Joseph Great Seminary of Xuân Lộc is the seventh major seminary of the Catholic Church in Vietnam
*Cathedral of Christ the King (Cathedral of Long Khánh)
*Monument of Victory
* Hang Gon dolmen tomb (Hàng Gòn, Long Khánh)
* Chua Chan mountain (Xuân Lộc, Long Khánh)

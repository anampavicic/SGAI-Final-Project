'''Vĩnh Yên''' ({{IPA|vi|vǐɲˀ ʔīən|lang|Vinh Yen.ogg}}) is the city capital of [[Vĩnh Phúc Province]], in the [[Red River Delta]] region of northern [[Vietnam]]. As of census 2019, the population is 119,128 people, the area is 50.78&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>.<ref>General Statistics Office of Vietnam (web)</ref>

This city hosted [[2012 Asian Men's Cup Volleyball Championship]].

'''Thủ Dầu Một''' ({{Audio|Thu Dau Mot.ogg|listen|help=no}}) is the capital city of [[Bình Dương province]], Vietnam, located at around {{coord|10|58|0|N|106|39|0|E|region:VN_type:city}}. The city has an area of 118.91&nbsp;km², with a population of 336.705 (as of 2021),<ref>{{Cite web |title=Thủ Dầu Một (District-level City, Bình Dương, Vietnam) - Population Statistics, Charts, Map and Location |url=https://www.citypopulation.de/en/vietnam/admin/b%C3%ACnh_d%C6%B0%C6%A1ng/718__th%E1%BB%A7_d%E1%BA%A7u_m%E1%BB%99t/ |access-date=2024-02-07 |website=www.citypopulation.de}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://vanban.chinhphu.vn/portal/page/portal/chinhphu/hethongvanban?class_id=2&_page=1&mode=detail&document_id=158454 |title=Nghị quyết số 11/NQ-CP của Chính phủ : Về việc thành lập thành phố Thủ Dầu Một thuộc tỉnh Bình Dương |date=2012 |website=vanban.chinhphu.vn |access-date=2019-07-10}}</ref> and is located 20&nbsp;km north of downtown [[Ho Chi Minh City]], on the left bank of the [[Saigon River]], upstream from the city.

==Geography==
Thu Dau Mot lies to the south of [[Bình Dương province]] and is contiguous with [[Saigon River]]:
* To the east is [[Tân Uyên, Bình Dương|Tân Uyên]]
* To the west is [[Củ Chi district]], [[Ho Chi Minh City]]
* To the south is [[Thuận An]]
* To the north is [[Bến Cát]]

==Etymology==
In the past, some researchers stated that the name "Thu Dau Mot" had originated from Cambodian language, but most of people claimed that the name "Thu Dau Mot" was a Vietnamese phrase, which combines two phrases that are Thu (means ''keep'') and Dau Mot, whose the first word is a tree called [[dipterocarpaceae]] and the second one is a number. According to rumors, in a defense base (literally means "Thu") in [[Bình An, Bình Dương|Bình An]], there was a tree on the hill (tree here is a kind of species in [[dipterocarpaceae]], and it is called "Dau" in Vietnamese language), "Mot" is number one in [[Vietnamese language]], so the name "Thu Dau Mot" was born, which means "one-tree-defense" literally.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sugia.vn/home|title=Historical linguistics in Binh Duong}}</ref>

==Economy==
Thủ Dầu Một has seen a rapid expansion and economic development since 1997, as the province has become an important industrial hub of the region. In January 2007, the [[district-level town]] then was officially recognized as a third-class town.<ref>[http://baobinhduong.org.vn/newsdetails/1D3FE18A201/Len_thanh_pho_nguoi_dan_duoc_huong_rat_nhieu_loi_ich.aspx Lên thành phố, người dân được hưởng rất nhiều lợi ích] Quyết định số 115/QĐ-BXD ngày 23/1/2007 công nhận thành phố Thủ Dầu Một là đô thị loại III</ref> It became a city in July 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.gov.vn/Home/Thu-Dau-Mot-becomes-city/20125/14261.vgp|title=Thu Dau Mot becomes city|website=news.gov.vn|access-date=21 April 2018}}</ref> There is a 4,200-ha urban-tech park under development. Thủ Dầu Một will be a modern city within the [[Ho Chi Minh City metropolitan area|Hồ Chí Minh City Metropolitan Area]] (including Thủ Dầu Một, [[Nhơn Trạch district|Nhơn Trạch]], [[Tân An]], [[Biên Hòa]] and parts of [[Đồng Nai province|Đồng Nai]] and [[Bình Dương province|Bình Dương]] provinces).

It became a first-class city on 6 December 2017.<ref name=" thuvienphapluat.vn">{{cite web | author= thuvienphapluat.vn | title=Quyết định 1959/QĐ-TTg 2017 công nhận Thủ Dầu Một là đô thị loại I trực thuộc Bình Dương | website=THƯ VIỆN PHÁP LUẬT _ Tra cứu, Nắm bắt Pháp Luật Việt Nam | url=https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Xay-dung-Do-thi/Quyet-dinh-1959-QD-TTg-2017-cong-nhan-Thu-Dau-Mot-la-do-thi-loai-I-truc-thuoc-Binh-Duong-369142.aspx | language=vi | access-date=November 8, 2019}}</ref>


Thủ Dầu Một is home to [https://wtcbinhduong.vn/en/ World Trade Center Binh Duong New City], a [https://e.vnexpress.net/news/business/new-mice-center-to-open-in-binh-duong-4494978.html destination] for international trade activities in the Southern Key Economic Region of Vietnam. This development is a milestone for the city, as it attracts domestic and international visitors to the city and significantly boosts business in the region.

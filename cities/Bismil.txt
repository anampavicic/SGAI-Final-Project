'''Bismil''' ({{langx|ku|Bismil}})<ref>{{Cite book |last=Avcıkıran |first=Adem |url= |title=Kürtçe Anamnez, Anamneza bi Kurmancî |year=2009 |page=55 |language=ku, tr}}</ref> is a municipality and [[Districts of Turkey|district]] of [[Diyarbakır Province]], [[Turkey]].<ref>[https://www.e-icisleri.gov.tr/Anasayfa/MulkiIdariBolumleri.aspx Büyükşehir İlçe Belediyesi], Turkey Civil Administration Departments Inventory. Retrieved 12 July 2023.</ref> Its area is 1,679&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.harita.gov.tr/uploads/files-folder/il_ilce_alanlari.xlsx|title=İl ve İlçe Yüz ölçümleri|publisher=General Directorate of Mapping|access-date=12 July 2023}}</ref> and its population is 118,698 (2022).<ref name=tuik>{{Cite web |title=Address-based population registration system (ADNKS) results dated 31 December 2022, Favorite Reports|url=https://biruni.tuik.gov.tr/medas/?kn=95&locale=en |access-date=12 July 2023|publisher=[[TÜİK]]|language=en|format=XLS}}</ref> The district was established on 4 January 1936.<ref>{{Cite web |title=İl İdaresi ve Mülki Bölümler Şube Müdürlüğü İstatistikleri - İl ve İlçe Kuruluş Tarihleri |url=https://www.icisleri.gov.tr/kurumlar/icisleri.gov.tr/IcSite/illeridaresi/İstatistiki%20Bilgiler/İl%20İdaresi%20ve%20Mülki%20Bölümler/il%20ilce%20kurulus%20tarihleri_2019.pdf |access-date=27 December 2022 |page=28 |language=tr}}</ref>

The pre-2013 municipality was divided into the neighborhoods of Akpınar, Altıok, Dicle, Dumlupınar, Esentepe, Fatih, Fırat, Kurtuluş, Sanayi, Seyrantepe, Şentepe, Tekel and Ulutürk.

== History ==
On the Körtik hill exists an archeological site.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.hurriyetdailynews.com/kortik-hill-offers-thousands-of-artifacts-116813|title=Körtik Hill offers thousands of artifacts|website=Hürriyet Daily News|language=en|access-date=2019-11-05}}</ref>

== Politics ==
In the elections on the 31 March 2019 Orhan Ayaz from the [[Peoples' Democratic Party (Turkey)|Peoples' Democratic Party]] was elected as Mayor.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.yenisafak.com/yerel-secim-2019/diyarbakir-bismil-ilcesi-secim-sonuclari|title=Diyarbakır Bismil Seçim Sonuçları – Bismil Yerel Seçim Sonuçları|last=Şafak|first=Yeni|date=2019-05-11|website=Yeni Şafak|language=tr-TR|access-date=2019-11-05}}</ref> But in October he got dismissed due to an investigation.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://mezopotamyaajansi23.com/en/ALL-NEWS/content/view/73883|title=OPeration against co-mayors: Mızraklı, Yılmaz and Ayaz under custody|website=mezopotamyaajansi23.com|access-date=2019-11-05}}</ref> A trustee was appointed instead.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://anfenglish.com/news/trustees-appointed-to-4-hdp-run-city-councils-38648|title=Trustees appointed to 4 HDP-run city councils|website=ANF News|language=en|date=22 October 2019|access-date=2021-12-18}}</ref>

'''Bharatpur''' is a city in the Indian state of [[Rajasthan]], {{cvt|180|km}} south of India's capital, [[New Delhi]], {{cvt|178|km}} from Rajasthan's capital [[Jaipur]], {{cvt|55|km}} west of [[Agra]] of [[Uttar Pradesh]] and {{cvt|38|km}} from [[Mathura]] of [[Uttar Pradesh]]. It is the administrative headquarters of [[Bharatpur District]] and the headquarters of [[Bharatpur Division]] of [[Rajasthan State]]. Bharatpur is part of [[National Capital Region]] of [[India]].<ref name="The Hindu">{{cite news|title=NCR expanded to include Bhiwani, Bharatpur |url=http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-national/tp-newdelhi/ncr-expanded-to-include-bhiwani-bharatpur/article4871656.ece |location=Chennai, India |work=The Hindu |date=2 July 2013}}</ref> The city was the capital of the [[Bharatpur State]]. It became a [[municipal corporation]] with 65 wards in 2014.<ref name="Business Standard">{{cite news|title=Bharatpur to be a Municipal Corporation |url=https://www.business-standard.com/article/pti-stories/bharatpur-to-be-a-municipal-corporation-114021900035_1.html |location=Jaipur, India |work=Business Standard |date=19 Feb 2014}}</ref><ref name="News 18">{{cite news|title=Vasundhara's cabinet decides to make Bharatpur a Municipal Corporation |url=https://www.news18.com/news/india/vasundharas-cabinet-decides-to-make-bharatpur-a-municipal-corporation-669274.html) |location=Jaipur, India |work=News 18 |date=19 Feb 2014}}</ref><ref name="Outlook India">{{cite news|title=Rajasthan Govt to Make Bharatpur a Municipal Corporation |url=https://www.outlookindia.com/newswire/story/rajasthan-govt-to-make-bharatpur-a-municipal-corporation/829525) |location=Jaipur, India |work=Outlook India |date=19 Feb 2014}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=25 January 2022|title=भरतपुर के कुछ चोर लन्दन में सप्प्लाई हो रही चीनी को रास्ते में ही गायब कर दिया।|url=https://www.khabarbalotra.com/2022/01/blog-post_27.html|access-date=25 January 2022|website=Khabar Balotra|language=hi}}</ref>

'''Shikarpur district''' ({{langx|sd|شڪارپور ضلعو}}, {{langx|ur|{{Nastaliq|ضلع شكارپور}}}}), is a district in [[Larkana Division]] of [[Sindh]] province in [[Pakistan]]. 

The city of [[Shikarpur, Pakistan|Shikarpur]] is the district headquarters. There are 4 talukas: [[Lakhi, Sindh|Lakhi]], [[Garhi Yasin]], [[Khanpur, Sindh|Khanpur Tehsil]] and Shikarpur Tehsil itself. It is spread over an area of 2,512&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>.<ref name="2017census"/><ref>{{Cite web|title=Population of District Shikarpur Census 2017 Information 2022|url=http://www.pakinformation.com/population/shikarpur.html|access-date=2022-02-15|website=www.pakinformation.com}}</ref>

==Geography==
[[File:Shikarpur.jpg|thumb|An old building architecture of Shikarpur.|left]]Shikarpur district borders [[Larkana]], [[Jacobabad]], [[Khairpur District|Khairpur]] and [[Sukkur]]. Two National Highways (N-65 & N-55) intersect the city of Shikarpur making it the junction points of 4 provinces.

==Administrative divisions==
The district of Shikarpur is sub-divided into four [[List of tehsils of Sindh|Tehsils]] these are:<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20060220153405/http://www.lgdsindh.com.pk/shikarpur2.htm DISTRICT GOVERNMENT Shikarpur]</ref>
* [[Garhi Yasin Taluka|Garhi Yasin]]<ref name="2017census"/>
* [[Khanpur, Sindh|Khanpur]]
* [[Lakhi, Sindh|Lakhi]]
* [[Shikarpur, Pakistan|Shikarpur]]<ref name="2017census"/>

At the time of the 2017 census, Shikarpur had a sex ratio of 941 females per 1000 males and a literacy rate of 40.48%: 51.65% for males and 28.79% for females. 304,441 (24.68%) lived in urban areas. 428,885 (34.76%) were under 10 years of age.<ref name="2017census">{{cite web |title=District And Tehsil Level Population (Census - 2017) - Shikarpur District |url=http://www.pbscensus.gov.pk:80/sites/default/files/bwpsr/sindh/SHIKARPUR_SUMMARY.pdf|access-date=22 January 2024| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180328214917/http://www.pbscensus.gov.pk:80/sites/default/files/bwpsr/sindh/SHIKARPUR_SUMMARY.pdf|archive-date=28 March 2018 |url-status=dead|website=[[Pakistan Bureau of Statistics]]}}</ref> In 2023, the district had 214,901 households and a population of 1,386,330.<ref name="2023census">{{cite web |date=2023 |title=TABLE 1 : HOUSEHOLDS, POPULATION, HOUSEHOLD SIZE AND ANNUAL GROWTH RATE |url=https://www.pbs.gov.pk/sites/default/files/population/2023/Sindh.pdf|publisher=[[Pakistan Bureau of Statistics]]}}</ref>

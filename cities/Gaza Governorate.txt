The '''Gaza Governorate''' ({{langx|ar|محافظة غزة|Muḥāfaẓat Ġazza}}), also alternatively known as '''Gaza City Governorate''', is one of the 16 [[Governorates of Palestine]], located in the north central [[Gaza Strip]]. Gaza is claimed by the [[State of Palestine]], but the land is [[Governance of the Gaza Strip|under the control]] of [[Hamas]],{{Efn|Designated as a terrorist group by Australia, Canada, the European Union, Israel, Japan, Paraguay, the United Kingdom, and the United States|name=Hamas}} while its border with Israel, airspace and maritime territory are controlled by the [[Israel Defense Forces|IDF]]. According to the [[Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics]], the district's population was 505,700 in 2006. All of its seats were won by Hamas members in the 2006 parliamentary elections. It is governed by Mohammed Qadoura.

The governorate consists of one city, three towns and a number of refugee camps.

The '''Saint-Louis Region''' ({{Langx|fr|Région de Saint-Louis}}) of [[Senegal]] is on the border with [[Mauritania]]. Its capital is [[Saint-Louis, Senegal|Saint-Louis]].

Famous for the [[cast iron]] [[bridge]] in its capital, built by [[France|French]] colonialists in the 19th century, the region includes the [[Djoudj National Bird Sanctuary]], home to thousands of birds, some indigenous to the area.

==Départements==
Saint-Louis region is divided into 3 [[Department (country subdivision)|départements]]:
*[[Dagana Department, Senegal|Dagana Département]]
*[[Podor Department|Podor Département]]
*[[Saint-Louis Department|Saint-Louis Département]]

==Geography==
Saint-Louis is traversed by the northwesterly line of equal latitude and longitude.

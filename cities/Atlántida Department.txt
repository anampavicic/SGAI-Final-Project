'''Atlántida''' ({{IPA|es|aˈtlantiða}}) is a [[Departments of Honduras|department]] located on the north [[Caribbean Sea|Caribbean]] shore of [[Honduras]]. The capital is the [[seaport|port]] city of [[La Ceiba]].

In the past few decades, tourism has become the most important legitimate economic source for the coastal area. In 2005 it had an estimated population of about 372,532 people. The department covers a total surface area of 4,251&nbsp;km².

==History==
{{Unreferenced section|date=January 2023}}
{{stack|[[File:Land registry.JPG|thumb|200px|right|Land Registry Office for the Atlántida department in [[La Ceiba]]]]}}
The department was formed in 1902 from territory previously parts of the departments of [[Colón Department, Honduras|Colón]], [[Cortés Department|Cortés]], and [[Yoro Department|Yoro]]. In 1910 it had a population of about 11,370 people.{{cn|date=August 2023}}

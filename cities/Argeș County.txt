'''Argeș County''' ({{IPA|ro|ˈardʒeʃ|-|Ro-Argeș.ogg}}) is a county (''[[județ]]'') of [[Romania]], in [[Muntenia]], with the capital city at [[Pitești]].

== Demographics ==
At the [[2021 Romanian census|2021 census]], the county had a population of 569,932 and the population density was {{Pop density|569932|6862|km2|sqmi|prec=1}}. At the [[2011 Romanian census|2011 census]], it had a population of 612,431 and the population density was {{Pop density|612431|6862|km2|sqmi|prec=1}}.<ref name="INSSER">{{cite web|url=http://www.recensamantromania.ro/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/sR_TAB_3.xlsx |title=Population at 20 October 2011 |date=5 July 2013 |access-date=9 July 2013 |publisher=[[National Institute of Statistics (Romania)|INSSE]] |language=ro }}{{dead link|date=October 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

* [[Romani people in Romania|Roma]] (Gypsies) and [[Minorities of Romania|other ethnic groups]] – 3%

==Geography==
[[File:Argeş County Romania bgiu.jpg|thumb|left|Landscape in central and southern Argeș County]]
This county has a total area of {{cvt|6862|km2}}. The landforms can be split into 3 distinctive parts. In the north side there are the mountains, from the [[Southern Carpathians]] group – the [[Făgăraș Mountains]] with [[Moldoveanu Peak]] (2,544 m), [[Negoiu Peak]] (2,535 m) and Vânătoarea lui Buteanu peak (2,508 m) towering the region, and in the North-East part the [[Leaotă Mountains]]. Between them there is a pass towards [[Brașov]], the [[Rucăr–Bran Pass]]. The heights decrease, and in the center there are the sub-carpathian hills, with heights around {{cvt|800|m}}, crossed with very deep valleys. In the south there is the northern part of the [[Wallachian Plain]].

The main river that crosses the county is the [[Argeș River]] in which almost all the other rivers coming from the mountains flow. In the south the main rivers are the [[Vedea River]] and the [[Teleorman River]].

===Neighbours===
*[[Dâmbovița County]] in the east.
*[[Vâlcea County]] and [[Olt County]] in the west.
*[[Sibiu County]] and [[Brașov County]] in the north.
*[[Teleorman County]] in the south.

==Economy==
{{Romanian counties map|width=250px}}
The county is one of the most industrialized counties in [[Romania]]. There is one oil refinery and two automobile plants at [[Mioveni]] – the [[Automobile Dacia|Dacia]] Renault car plant, and at [[Câmpulung]] the [[ARO (company)|ARO]] plant.

The predominant industries in the county are:
* Automotive
* Chemical
* Electrical equipment
* Home appliances
* Food
* Textiles
* Construction materials

Oil is being extracted in the center and in the south. Also there are a few coal mines and close to Mioveni there is a nuclear research and production facility making nuclear fuels for the [[Cernavodă]] [[nuclear power plant|Nuclear Electric Power Plant]]. On the [[Argeș River]] there are a great number of hydroelectric power plants, the most impressive being the [[Vidraru]] power plant and dam.

The hillsides are well suited for wines and fruit orchards, and the south is suited for cereal crops.

==Tourism==
The main tourist destinations are:
* The city of Pitești
* The city of [[Curtea de Argeș]], where [[Curtea de Argeș Cathedral|one of the most well known monasteries]] in Romania is located
* The [[Câmpulung]] – [[Rucăr]] area
* The [[Făgăraș Mountains]] – the [[Transfăgărășan]]
* The [[Leaota Mountains]]
* The [[Poienari Castle]]
* The [[Cotmeana]] monastery.

The county was located in the central-southern part of the [[Greater Romania]], in the western part of the historic [[Muntenia]] region. Its territory comprised a large part of the current county, and a piece of the western part of the present [[Vâlcea County]]. It was bordered on the west by the counties of [[Olt County|Olt]] and Vâlcea, to the north by the counties [[Făgăraș County|Făgăraș]] and [[Sibiu County|Sibiu]], to the east by the counties [[Muscel County|Muscel]] and [[Dâmbovița County|Dâmbovița]], and to the south by the counties [[Teleorman County|Teleorman]] and [[Vlașca County|Vlașca]].

=== Population ===
According to the 1930 census data, the county population was 257,378 inhabitants, out of which 97.6% were ethnic Romanians.<ref name=Rec_1930_16>Recensământul general al populației României din 29 decemvrie 1930, Vol. II, pag. 16-17</ref> From the religious point of view, the population was 99.1% Eastern Orthodox, 0.3% Roman Catholic, 0.3% Jewish, as well as other minorities.

==== Urban population ====
In 1930, the county's urban population was 26,341 inhabitants, comprising 90.4% Romanians, 2.2% Jews, 2.0% Hungarians, 1.7% Romanies, 1.1% Germans, as well as other minorities.<ref name=Rec_1930_16/> From the religious point of view, the urban population was composed of 93.0% Eastern Orthodox, 2.4% Roman Catholic, 2.4% Jewish, 0.7% Reformed, 0.7% Lutheran, as well as other minorities.

'''Hasilpur''' ([[Punjabi language|Punjabi]] and {{langx|ur|{{Nastaliq|حاصل پور}}}}), is a city in [[Hasilpur Tehsil]] of [[Bahawalpur District]] in [[Punjab, Pakistan]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thepostalcodes.net/hasil-pur-mandi/|title=Hasil Pur Mandi Postal code / Zip codes for Hasil Pur Mandi|date=July 12, 2019|website=THE POSTAL CODES}}</ref> The city is located between the [[Satluj]] River and the [[India]]n border and lies {{cvt|96|km}} east of the district [[Bahawalpur]].

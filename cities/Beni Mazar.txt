'''Beni Mazar''' ({{Langx|ar|بَنِي مَزَار}}) is a rural [[List of cities and towns in Egypt|town]] in [[Egypt]]. It is located in the [[Minya Governorate]], on the west bank of the [[Nile]]. The older name of the town is '''Shinwada''' ({{Langx|ar|شِنُوادة}} or شِنُوَدة) which comes from Sahidic {{Langx|cop|ϭⲓⲛⲟⲩⲟⲟⲧⲉ}} (Bohairic: ϣⲓⲛⲟⲩⲟϯ)<ref>{{Cite book|last=Maher Ishak|first=Emile|title=The phonetics and phonology of the Bohairic dialect of Coptic and the survival of Coptic words in the colloquial and Classical Arabic of Egypt and of Coptic grammatical constructions in colloquial Egyptian Arabic}}</ref> meaning "kailyard".<ref>{{Cite book|last=Peust|first=Carsten|title=Die Toponyme vorarabischen Ursprungs im modernen Ägypten}}</ref>

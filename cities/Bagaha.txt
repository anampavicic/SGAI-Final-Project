'''Bagaha''' is a city and a [[municipality]] in the district of [[West Champaran district|Pashchim Champaran]]<ref>http://www.censusindia.gov.in/pca/SearchDetails.aspx?Id=245817 {{Dead link|date=May 2022}}</ref> in the state of [[Bihar]], India. It is a [[Administrative divisions of India|subdivision]] of [[West Champaran district|Pashchim Champaran]] district and is a Police District. It is located around {{convert|64|km}} northwest of its [[List of districts of Bihar|district headquarters]] [[Bettiah]]. It is {{cvt|294|km}} away from Bihar's state capital [[Patna]].

==Demographics==
{{As of|2011}} India [[census]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.census2011.co.in/census/city/158-bagaha.html | title=Bagaha City Population Census 2011}}</ref> Bagaha had a population of 112,634. Males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. Bagaha has an average literacy rate of 38%, lower than the national average of 59.5%; with 66% of the males and 34% of females literate. 19% of the population is under 6 years of age. Bagaha is located on the bank of [[Gandaki River|Gandak River]] and near the [[Valmiki National Park|Valmiki Tiger Reserve]].

== Transportation ==
[[Bagaha railway station]] is the main railway station of Bagaha and it is situated on [[Muzaffarpur–Gorakhpur main line]] under the [[Samastipur railway division]] of [[East Central Railway zone]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Bagaha Railway Station (BUG) : Station Code, Time Table, Map, Enquiry|url=https://www.ndtv.com/indian-railway/bagaha-bug-station|access-date=2021-08-30|website=www.ndtv.com|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Mehrotra|first=Yash|title=Bagaha Railway Station Map/Atlas ECR/East Central Zone - Railway Enquiry|url=https://indiarailinfo.com/station/map/bagaha-bug/566|access-date=2021-08-30|website=indiarailinfo.com}}</ref>
Otherwise, the main form of transportation is by road through the NH 727. 
The road extends towards other urban centers; from Bagaha to Patna,
Bagaha to Bettiah, and Bagaha to Gorakhpur (U.P).
Public transport is available from 4:00 Am to 9:00 PM.

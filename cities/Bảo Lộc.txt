'''Bảo Lộc''' (old name in [[Mạ people|Ma language]]: '''B’Lao''' is a [[provincial city (Vietnam)|city]] of [[Lâm Đồng Province]] in the [[Central Highlands (Vietnam)|Central Highlands]] region of [[Vietnam]]. Bảo Lộc is famous for its registered trademark: [[B'lao tea]]. As of 2018 the town district had a population of 170,920.<ref name="Statoids">{{cite web|url=http://www.statoids.com/yvn.html|title=Districts of Vietnam|publisher=Statoids|access-date=March 13, 2009}}</ref> The district covers an area of 229&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>. The district capital lies at Bảo Lộc.<ref name="Statoids"/>

==Name==
Its name is ''Bảo Lộc'' from ''B'lao'' in [[K'ho]] [[language]], what means [the land of] [[mu oil tree]]s.

==Subdivisions==
Bảo Lộc has six urban wards and five communes.

The wards are:

*Lộc Phát Ward- 25.7302&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>
*Lộc Tiến Ward - 13.0119&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>
*Lộc Sơn Ward - 12.3669&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>

The communes are:

*Lộc Thanh - 20.8098&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>
*Đam B’ri - 32.201&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>

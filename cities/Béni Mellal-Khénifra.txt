'''Béni Mellal-Khénifra''' ({{langx|ar|بني ملال - خنيفرة|banī mallāl - ḵunayfira}}) is one of the twelve [[regions of Morocco|regions of]] [[Morocco]]. It covers an area of 28,374&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup><ref name=area/> and recorded a population of 2,520,776 in the [[2014 Moroccan census]].<ref name=census2014/> The capital of the region is [[Beni Mellal]].<ref name=Bulletin>{{cite web |url=http://www.pncl.gov.ma/fr/EspaceJuridique/DocLib/d%C3%A9cret%20fixant%20le%20nombre%20des%20r%C3%A9gions.pdf |title=Décret fixant le nom des régions |date=20 February 2015 |access-date=11 December 2015 |format=pdf |work=Portail National des Collectivités Territoriales |language=fr |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150518083233/http://www.pncl.gov.ma/fr/EspaceJuridique/DocLib/d%C3%A9cret%20fixant%20le%20nombre%20des%20r%C3%A9gions.pdf |archive-date=18 May 2015 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

==Geography==
Béni Mellal-Khénifra is located in the interior of the country. It borders [[Rabat-Salé-Kénitra]] to the north, [[Fès-Meknès]] to the northeast, [[Drâa-Tafilalet]] to the southeast, [[Marrakesh-Safi]] to the southwest and [[Casablanca-Settat]] to the northwest.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.khouribgapress.com/region-beni-mellal-khenifra-pour-un-projet-societal-et-un-programme-daction-mobilisant-les-efforts-de-tous-les-acteurs-et-intervenants-locaux/ |title=Région Béni Mellal-Khénifra: Pour un projet sociétal et un programme d’action mobilisant les efforts de tous les acteurs et intervenants locaux |language=fr |access-date=13 December 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304070045/http://www.khouribgapress.com/region-beni-mellal-khenifra-pour-un-projet-societal-et-un-programme-daction-mobilisant-les-efforts-de-tous-les-acteurs-et-intervenants-locaux/ |archive-date=4 March 2016 |url-status=dead }}</ref> In the western and central part of the region is the productive [[Tadla]] plain irrigated by the [[Oum Er-Rbia River]].<ref>{{cite journal |last=Ouassou |first=A. |last2=Ameziane |first2=T. |last3=Ziyad |first3=A. |last4=Belghiti |first4=M. |date=2007 |title=Application of the Drought Management Guidelines in Morocco |url=http://om.ciheam.org/article.php?IDPDF=800547 |journal=Options Méditerranéennes, Series B |publisher=CIHEAM |issue=58 |pages=343–372 |access-date=13 December 2015}}</ref> This plain is bracketed by the [[High Atlas]] mountains which run through the southern and eastern parts of the region, and the foothills of the [[Middle Atlas]] to the north.

==History==
[[File:Béni Mellal's castle.jpg|thumb|Béni Mellal fortress]]
Béni Mellal-Khénifra was formed in September 2015 by adding [[Khouribga Province]] of [[Chaouia-Ouardigha]] region and [[Khénifra Province]] of [[Meknès-Tafilalet]] region to the three provinces previously making up the region of [[Tadla-Azilal]].<ref name=Bulletin />

==Government==
[[File:BeniMellal,AinAsserdoun.jpg|thumb|Ain Asserdoun spring, Béni Mellal]]
Ibrahim Moujahid, a member of the [[Authenticity and Modernity Party]], was elected as the regional council's first president on 14 September 2015.<ref>{{cite press release |date=14 September 2015 |title=Ministère de l'Intérieur : l'élection des présidents des Conseils des régions s'est déroulée dans de bonnes conditions et dans un climat de transparence |trans-title=Ministry of the Interior: the regional council presidential elections took place under good conditions and in an air of transparency |url=http://www.maroc.ma/fr/actualites/ministre-de-linterieur-lelection-des-presidents-des-conseils-des-regions-sest-deroulee |language=fr |publisher=[[Maghreb Arabe Press]] |access-date=11 December 2015}}</ref> Mohamed Derdouri was appointed governor (''wali'') of the region on 13 October 2015.<ref>{{cite news |date=14 October 2015 |title=SM le Roi a procédé à la nomination les Walis des régions |trans-title=His majesty the King appointed the Walis of the regions |url=http://lavieeco.com/news/actualite-maroc/sm-le-roi-a-procede-a-la-nomination-les-walis-des-regions-35593.html |language=fr |newspaper=[[La Vie Éco]] |access-date=13 December 2015 |archive-date=22 December 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151222092322/http://lavieeco.com/news/actualite-maroc/sm-le-roi-a-procede-a-la-nomination-les-walis-des-regions-35593.html |url-status=dead }}</ref>

==Economy==
[[Agriculture in Morocco|Agriculture]] is the backbone of the region's economy. Major crops include cereals, beetroot, olives, citrus fruits and [[Pomegranate|pomegranates]]; the production of milk and meat is also substantial.<ref>{{Cite report |title=Région de Tadla, Azilal |url=http://www.fao.org/fileadmin/templates/est/Climate_change/Morocco/Plan_maroc_vert_Tadla_Azilal.pdf |publisher=[[FAO]] |page=5 |access-date=13 December 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tadla-azilal.ma/en/decline.aspx?mod=8&rub=115&srub=122 |title=Agriculture and Agri-food industry: Sub sectors |publisher=Regional Investment Center of Tadla-Azilal |access-date=13 December 2015 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151222081104/http://www.tadla-azilal.ma/en/decline.aspx?mod=8&rub=115&srub=122 |archive-date=22 December 2015 }}</ref> The [[Ouled Abdoun Basin]] near [[Khouribga]] holds 44% of Morocco's [[phosphate]] reserves.<ref name=Zouhrietal2008>{{cite book|author1=S. ==Infrastructure==
The [[Berrechid–Beni Mellal expressway]] connects the region with the rest of the national expressway network, passing through the cities of Beni Mellal, [[Oued Zem]] and Khouribga as it heads northwest to [[Berrechid]] just south of [[Casablanca]]. [[National Route 8 (Morocco)|National Route 8]] is another important highway, connecting Beni Mellal and [[Khenifra]] with [[Marrakesh]] in the southeast and [[Fez, Morocco|Fez]] in the northwest. [[Beni Mellal Airport]] is served by [[Royal Air Maroc]], and there is a rail line with passenger service that runs between Oued Zem, Khouribga and Casablanca.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.oncf.ma/OffreDeTransport/Pages/TrainsGrandesLignes.aspx |title=Trains Grandes Lignes |language=fr |publisher=[[ONCF]] |access-date=13 December 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151127183541/http://www.oncf.ma/OffreDeTransport/Pages/TrainsGrandesLignes.aspx |archive-date=27 November 2015 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

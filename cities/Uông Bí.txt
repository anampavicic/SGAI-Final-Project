'''Uông Bí''' ({{audio|Uong Bi.ogg|listen|help=no}}) is a [[Provincial city (Vietnam)|city]] of [[Quảng Ninh Province]] in the [[northeast (Vietnam)|north-eastern]] region of [[Vietnam]]. As of 2019 the city had a population of 120,982.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Uông Bí (District-level City, Quảng Ninh, Vietnam) - Population Statistics, Charts, Map and Location |url=https://www.citypopulation.de/en/vietnam/admin/qu%E1%BA%A3ng_ninh/196__u%C3%B4ng_b%C3%AD/ |access-date=2024-02-07 |website=www.citypopulation.de}}</ref><ref name="Statoids">{{cite web|url=http://www.statoids.com/yvn.html|title=Districts of Vietnam|publisher=Statoids|access-date=March 23, 2009}}</ref> The City covers an area of 240&nbsp;km².<ref name="Statoids"/> Uong Bi was promoted from a town to a provincial city February 25, 2011.

==Administrative divisions==
The City contains 9 [[Ward (Vietnam)|ward]]s: 
* Phương Nam
* Phương Đông
* Yên Thanh
* Nam Khê
* Quang Trung 
* Trưng Vương
* Thanh Sơn
* Bắc Sơn
* Vàng Danh

and '''2''' [[Commune-level subdivisions (Vietnam)|communes]]:

* [[Thượng Yên Công]]
* [[Điền Công]]

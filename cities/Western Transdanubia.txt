'''Western Transdanubia''' ({{langx|hu|Nyugat-Dunántúl}} {{IPA-hu|ˈɲuɡɒt ˈdunaːntuːl|}}) is a statistical ([[Nomenclature of Territorial Units for Statistics|NUTS]] 2) region of [[Hungary]]. It is part of the [[Transdanubia]] (NUTS 1) region. Western Transdanubia includes the counties of [[Zala County|Zala]], [[Vas]], and [[Győr-Moson-Sopron]].

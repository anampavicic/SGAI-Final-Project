'''Oral''' ({{lang-kz|Орал}}, {{IPA-kk|woˈrɑɫ|pron|Kk-oral.oga}}), known in Russian as '''Uralsk''' ({{lang-rus|Уральск|p=ʊˈralʲsk}}), is a city in northwestern [[Kazakhstan]], at the confluence of the [[Ural (river)|Ural]] and [[Chagan (Ural)|Chagan]] rivers close to the [[Russia]]n [[Kazakhstan–Russia border|border]]. As it is located on the western bank of the Ural river, it is considered geographically in [[Europe]]. It is the [[capital city|capital]] of the [[West Kazakhstan Region]]. The ethnic composition is majority [[Kazakhs|Kazakh]] (71%), followed by the second-largest ethnic group [[Russians]] (25%). Population: {{Kz-census2009|271,900|punct=;}} {{Kz-census1999|194,905|punct=.}}

==Sport==
[[File:Стадион имени Петра Атояна.jpg|thumb|[[Petr Atoyan Stadium]]]]
[[FC Akzhayik]] are a Kazakhstani [[association football|football]] club based in [[Petr Atoyan Stadium]].

[[Bandy]] is the principal sport in the city. [[Akzhayik Sports Club]], based in the [[Yunost Stadium (Oral)|Yunost Stadium]], is the only professional team in the country and plays in the second highest division of Russia.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.livetv.ru/en/matchstory/06728rhjjfjyiijbkjj/11/ |title=Akzhayik / LiveTV |website=www.livetv.ru |access-date=16 April 2018}}</ref> In September&nbsp;2017, the [[Kazakhstan Bandy Federation]] bought the club.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://translate.google.co.uk/translate?hl=en&sl=ru&u=http://www.rusbandy.ru/news/11104 |title=Google Translate |website=translate.google.co.uk |access-date=16 April 2018}}</ref>

The city sent a team to the [[Spartakiade]] 2009 and the finished second.<ref>[http://akghaik.ucoz.ru/photo/9-1-0-0-2]{{dead link|date=September 2018}}</ref> All the players of the Under-23 national team in the 2013–14 season came from Oral.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://translate.google.com/translate?hl=en&sl=ru&u=http://www.rusbandy.ru/nationalteam/388/roster/1056/&prev=/search?q=http://www.rusbandy.ru/nationalteam/388/roster/1056/&biw=1093&bih=552 |title=Google Translate |website=translate.google.com |access-date=16 April 2018}}</ref> After the [[Kazakhstan national bandy team]] took the gold medal at the [[Bandy at the 2011 Asian Winter Games|2011 Asian Winter Games]], the team came back to Oral in triumph.<ref>[http://kaztrk.kz/kaz/news/sport/Dopti_hokkej_sheberleri_Oralga_oraldi.html Video from the triumphant homecoming] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131202223201/http://kaztrk.kz/kaz/news/sport/Dopti_hokkej_sheberleri_Oralga_oraldi.html |date=2013-12-02 }}</ref>

==Geography==
===Climate===

The climate of Oral is [[continental climate|continental]] with long cold winters and warm, often hot summers. Under the [[Köppen climate classification]], Oral has a cold [[semiarid climate]] (Köppen ''Bsk''). Summers are extremely hot considering its position north of the [[51st parallel north|51st parallel]], but winters are more reminiscent of continental climates further east than Europe.

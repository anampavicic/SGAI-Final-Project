'''Dongying''' ({{zh|s=东营 |t=東營 |p=Dōngyíng}}), a [[prefecture-level city]] on the northern ([[Bohai Sea]]) coast of [[Shandong]] province, People's Republic of China. As of the 2020 census, 2,193,518 people resided within its administrative area of {{convert|7923.26|km2|abbr=on}} and 1,188,656 in the built-up area made up of Dongying and Kenli districts. Dongying is home to the [[Shengli Oilfield]], which after the [[Daqing Field|Daqing oilfield]] is the second largest oilfield in China.<ref>{{Cite web |title=鉴往知来丨跟着总书记学历史：走过一甲子，一个油田的“胜利”之路--时政--人民网 |url=http://politics.people.com.cn/n1/2021/1022/c1001-32261683.html |access-date=2024-10-19 |website=politics.people.com.cn}}</ref>

==History==

The city was established in 1983, as a base for developing the [[Yellow River]] Delta and China's second largest [[oilfield]], [[Shengli Field]]. It was named after the establishment of Dongying and Xiying camps during the [[Eastern Expedition]] by [[Emperor Taizong of Tang|Emperor Taizong]] of [[Tang dynasty|Tang Dynasty]]. Later, Dongying Village was formed, and in 1964, the headquarters of the [[North China]] Petroleum Exploration Battle was established in this village.The following year, the Dongying Office in the Huimin area was established here. In 1982, [[State Council of the People's Republic of China|the State Council]] approved the establishment of a prefecture level city, named "Dongying".<ref>{{Cite web |last=Dili |first=Dala |date=2021-10-22 |title=从黄河入海口盐碱滩上的东营村到黄河三角洲的石油名城东营市 |trans-title=From Dongying village on the saline beach at the mouth of the Yellow River to Dongying City, a famous oil city in the Yellow River Delta |url=https://www.zhihu.com/pin/1434909767001534464 |access-date=2024-06-11 |website=知乎 |language=zh}}</ref>

===Climate===
Dongying City is located at mid latitudes, facing away from the land and sea, and is influenced by both the Eurasian continent and the Pacific Ocean.<ref>{{Cite web |title=东营市气候特点 - 山东气候 |url=https://sd.weather.com.cn/sdqh/gsqhtd/11/86236.shtml |access-date=2024-10-19 |website=sd.weather.com.cn}}</ref>Dongying has a [[monsoon]]-influenced, four-season [[humid continental climate]] [[Köppen climate classification|Köppen]] ''Dwa''), with hot, humid summers, and cold but dry winters. The city is dry and nearly rainless in spring, hot and rainy in summer, crisp in autumn and dry and cold (with little snow) in winter. The average annual temperature is {{convert|13.5|°C|1|}}, and the annual precipitation is {{convert|527.7|mm|1}}, with a strong summer maximum, and high variability from year to year. January is the coldest and driest month, with a mean temperature of {{convert|-2.2|°C|1}} and {{convert|5.1|mm|2|abbr=on}} of equivalent rainfall. July is the hottest and wettest month; the corresponding numbers are {{convert|27.0|°C|1}}, and {{convert|136.7|mm|2|abbr=on}}.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2024-06-11 |title=自然环境 |trans-title=environment |url=http://www.dongying.gov.cn/col/col40583/index.html |website=Dongying Municipal People's Government |language=zh}}</ref>

==Economy==

A large part of the city's economy revolves around petroleum and the nearby Shengli Oil Field.

Industries include petroleum, petrochemistry, saline chemistry, papermaking, machinery, electronics, construction, building materials, foodstuff processing, pneumatic tires and rubber, textiles, and light industries.

Dongying is one of the world's leading producers of rubber tires. <ref>{{Cite web |title=中国轮胎之路 |url=https://m.yicai.com/news/101950245.html |access-date=2024-10-19 |website=m.yicai.com}}</ref>With the release of the 2023 ERJ Global Tire Ranking, a total of 36 tire companies from China appear on the list, accounting for nearly 50%. Among them, the number of Shandong tire companies on the list has reached 21, accounting for nearly 60% of China's listed tire companies; Sales account for 51% of domestic tire companies.As one of the main concentration areas of Shandong's tire industry, Dongying has a total of 7 tire companies listed on the latest Top 75 list, with 6 of them showing a significant leap in ranking; Two tire companies also achieved high sales growth in the industry headwinds of 2022.<ref>{{Cite web |title=东营轮胎75强，谁是王中王？ - 综合新闻 - 轮胎商业网 |url=http://www.chinatiredealer.com/news/show-309106.html |access-date=2024-10-19 |website=www.chinatiredealer.com}}</ref>

The city's growing manufacturing sector and its proximity to oil reserves have led to increased company investments. An example is [[DuPont]], which invested five billion yuan in 2005 to build a [[titanium dioxide]] factory in the area. After this project's completion, it became the largest investment outside of the US for DuPont.<ref>{{Cite web |title=杜邦海外最大项目落户中国引发环保质疑_新闻中心_新浪网 |url=https://news.sina.com.cn/c/2007-12-20/102914564675.shtml |access-date=2024-10-19 |website=news.sina.com.cn}}</ref>

== Culture ==
Dongying History Museum (Guangrao County History Museum) is a comprehensive museum of geography established in May 1993. It is a national second level museum and AAA level tourist attraction, including a comprehensive building and Sun Wu Temple. It covers a total area of 42763 square meters and currently has more than 12000 collections, including 417 precious cultural relics above the national third level (pieces/sets).<ref>{{Cite web |title=东营市历史博物馆 - 场馆介绍 - 山东省爱国主义教育基地 |url=http://www.sd-aiguo.com/hdql/index/venuedetail?id=96 |access-date=2024-10-19 |website=www.sd-aiguo.com}}</ref>

The Sun Tzu Cultural Tourism Area is located in the Chengdong New District of Guangrao County, Dongying City. Based on the standards of national level ecological tourism demonstration areas and tourist resorts, it is a cultural experiential tourism and vacation project with Sun Tzu culture as its theme, created according to the functional positioning of ecology, tourism, culture, and city. In August 2012, it was rated as a national 4A level tourist attraction.<ref>{{Cite web |title=欢迎访问中国孙子文化园 |url=http://www.tianxiaqiguan.com/detail/intro/sid/14718310186234540 |access-date=2024-10-19 |website=www.tianxiaqiguan.com}}</ref>

==Education==

Dongying is home to one major university, the [[China University of Petroleum (Huadong)|China University of Petroleum]], as well as several colleges and technical schools.
There are also many top high schools located in Dongying, one of those is Shengli Oilfield No.1 Middle School. Founded in 1965, rated as the most successful middle and high schools in China in a research hold by Beijing University in 2014, it is the first provincial standardized school in Shandong Province. There are 114 classes in the school with more than 5,300 students.<ref>http://www.sldyzx.cn/Item/4786.aspx</ref>

==Explosion==
{{Main|2015 Dongying explosion}}
There was an explosion that occurred at a [[chemical]] factory starting on Monday, 31 August 2015. Thirteen people died and several were detained by authorities.<ref>[https://www.cnbc.com/2015/08/31/major-explosion-reported-in-dongying-china.html Dongying factory blast kills one, six execs arrested, Asia Pacific News, 01-09-2014]</ref>According to Dongying municipal party media reports, the accident occurred at Binyuan Chemical Company in Diaokou Township, Lijin County. An explosion occurred late at night on Monday (August 31), and the flames were extinguished in the early morning of Tuesday (September 1).

According to the report, the accident resulted in one death, and the relevant company leaders have been brought under control.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2015-09-01 |title=山东利津化工厂爆炸“一人死亡” |url=https://www.bbc.com/zhongwen/simp/china/2015/09/150901_china_shandong_chemical_blast |access-date=2024-10-19 |website=BBC News 中文 |language=zh-Hans}}</ref>

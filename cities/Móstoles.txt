'''Móstoles''' ({{IPA|es|ˈmostoles|lang|Pronunciation of Móstoles in Spanish.ogg}}) is a [[municipalities of Spain|municipality]] of [[Spain]] located in the [[Community of Madrid]]. With over 200,000 inhabitants, it is the region's second most populated municipality after [[Madrid]]. Móstoles was a small town for a long time, but expanded rapidly in the second half of the 20th century.

The city also hosts the main campus of the [[Rey Juan Carlos University]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Campus - Universidad Rey Juan Carlos|url=https://www.urjc.es/universidad/campus|access-date=2020-08-26|language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=7. Recursos materiales y servicios|url=https://sede.educacion.gob.es/cid/152353585126867139776897.pdf|access-date=2020-08-26|website=Sede electrónica - sede electrónica I Ministerio de Educación y Formación Profesional|page=4|language=es}}</ref>

==Geography==
[[File:(Móstoles) Madrid ESA354454 (cropped).jpg|thumb|left|Móstoles as seen by the [[European Space Agency]]'s [[Sentinel-2]].]]
The municipality presents a largely flat relief.{{Sfn|Martín-Serrano García|2004|p=177}} The main hydrographic features are the [[Guadarrama (river)|Guadarrama river]] at the western end of the municipality and several of the latter's left bank tributary creeks, including the Arroyo del Soto and the Arroyo de los Combos.{{Sfn|Martín-Serrano García|2004|p=177}}

It lies at 660 metres above sea level.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.aemet.es/es/eltiempo/prediccion/municipios/mostoles-id28092|publisher=[[AEMET]]|title=Móstoles}}</ref>

==History==
Human beings have been present since the [[Paleolithic]]. Evidence of that fact are sites in which there are flint gravel and arrow tips.<ref>{{Cite web|title=PPATRIMONIO_HISTORICO_001-2.pdf|url=http://www.madrid.org/cartografia/planea/planeamiento/planeamiento/Mostoles/Vigente/PPATRIMONIO_HISTORICO_001-2.pdf|access-date=2020-08-26|website=madrid.org|pages=49–52|language=es}}</ref>

Some traces of presence of Roman civilisation occur in the current municipality, specifically of the late period. They mainly consist of ceramic remains.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Rodríguez Morales|first=Jesús|title=Excavaciones arqueológicas en Móstoles|url=https://www.academia.edu/922885/Excavaciones_arqueol%C3%B3gicas_en_M%C3%B3stoles|access-date=2020-08-26|website=academia.edu|language=es}}</ref>

There is no evidence of a town in the current Móstoles during the Islamic rule era in Iberian Peninsula. However, there are some remains of this era in the territory.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Recorrido por la Historia de Móstoles I Museo de la Ciudad de Móstoles|url=https://www.mostoles.es/MuseoCiudad/es/recorrido-por-la-historia-de-mostoles|access-date=2020-08-27|website=mostoles.es|language=es}}</ref><ref name=":0" />

It was founded most probably circa 1085–1137, after the Christian conquest of the [[Taifa of Toledo|Kingdom of Toledo]] (1085) as it was not mentioned in the chronicles compiling the villages seized in the conquest.{{Sfn|Martín-Serrano García|2004|p=179}} Written mentions to the village in the Middle Ages, since its foundation up to the late-15th century are limited.{{Sfn|Martín-Serrano García|2004|p=179}} By the beginning of the 16th century, it was part of the Land of [[Toledo, Spain|Toledo]].{{Sfn|Martín-Serrano García|2004|p=180}}

On 6 December 1565, [[Philip II of Spain|Philip II]] issued a royal ''cédula'' granting Móstoles the independence from Toledo, becoming a town (''villa'') under {{ill|Realengo (jurisdiction)|es|Realengo|lt=''realengo''}} (directly owned by the monarch).{{Sfn|Martín del Hoyo|2016|pp=319–320}}

Móstoles became famous as, although it was only a small town, its municipal authorities called for a general rebellion against the French forces on 2 May 1808 immediately after the [[Dos de Mayo Uprising]] in the Spanish capital, reportedly issuing the following public announcement (''bando'') signed by the Mayor: "The homeland is in danger. Madrid is perishing, victim of the French perfidiousness. Spaniards, turn to save her".{{Sfn|Martín-Serrano García|2004|p=185|ps=&nbsp;"''La patria está en peligro. Madrid perece víctima de la perfidia francesa. Españoles, acudid a salvarla.''"}}

The municipality experienced a slow and steady population growth during the first half of the 20th century, followed by a huge demographic expansion from the 1960s onwards.{{Sfn|Martín-Serrano García|2004|p=192}}

== Human geography and administration ==
There are two main localities: Móstoles, which is placed in the east of the municipality, and Parque-Coimbra, which is located in the southwest. The city had a population of 209,184 in 2019 and Parque-Coimbra is home to 10,747 people.<ref>{{Cite web|title=IDEM Infraestructura de Datos Espaciales de la Comunidad de Madrid|url=https://idem.madrid.org/visor/?v=suelo&ZONE=425311.7085417244,4463969.591182804,13|access-date=2020-08-28|website=idem.madrid.org}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=IDEM Infraestructura de Datos Espaciales de la Comunidad de Madrid|url=https://idem.madrid.org/visor/?v=planea&ZONE=424666.8913797098,4463407.148546946,13|access-date=2020-08-28|website=idem.madrid.org}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=INEbase / Demography and population /Municipal Register. Only during 2003-2015 and again since 2023 the city has been ruled by the [[People's Party of the Community of Madrid]].

== Economy ==
Agriculture and animal husbandry are hardly performed in the municipality. Only a 0.2% of the [[Gross domestic product|GDP]] was collected from these activities in 2019. Mining, and industry activities equal 9.14% of that economic phenomenon in the same year. Building activities made the 8.63% of the money related to that economic measure. 11.9% of the registers of working people to an organism of the Welfare System in Spain named ''Tesorería General de la Seguridad Social'', were due to posts in this sector.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Datos ficha municipal 0920 2|url=http://www.madrid.org/desvan/almudena/FichaMunicipal.icm?codFicha=1&codMunZona=0920&tipoFicha=M&codSolapaActiva=2|access-date=2020-08-28|language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Datos ficha municipal 0920 5|url=http://www.madrid.org/desvan/almudena/FichaMunicipal.icm?generarIndice=T&codFicha=1&codMunZona=0920&tipoFicha=M&codSolapaActiva=5&codTablaActiva=15|access-date=2020-08-28|language=es}}</ref>

The industrial estates in the municipality are Polígono Industrial El Lucero, in the northeast end; Polígono Industrial Los Rosales, in the northeast end; Prado Regordoño and Polígono Industrial la, in the east end; and Arroyomolinos, in the southern half.<ref name=":2" />

== Healthcare ==
The municipality is home to a ''centro de salud'' in Parque Coimbra (primary care centre), 8 ''centros de salud'' in the main city, and two hospitals in the main locality.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Buscador de centros sanitarios │Comunidad de Madrid|url=https://www.comunidad.madrid/servicios/salud/buscador-centros-sanitarios|access-date=2020-09-19|language=es}}</ref>

== Education ==
Besides the university campus there are 34 public early childhood and primary education centres (CEIP) and 17 public secondary education centres (IES). 4 ''concertados'' (semiprivate or quasiprivate) centres are also located in the municipality. A centre for vocational education (CIFP) can also be found in Móstoles.<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|title=Servicios Web a Padres de Alumnos de la Comunidad de Madrid|url=https://www.madrid.org/wpad_pub/run/j/BusquedaSencilla.icm?accion_paginacion=1&prox_cdmuni=092&tipoBusqueda=PROX&codCentrosNav=&siPrivadoComp=&codCentrosComp=&prox_cddis=0&prox_cdvial=&prox_nmpoliciapk=&dscCentrosComp=&cdCentro=&esPaginacion=S&prox_txtvial=&formularioConsulta=&prox_distancia=500&prox_cdTpvial=&basica.strCodNomMuni=Nombre+del+centro+o+c%F3digo+del+centro&navegador=Netscape&dsCentro=&esPaginacion=S#|access-date=2020-09-19|website=Comunidad de Madrid|language=es}}</ref>

The campus includes the Higher School of Experimental Sciences and Technology, where people can choose among 13 degrees and 8 double degrees; the Higher Technical school of Computer Engineering, which includes 6 degrees and 6 double degrees; and the Faculty of social and legal sciences, where there are 9 degrees and 7 double degrees.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Estudios en el campus - Universidad Rey Juan Carlos|url=https://www.urjc.es/universidad/campus/campus-de-mostoles/338-estudios-campus-mostoles|access-date=2020-09-20|website=www.urjc.es|language=es-es}}</ref>

An adult education centre and a centre of the national language teaching institution are also located in the municipality.<ref name=":3" />

==Main sights==
===Historical landmarks===
[[File:Mostoles Ermita.JPG|thumb|left|Hermitage]]
Two of the most architectural landmarks in Móstoles are churches; the church of ''La Asunción de Nuestra Señora'' (whose construction dates back to the 13th century) features a [[Mudéjar art|Mudéjar]] [[apse]].{{Sfn|Martín-Serrano García|2004|pp=254–255}}
The [[Baroque]] [[Hermitage (religious retreat)|hermitage]] of ''La Virgen de los Santos'' dates from the 17th century.{{Sfn|Martín-Serrano García|2004|p=261}}

There is a sculpture by [[Aurelio Rodríguez-Vicente Carretero|Aurelio Carretero]] paying homage to Andrés Torrejón inaugurated in 1908 to mark the one hundredth anniversary of the Dos de Mayo uprising.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.abc.es/espana/madrid/abci-monumento-alcalde-mostoles-cumple-cien-anos-200805050300-1641844628416_noticia.html|website=[[ABC (Spain)|ABC]]|title=El monumento al alcalde de Móstoles cumple cien años|date=5 May 2008|first=Sara|last=Medialdea}}</ref>

===Museums and galleries===
[[File:Móstoles - Centro de Arte Dos de Mayo (CA2M) 5.jpg|thumb|right|Façade of the CA2M]]
Móstoles is the home of the Community of Madrid's modern art gallery, the Centro de Arte Dos de Mayo (CA2M). It was opened in 2008 to mark the two hundredth anniversary of the Dos de Mayo uprising.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://elpais.com/ccaa/2018/02/22/madrid/1519330304_207717.html|website=[[El País]]|title=Arte contemporáneo con memoria|date=23 February 2018|first=Sergio C.|last=Fanjul}}</ref>

The residence of Andrés Torrejón was rehabilitated by the city council and turned into a museum.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.teleprensa.com/es/madrid/mostoles-recuerda-a-su-heroico-alcalde-andres-torrejon-con-motivo-del-bicentenario-de-su-muerte-el-17-de-agosto.html|website=Teleprensa|title=Móstoles recuerda a su heroico alcalde, Andrés Torrejón, con motivo del bicentenario de su muerte el 17 de agosto|date=13 August 2012}}</ref>

===Walking and cycling===
There is [[cycling infrastructure]] (Spanish: ''aceras bici'', "bike-sidewalks") within central Móstoles. Funds were made available in 2017 to upgrade these cycleways.<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.lavanguardia.com/local/madrid/20170926/431576486585/mostoles-pone-en-marcha-proyecto-de-rehabilitacion-de-todas-las-aceras-bici.html |title=Móstoles pone en marcha proyecto de Rehabilitación de todas las Aceras Bici |newspaper=[[La Vanguardia]] |language=es |date=26 September 2017 |access-date=6 May 2018 |location=Móstoles |author=EFE |author-link=EFE}}</ref>

It is also possible to cycle into the nearby countryside. The railway line originally continued to [[Almorox]], but the section beyond Móstoles was closed in the 1960s. Between [[Móstoles-El Soto railway station]] and [[Navalcarnero]] the track has been converted into a greenway (''via verde'') for cyclists and walkers. The route crosses the [[Guadarrama (river)|river Guadarrama]] on an iron bridge.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.madrid.org/cs/Satellite?blobcol=urldata&blobheader=application%2Fpdf&blobheadername1=Content-Disposition&blobheadervalue1=filename%3D11.V%C3%ADa+Verde+del+Guadarrama.pdf&blobkey=id&blobtable=MungoBlobs&blobwhere=1352854988979&ssbinary=true |title=Vía Verde del Guadarrama |format=PDF |work=Comunidad de Madrid |language=es |access-date=6 May 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.viasverdes.com/en/itineraries/itinerario.asp?id=81 |title=Guadarrama River Greenway |work=Vías Verdes |publisher=Fundación de los Ferrocarriles Españoles |access-date=6 May 2018}}</ref>
The ''via verde'' connects to a waymarked long-distance walk to [[Guadalupe, Cáceres|Guadalupe]] in Extremadura. The waymarking is recent, but it marks a traditional route from Madrid to the [[Our Lady of Guadalupe in Extremadura|Our Lady of Guadalupe]] pilgrimage site.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://es.wikiloc.com/wikiloc/view.do?id=3094140 |title=Camino Real a Guadalupe. Etapa 2. Móstoles – Casarrubios del Monte. |work=[[Wikiloc]] |language=es |date=19 July 2012 |access-date=6 May 2018}}</ref>

== Festivities ==

* '''2nd of May''': The origin of this celebration is in a [[Dos de Mayo Uprising|historic event]] in which the locals rebelled against the French invaders.<ref>{{Cite web|title=2 de Mayo I Festejos|url=https://www.mostoles.es/Festejos/es/festejos/2-mayo|access-date=2020-08-26|website=Ayuntamiento de Mostoles|language=es}}</ref> Some activities that are performed during the festive period are a parade with ''[[Gigantes y cabezudos|Gigantes]]'' [[Gigantes y cabezudos|and ''Cabezudos'']], a mass dedicated to the majors which ruled during the uprising period, a performance about the historic event, violin concerts, piano concerts, a rhythmic gymnastics exhibition, pétanque tournaments, an artistic roller skating tournament, mus tournaments and ''cuatrola'' (card game) tournaments.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Fiestas 2 Mayo - Declaradas de interés turístico regional. Móstoles - Del 30 de abril al 4 de mayo de 2019|url=https://www.mostoles.es/es/noticias/mostoles-presenta-programa-fiestas-grandes-2-mayo.ficheros/887755-PROGRAMA%202%20DE%20MAYO%202019%20-%20Ultima%20versi%C3%B3n.pdf?usePlugin=true|access-date=2020-08-27|website=mostoles.es|language=es}}</ref>
* '''Patron saint festivities''': They are held in the second third of September. The origin consists in a consecration to the Virgin de los Santos (in a facet in which she's related to the saints).<ref>{{Cite web|title=Patronales I Festejos|url=https://www.mostoles.es/Festejos/es/festejos/patronales|access-date=2020-08-26|website=Ayuntamiento de Mostoles|language=es}}</ref> Locals perform activities such as flowers places to Virgin statues as an offering symbol, a ''Gigantes'' and ''Cabezudos'' parade, an opening address, cows encloses and let loose actions and concerts.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Móstoles - Fiestas Patronales - 11 al 15 de septiembre 2019 - En honor a Ntra. Sra. de los Santos|url=https://www.mostoles.es/es/noticias/mostoles-presenta-programa-fiestas-septiembre-inaugura-preg.ficheros/898449-Programafiestaspatronalesv15PDF.pdf?usePlugin=true|access-date=2020-08-27|website=mostoles.es|language=es}}</ref> 
The following festivities are also held in Spain generally:

* '''Carnival''': This festivity usually takes place during the second quarter of February. As typically in this festive event, parades and dances are perform in Móstoles during this period. There are also an opening address; some ''[https://dle.rae.es/pasacalles pasacalles]'', which are similar to standard parades; contests about carnival troupes, and ''[[chirigota]]s'' performances.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Carnaval │Festejos|url=https://www.mostoles.es/Festejos/es/festejos/carnaval|access-date=2020-08-29|website=mostoles.es|language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Carnaval — Móstoles — del 15 al 22 de febrero 2020|url=https://www.mostoles.es/Festejos/es/festejos/carnaval.ficheros/930792-Programa%20Carnaval%20M%C3%B3stoles%202020.pdf?usePlugin=true|access-date=2020-08-29|website=mostoles.es|language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Móstoles - Carnval del 3 al 10 de febrero|url=https://www.mostoles.es/es/noticias/preparado-mostoles-carnavales-2018.ficheros/750491-Programaci%C3%B3n%20Carnaval%20Mostoles%202018.pdf?usePlugin=true|website=mostoles.es|language=es}}</ref>
* '''Christmas:''' From early December to [[Epiphany (holiday)|Epiphany Day]], several activities are performed such as an event with illuminated objects, an illuminated ''pasacalles'', a nativity scene settlement, a Three Kings’ parade, and sports activities such as a basketball tournament, a rhythmic gymnastics exhibition, a volleyball tournament, a golf tournament, swimming competitions, artistic roller skate competitions and futsal tournaments.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Navidad │ Festejos|url=https://www.mostoles.es/Festejos/es/festejos/navidad|access-date=2020-08-29|website=mostoles.es|language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Folleto Navidad v10 Final sin marcas|url=https://www.mostoles.es/es/noticias/mostoles-presenta-programa-fiestas-navidenas.ficheros/914114-Folleto%20Navidad%20v8.pdf?usePlugin=true|access-date=2020-08-29|website=mostoles.es|language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=651306-Programación Navidad Motoles 2017.pdf|url=https://www.mostoles.es/es/noticias/viernes-arranca-navidad-mostoles.ficheros/651306-Programaci%C3%B3n%20Navidad%20Mostoles%202017.pdf?usePlugin=true|access-date=2020-08-29|website=mostoles.es|language=es}}</ref>

'''Port Louis''' ({{langx|fr|Port-Louis}}) is a [[districts of Mauritius|district]] of [[Mauritius]], located in the northwest of the island, it is the island nation's smallest district and has the highest population density. The district wholly encompasses [[Port Louis]], the capital of the country. Port Louis district has an area of 42.7&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup> and the estimated population was 118,123 at the end of 2019.<ref name="AnnDigestStats2019"/>

==Places of interest==
{{Expand section|date=July 2012}}
The district hosts the [[Aapravasi Ghat]], a [[UNESCO]] [[World Heritage Site]] since 2006.

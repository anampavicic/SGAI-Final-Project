'''Bà Rịa''' ({{IPA|vi|ɓaː˧˩ riə˧˨˧ˀ|lang|Ba Ria.ogg}}) is a city in [[Bà Rịa–Vũng Tàu province]] in the [[Southeast (Vietnam)|Southeast]] region of [[Vietnam]]. Bà Rịa is split from [[Vũng Tàu]] by a river crossed by Cỏ May Bridge.
This is the ''[[de jure]]'' seat of the province since most of the provincial administration agencies are located here. Bà Rịa officially became the provincial capital of Bà Rịa–Vũng Tàu from 2 May 2012, replacing Vũng Tàu in that role.

==Location==
Bà Rịa is located 90&nbsp;km east southeast of [[Ho Chi Minh City]] and 20&nbsp;km northwest of the petroleum city of Vũng Tàu. Bà Rịa borders [[Châu Đức District]] and part of [[Tân Thành District]] to the north, Vũng Tàu to the south, [[Long Điền District]] to the east, and Tân Thành District to the west. Bà Rịa has an area of 197.5&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>, including seven urban wards (''phường'') (Long Toàn, Phước Hiệp, Phước Hưng, Phước Nguyên, Phước Trung, Long Hương, and Kim Dinh), and 3 rural [[Commune-level subdivisions (Vietnam)|communes]] (''xã'') (Hòa Long, Tân Hưng, and Long Phước).
As of 2011, the town has 122,424 inhabitants; the density is 3,500 inhabitants/km<sup>2</sup>.

==Administration==
The city was promoted from the town of Bà Rịa to the capital city on 22 August 2012.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://news.gov.vn/Home/Ba-Ria-city-established/20128/15440.vgp|title=VGP News &#124; Ba Ria city established - Ba Ria city established|date=22 August 2012 }}</ref> The town was founded on 2 June 1994 from the split of the district of Châu Thành into the districts of Châu Đức, Tân Thành, and Bà Rịa. 
Many provincial agencies have been relocated from Vũng Tàu to Bà Rịa. By 2007, this city was a 3rd-class municipality in Vietnam<ref>{{cite web | url=http://vneconomy.vn/20120716062538557P0C9920/thi-xa-ba-ria-se-duoc-nang-cap-len-thanh-pho.htm | title=Bà Rịa town will be upgraded to city | publisher=VnEconomy | date=July 16, 2012 | access-date=July 20, 2012 | language=vi | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140701231158/http://vneconomy.vn/20120716062538557P0C9920/thi-xa-ba-ria-se-duoc-nang-cap-len-thanh-pho.htm# | archive-date=2014-07-01 |url-status=dead}}</ref> and was upgraded to 2nd-class municipality on 27 November 2014.

==Transportation==
Bà Rịa is an important transport hub of the province, on [[National Route 51 (Vietnam)|Route 51]], [[Road 52, Vietnam|Road 52]] and [[Road 56, Vietnam|Road 56]]. Bà Rịa is situated about 48&nbsp;km southeast of the planned [[Long Thành International Airport]] on National Route 51 (Vietnam). Bà Rịa has Bà Rịa Secres Port. It is connected to [[Xuân Lộc District|Xuân Lộc]] by road.

==Tourism==
Bà Rịa is on the way from Ho Chi Minh City to the seaside resorts at the [[Long Hải (township)|Long Hải]] and [[Bình Châu, Bà Rịa–Vũng Tàu|Bình Châu]] hot springs.

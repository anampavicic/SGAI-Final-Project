'''Dairut''' ({{Langx|cop|ⲧⲉⲣⲱⲧ}} {{transl|cop|terōt}} {{IPA-cop|dæˈroːt|Late Coptic}}) is a [[List of cities and towns in Egypt|city]] in [[Egypt]]. It is located on the west bank of the [[Nile]], in the [[Asyut Governorate]]. This is the point where the Nile divides, and a branch wanders off and eventually ends up in the [[Faiyum]].

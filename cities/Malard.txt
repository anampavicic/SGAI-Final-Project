'''Malard''' ({{langx|fa|ملارد}}){{efn|Also [[romanize]]d as '''Malārd''' and '''Melārd'''; also known as '''Malār'''<ref>{{GEOnet3|-3073679}}</ref>}} is a city in the [[Central District (Malard County)|Central District]] of [[Malard County]], [[Tehran province|Tehran]] province, [[Iran]], serving as capital of both the county and the district.<ref name="Malard, Qods, and Gomishan Counties Establishment">{{cite news|title=The government's agreement with 18 changes in national divisions: Three counties were added to the geographical map of the country|language=fa|website=dolat.ir|via=Secretariat of the Government Information Council|url=https://dolat.ir/detail/176393|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180205130020/https://dolat.ir/detail/176393|archive-date=5 February 2018|publisher=Ministry of the Interior, Board of Ministers|last=Davodi|first=Parviz|date=22 April 2009|access-date=22 November 2023}}</ref> It is also the administrative center for [[Malard-e Shomali Rural District]].<ref name="Karaj County Rural Districts">{{cite report|title=Creation and formation of 29 rural districts including villages, farms and places in Karaj County under Tehran province|id=Notification 118172T943|language=fa|website=rc.majlis.ir|via=The Islamic Council Research Center|url=https://rc.majlis.ir/fa/law/show/110256|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121220045253/https://rc.majlis.ir/fa/law/show/110256|last=Mousavi|first=Mirhossein|archive-date=20 December 2012|orig-date=Approved 10 April 1366|date=30 September 1391|publisher=Ministry of the Interior, Council of Ministers|access-date=15 December 2023}}</ref>{{efn|Formerly [[Malard-e Shomali Rural District|Malard Rural District]]<ref name="Malard, Qods, and Gomishan Counties Establishment"/>}}

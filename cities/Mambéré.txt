'''Mambéré''' {{IPA|fr|mɑ̃.be.ʁe|}} is a prefecture in the [[Central African Republic]]. In 2022, the prefecture had a population of around 265,479.<ref name="MA">{{cite web |last1=Oual |first1=Appolinaire Godom |title=Mambéré : 265 479 hbts|url=https://icasees.org/index.php/prefectures/mambere-307-636-hbts|publisher=Institut centrafricain de la Statistique et des Etudes Economiques et Sociales |access-date=10 March 2023}}</ref> Mambéré has a size of 15,740&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>.<ref>{{cite web |title=MAMBÉRÉ:Prefecture in Central African Republic |url=https://www.citypopulation.de/en/centralafrica/admin/CF24__mamb%C3%A9r%C3%A9/|website=citypopulation.de |publisher=City Population |access-date=10 March 2023}}</ref> [[Carnot, Central African Republic|Carnot]] is the capital of the prefecture.<ref name="OU">{{cite web |last1=Oubangui Médias |first1=Oubangui Médias |title=La Centrafrique dispose désormais de 20 préfectures et de 84 sous-préfectures |url=https://oubanguimedias.com/2020/12/11/la-centrafrique-dispose-desormais-de-20-prefectures-et-de-84-sous-prefectures/ |website=oubanguimedias.com |publisher=Oubangi Medias |access-date=10 March 2023}}</ref> It is named for the [[Mambéré River]].

== History ==
Together with Lim-Pendé and Ouham Fafa, Mambéré Prefecture was established on 10 December 2020.<ref name="OU" /> Previously all sub-prefectures and communes of Mambéré were part of [[Mambéré-Kadéï]].

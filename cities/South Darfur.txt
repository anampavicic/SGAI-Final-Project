'''South Darfur State''' ({{langx|ar|ولاية جنوب دارفور}} Wilāyat Ǧanūb Dārfūr; Janob Darfor) is one of the [[States of Sudan|wilayat]] or states of [[Sudan]]. It is one of the five states that compose the [[Darfur|region of Darfur]] in western Sudan.

==Overview==
[[File:DSC 0597رمل.jpg|thumb|left|Roadworks near Nyala]]
Prior to the creation of two new states in the Darfur region in January 2012, South Darfur had an area of {{convert|127300|km2}} and an estimated population of approximately 2,890,000 (2006). [[Nyala, Sudan|Nyala]] is the capital of the state. The State was affected by the [[2010 Sahel famine]].

'''Honam''' ({{IPA|ko|ho.nam}}; literally "south of the lake") is a region coinciding with the former [[Jeolla]] Province in what is now [[South Korea]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.yonhapnews.co.kr/bulletin/2015/02/27/0200000000AKR20150227162400064.HTML|title=Where is Ho (Lake) in Honam / Hoseo? Byeolgogje? Seungrimji?|date=2015-03-01|work=yonhapnews|access-date=2018-04-28|language=ko-KR}}</ref> Today, the term refers to [[Gwangju]], [[South Jeolla]] Province and [[Jeonbuk State]].<ref name=":0" /> The name "Jeonla-do" is used in the names of the [[Honam Line|Honam railway line]] and [[Honam Expressway]], which are major transportation corridors connecting [[Seoul]] and [[Daejeon]] to the Honam region. The name is often used to refer to people residing in the region. There is also [[Honam University]], which is located in [[Gwangju]], the biggest city in Honam.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.honam.ac.kr/Directions|title=How to get to Honam University|last=sourcers|website=www.honam.ac.kr|language=ko|access-date=2018-04-28}}</ref>

'''Córdoba''' ({{IPA|es|ˈkoɾðoβa}}, {{langx|es|Departamento de Córdoba}}) is a [[departments of Colombia|Department]] of the [[Republic of Colombia]] located to the north of this country in the Colombian [[Caribbean Region, Colombia|Caribbean Region]]. Córdoba faces to the north with the Caribbean Sea, to the northeast with the [[Sucre Department]], east with the [[Bolívar Department]] and south with the [[Antioquia Department]]. As of 2018, the population of Córdoba was estimated to be 1,784,783. Its capital is the city of [[Montería]].

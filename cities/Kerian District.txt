The '''Kerian District''' (alternately '''Krian''') is an administrative district in [[Perak]], [[Malaysia]]. It covers the northwestern corner of [[Perak]], bordering the states of [[Penang]] and [[Kedah]] to the north; the main town of Parit Buntar is located a mere {{Convert|37|km|abbr=on}} southeast of [[George Town, Penang|George Town]], [[Penang]]'s capital city. The district is well known for [[Bukit Merah, Perak|Bukit Merah]], a popular tourist destination.

Due to its closer proximity to [[Penang]], much of Kerian is also part of [[Greater Penang Conurbation|Greater Penang]], Malaysia's second largest conurbation.

The district is one of the main [[rice]]-growing areas in [[Perak]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.utusan.com.my/berita/wilayah/perak/6-tan-sehektar-1.133281|title=6 tan sehektar|first=A. MALEK|last=ASLIE|website=M.utudasn.com.my|access-date=2017-02-24|archive-date=2016-05-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160509105625/http://m.utusan.com.my/berita/wilayah/perak/6-tan-sehektar-1.133281|url-status=dead}}</ref>

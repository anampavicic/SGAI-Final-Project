'''Caquetá Department''' ({{IPA|es|kakeˈta}}) is a [[departments of Colombia|department]] of [[Colombia]]. Located in the [[Amazonas Region (Colombia)|Amazonas region]], Caquetá borders with the departments of [[Cauca Department|Cauca]] and [[Huila Department|Huila]] to the west, the department of [[Meta Department|Meta]] to the north, the department of [[Guaviare Department|Guaviare]] to the northeast, the department of [[Vaupés Department|Vaupés]] to the east, the departments of [[Amazonas (Colombian department)|Amazonas]] and [[Putumayo Department|Putumayo]] to the south covering a total area of 88,965&nbsp;km², the [[List of Colombian Departments by area|third largest in the country]]. Its capital is the city of [[Florencia, Caquetá|Florencia]].

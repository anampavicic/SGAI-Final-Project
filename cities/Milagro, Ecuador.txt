'''San Francisco de Milagro''' (also known as '''Milagro''', which is Spanish for ''Miracle'') is a [[city]] located in [[Guayas Province|Guayas]], [[Ecuador]]. It is the [[Canton seat|seat]] of [[Milagro Canton]].

Milagro is the fourth largest city in the province of [[Guayas (province)|Guayas]].  As of the [[census]] of 2022, there were 195,943 people residing within the canton limits.<ref>[https://www.citypopulation.de/en/ecuador/admin/guayas/0910__milagro/ Citypopulation.de] Population of Milagro Canton</ref>

[[Denisse Robles]], mayor (2014), pledged to get a city railway station and to improve the coverage of Milagro's drinking water.<ref>{{cite web|title=Entre el cambio y la continuidad|url=http://www.eltelegrafo.com.ec/noticias/politica/1/entre-el-cambio-y-la-continuidad
|work=El Telégrafo|date=13 January 2014|accessdate=30 July 2016|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160403093546/http://www.eltelegrafo.com.ec/noticias/politica/1/entre-el-cambio-y-la-continuidad|archivedate=3 April 2016|language=Spanish}}</ref> She was elected the mayor of Milagro in 2014 with 38% of the vote, finishing ahead of the incumbent mayor Francisco Asan.<ref>{{cite web|title=La reelección no fue para todos|url=http://expreso.ec/historico/la-reeleccion-no-fue-para-todos-FWGR_5765224|work=Expreso|date=24 February 2014|accessdate=29 July 2016|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160427045435/http://expreso.ec/historico/la-reeleccion-no-fue-para-todos-FWGR_5765224|archivedate=27 April 2016}}</ref> Robles assumed office in May 2014, becoming the first female mayor of the city, and the youngest mayor in the country.<ref>{{cite web|title=Denisse Robles asumió alcaldía de Milagro|url=http://www.tctelevision.com/elnoticiero/denisse-robles-asumio-alcaldia-milagro|work=TC Television|date=15 May 2014|accessdate=29 July 2016|language=Spanish|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304221000/http://www.tctelevision.com/elnoticiero/denisse-robles-asumio-alcaldia-milagro|archivedate=4 March 2016}}</ref>

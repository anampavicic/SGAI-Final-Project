'''Qods''' ({{langx|fa|قدس}}){{efn|Also known as '''Shahr-e Qods''' ("City of Qods"); formerly '''Karaj''', '''Qal‘eh Hasan''', and '''Qal‘eh-ye Ḩasan Khān'''.<ref>{{GEOnet3|-3079199}}</ref> Before Qods officially became a municipality in 1989, it was named '''Qal‘eh Hasan'''. The city is named after [[Jerusalem]], in Arabic spelling}} is a city in the [[Central District (Qods County)|Central District]] of [[Qods County]], [[Tehran province|Tehran]] province, [[Iran]], serving as capital of both the county and the district.<ref name="Malard, Qods, and Gomishan Counties Establishment">{{cite news|title=The government's agreement with 18 changes in national divisions: Three counties were added to the geographical map of the country|language=fa|website=dolat.ir|via=Secretariat of the Government Information Council|url=https://dolat.ir/detail/176393|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180205130020/https://dolat.ir/detail/176393|archive-date=5 February 2018|publisher=Ministry of the Interior, Board of Ministers|last=Davodi|first=Parviz|date=22 April 2009|access-date=22 November 2023}}</ref>

==Higher education==
The city has three universities: [[Islamic Azad University, Shahr-e-Qods Branch|Islamic Azad University, Shahr-eQods Branch]], University of Applied Science and Technology, Shahr-e-Qods Branch and Payam-e-Nour university.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.irancities.ir/showcity.aspx?code%3D288%26code2%3D16|title=قدس|access-date=March 26, 2016|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160328154253/http://irancities.ir/showcity.aspx?code=288&code2=16|archive-date=March 28, 2016}}</ref>

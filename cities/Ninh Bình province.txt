'''Ninh Bình''' is a [[Provinces of Vietnam|province]] of [[Vietnam]] in the [[Red River Delta]] region of the northern part of the country.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Tổng quan Du lịch Ninh Bình |url=https://sodulich.ninhbinh.gov.vn/vi/gioi-thieu/tong-quan-du-lich-ninh-binh-5.html |access-date=2023-11-13 |website=sodulich.ninhbinh.gov.vn}}</ref> The province is famous for a high density of natural and cultural attractions, including reserved parks in [[Cúc Phương National Park]] and Vân Long, grotto caves and rivers in [[Tràng An]], [[Tam Cốc-Bích Động]] and Múa Caves, historic monuments in the [[Hoa Lư]] ancient capital, Vietnam's largest buddhist worshiping complex ([[Bái Đính Temple]]), and the [[Phát Diệm Cathedral]] with "eclectic architectural style". Thanks to its adjacency to [[Hanoi]], day trips from the capital are easily manageable.

==Geography==
Ninh Bình is located to the south of the [[Northern Delta]], between the [[Red River (Vietnam)|Red]] and [[Ma River|Ma]] rivers. It is bordered by [[Hòa Bình province|Hòa Bình]] and [[Hà Nam province|Hà Nam]] to the north, [[Nam Định province|Nam Định]] to the northeast, and [[Thanh Hóa province|Thanh Hóa]] to the south and west. Ninh Bình also has a very short coastline abutting the [[Gulf of Tonkin]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Điều kiện tự nhiên |url=https://ninhbinh.gov.vn/dieu-kien-tu-nhien |access-date=2023-11-13 |website=ninhbinh.gov.vn}}</ref>

The population is 898,500 people, with a total area of {{Convert|1411.78|km2|mi2|abbr=on}}.<ref name="area_2022"/>
== Travel destinations ==
Thung Nham Ninh Binh, also known as Thung Chim, is about 12km west of [[Ninh Bình]] city center. Thung Nham has an area of more than 300 hectares, including primeval forest landscapes, flooded forests, terrestrial cave systems, and bird gardens with a variety of precious bird species. Thung Nham Ninh Binh has a wild and lyrical beauty that has become one of the attractive destinations for domestic and foreign tourists. ==Demographics==
The [[List of ethnic groups in Vietnam|ethnic groups]] include the [[Vietnamese people|Viet]] (also called the [[Kinh]], the Vietnamese ethnic majority), as well as other groups such as the [[Yao people|Dao]], [[Hoa people|Hoa]], [[Hmong people|Hmong]], [[Muong people|Mường]], [[Nùng people|Nùng]], [[Tay people|Tày]] and [[Thai people|Thai]]. There are 23 ethnic groups, of which the Kinh account for more than 98%.

==Transportation==
Ninh Bình is located 91&nbsp;km from [[Hanoi]] and has both rail and road transport links. It has express rail connections with Hanoi in the north and [[Thanh Hóa]] and [[Vinh]] in the south. Buses from Hanoi's South Bus Station stop by Ninh Bình; the bus routes parallel and complement the rail route.

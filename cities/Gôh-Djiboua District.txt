'''Gôh-Djiboua District''' ({{langx|fr|District du Gôh-Djiboua}}) is one of fourteen [[Districts of Ivory Coast|administrative districts]] of [[Ivory Coast]]. The district is located in the southwest corner of the country. The capital of the district is [[Gagnoa]].

==Creation==
Gôh-Djiboua District was created in a 2011 administrative reorganisation of the [[subdivisions of Ivory Coast]].<ref>Décret n° 2011-263 du 28 septembre 2011 portant organisation du territoire national en Districts et en Régions.</ref> The territory of the district was composed by merging the former regions of [[Sud-Bandama]] (except the department of [[Fresco Department|Fresco]]) and [[Fromager]].<ref name=statoids>{{cite web|title=Districts of Côte d'Ivoire (Ivory Coast)|url=http://www.statoids.com/uci.html|website=Statoids.com|access-date=24 June 2015}}</ref>

==Administrative divisions==
Gôh-Djiboua District is currently subdivided into two [[Regions of Ivory Coast|regions]] and the following [[departments of Ivory Coast|departments]]:
* '''[[Gôh Region]]''' (formerly [[Fromager Region]]) (region seat also in [[Gagnoa]])
** [[Gagnoa Department]]
** [[Oumé Department]]
* '''[[Lôh-Djiboua Region]]''' (formerly [[Sud-Bandama Region]]) (region seat in [[Divo, Ivory Coast|Divo]])
** [[Divo Department]]
** [[Lakota Department]]
** [[Guitry Department]]

==Population==
According to the 2021 census, Gôh-Djiboua District has a population of 2,088,440.<ref name="pop"/>

'''Gonda''' is a city and [[municipal board]] of [[Gonda district]] in the [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Uttar Pradesh]]. It is situated 190&nbsp;km north east of the state capital [[Lucknow]]. Gonda is divided into four [[tehsil]]s named Gonda, Colonelganj, Tarabganj and [[Mankapur]].

From Mumbai many trains are available for Gonda the most comfortable train in the timing point of view is Khushi Nagar express, which arrives at Gonda at 5am around.

From Gonda Station you can get shared vehicles for Utraola, Baggi Road, Balrampur, Mankapur etc.

'''Capiatá''' ({{IPA|es|ka.pjaˈta}}) is a city and a [[Districts of Paraguay|district]] in [[Central Department]], [[Paraguay]].

It is the only city in [[Central Department|Central]] that borders seven cities: [[Aregua]], [[Itaugua]], [[Juan Augusto Saldívar]], [[Luque]], [[Ñemby]], [[San Lorenzo, Paraguay|San Lorenzo]] and [[Ypane]].

It is the location of the [[Francisco López Military Academy]].

== Etymology ==
The city was called Capiâtá in [[Classical Guarani]]. The name is believed to derive from the Spanish reading of the written name, thought to be derived from ''capyí'' "grass, hay" and ''atȃ́'' "hard."

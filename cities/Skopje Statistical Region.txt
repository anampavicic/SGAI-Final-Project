The '''Skopje Statistical Region''' ({{lang-mk|Скопски Регион}}; [[Albanian language|Albanian]]: Rajoni i Shkupit) is one of eight [[statistical regions of North Macedonia]]. The region is located in the north of the country, bordering [[Kosovo]]. Internally, it borders the [[Vardar Statistical Region|Vardar]], [[Polog Statistical Region|Polog]], [[Northeastern Statistical Region|Northeastern]], [[Eastern Statistical Region|Eastern]], and [[Southwestern Statistical Region|Southwestern]] statistical regions.

==Demographics==
[[File:Ethnic groups in Skopje Region of North Macedonia en.svg|thumb|right|300px|Map of the majority ethnic groups in the region]]

===Population===
The current population of the Skopje Statistical Region is 607,007 citizens, according to the last population census in 2021, accounting for 33.0% of the total national population. The region is the largest by population in North Macedonia.

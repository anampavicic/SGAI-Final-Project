'''Betim''' is a town in [[Minas Gerais]], [[Brazil]]. The city belongs to the mesoregion Metropolitan of Belo Horizonte (BH) and to the microregion of Belo Horizonte. It is the fifth largest city in Minas Gerais and one of the 50 largest cities in the Southeast of Brazil.

Betim is home to a [[Petrobras]]'s oil refineries, as well as [[Fiat]]'s largest factory, which opened in 1976.<ref name=istoe>{{cite news|title=Morre ex-governador de Minas Gerais Rondon Pacheco |url=http://istoe.com.br/morre-ex-governador-de-minas-gerais-rondon-pacheco/ |work=[[ISTOÉ]] |date=2016-07-04 |access-date=2016-07-25}}</ref> The city has an important role not only in the state's economy, but in the whole country's as well.

== Geography ==
Betim is located on the southwest of [[Intermediate Geographic Region of Belo Horizonte|Belo Horizonte IGR]], with Rio Paraopeba flowing through its south. Its average elevation is 830 meters above the sea level.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Betim topographic map, elevation, terrain |url=https://en-gb.topographic-map.com/map-dc2xtf/Betim/?center=-19.96819,-44.19834 |access-date=2023-04-27 |website=Topographic maps |language=en}}</ref>

=== Climate ===
Betim has [[Humid subtropical climate|Humid Subtropical Climate]] (Cwa). It sees the most rainfall in December, with 268.5 mm of average precipitation; and the least rainfall in July, with 5.5 mm of average precipitation.

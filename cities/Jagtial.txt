====Bus====
[[TGSRTC]] has a bus depot named JAGTIAL(JGTL).Jagtial bus depot is one of the major profitable depots in TGSRTC.Many rural people ply from villages to this city daily for their needs.Jagtial has direct connectivity to [[Hyderabad]], [[Vijayawada]], [[Shirdi]], [[Mumbai]], [[Ongole]], [[Tirupati]] etc.,

===Railway===
<!-- [[File:Jagtiyal Rail.png|thumb|Jagtiyal Railway Station]] -->
Station Code : LPJL
There is a railway station at Jagityal located at [[Lingampet]] village. So station name is Lingampet Jagityal. It is a part of the [[Peddapalli–Nizamabad line|Peddapalli-Nizamabad line]].

This station is a major exporter of [[mangoes]], [[maize]], rice and other agricultural products. In the future this station will become a major junction with passengers and goods transport. A weekly Train between Lokmanya Tilak (Mumbai) - Karimnagar was started in 2022 has a halt at Jagityal . Daily a [[MEMU]] Pushpull train from Nizamabad-Sirpur town runs and stops at this station. There is a proposal of Jagityal City people and surrounding rural people for running of Tirupati- Karimnagar express to extend and Start from Jagityal Railway Station.

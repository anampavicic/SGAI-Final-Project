'''Naujan''' ({{IPA|fil|naʔuˈhan}} {{Respell|naʔ|OO|han}}), officially the '''Municipality of Naujan''' ({{lang-tgl|Bayan ng Naujan}}),  is a 1st class [[municipality of the Philippines|municipality]] in the [[Philippine Province|province]] of [[Oriental Mindoro]], [[Philippines]]. Its population was 109,587 at the 2020 census.{{PH census|current}}

==History==
On January 4, 1905, under Act 1280, Naujan assumed the status of a full-fledged municipality. Its boundaries were permanently established in 1919.

==Geography==
It covers a land area of {{convert|50310|ha}}, making it the largest municipality in the province and accounting for 12% of the province's total land area. Naujan is {{convert|26|km}} from [[Calapan]].

===Barangays===
Naujan is politically subdivided into 71 [[barangay]]s. Each barangay consists of [[purok]]s and some have [[sitios]].

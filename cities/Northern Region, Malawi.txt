The '''Northern Region''' is a [[Regions of Malawi|region]] of [[Malawi]]. It had a population of 2,289,780 in 2018, and covers an area of 26,931&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>, making it the smallest region both by population and area. Its capital city is [[Mzuzu]]. Starting in the north and going clockwise, the Northern Region borders on [[Tanzania]], [[Lake Malawi]], Malawi's [[Central Region, Malawi|Central Region]], and [[Zambia]].

==Geography==
Of the 28 [[Districts of Malawi|districts]] in Malawi, six are located within the Northern Region.

*[[Chitipa District|Chitipa]]
*[[Karonga District|Karonga]]
*[[Likoma District|Likoma]]
*[[Mzimba District|Mzimba]]
*[[Nkhata Bay District|Nkhata Bay]]
*[[Rumphi District|Rumphi]]

In addition to mainland parts of Malawi, the Northern Region also includes the islands of [[Chizumulu Island|Chizumulu]] and [[Likoma Island|Likoma]] in [[Lake Malawi]], which together make up [[Likoma District]].

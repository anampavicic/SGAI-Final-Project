'''Negage''' is a town and [[municipalities of Angola|municipality]] (''município'') of the [[Uíge Province|Uíge]] [[provinces of Angola|province]] in [[Angola]].<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.statoids.com/yao.html | title = City councils (''municípios'') of Angola | publisher = Statoids | access-date = 9 Apr 2009}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.angola.org/referenc/provinces.html | title = Reference Center: Provinces | publisher = Angolan Embassy in the United States | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20060211115650/http://www.angola.org/referenc/provinces.html | archive-date= 11 February 2006}}</ref> The municipality had a population of 137,559 in 2014.<ref name =Censo2014>{{cite web|url=https://www.ine.gov.ao/images/Censo_2014/Publicacao__Uige__14_11_2016.pdf |title=Resultados Definitivos Recenseamento Geral da População e Habitação – 2014 Província do Uíge |access-date=3 May 2020 |website=Instituto Nacional de Estatística, República de Angola| df=dmy }}</ref> It is served by [[Negage Airport]].

The city is crossed by the [[Cauã River]]. The soil is arable and the area is noted for coffee production. It has a mission of Capuchin priests, a church dating from the 1970s, and  two soccer fields located in the neighborhood of Capopa. It has a hospital and clinic space for more than 40 beds, and provided housing for clinicians, doctors and nurses within the hospital perimeter.

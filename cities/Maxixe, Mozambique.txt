'''Maxixe''' ({{IPA|pt|maˈʃiʃi}}) is the largest [[List of cities in Mozambique|city]] and economic capital of the province of [[Inhambane Province|Inhambane]], Mozambique. It is situated on the [[Indian Ocean]] at 23°52′S 35°23′E just south of the [[Tropic of Capricorn]], in a bay opposite historic [[Inhambane City]] which can be reached by local ferries.  Although Maxixe is the province's principal city, Inhambane City is the seat of the provincial government.

[[Image:Inhambane-dhow.JPG|thumb|left|Dhow ferrying passengers from Inhambane to Maxixe in 2006.]]

By road the distance to Inhambane is far longer and is approximately 60 kilometres.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.go2africa.com/mozambique/maxixe|title=www.go2africa.com|accessdate=2008-06-18}}</ref>

Maxixe serves as a convenient stop for transportation heading North or South along the EN1.

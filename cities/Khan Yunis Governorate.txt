The '''Khan Yunis Governorate''' ({{langx|ar|محافظة خان يونس}} ''{{transl|ar|DIN|Muḥāfaẓat Ḫān Yūnis}}'') is one of 16 [[Governorates of Palestine]], located in the southern [[Gaza Strip]]. Its district capital is [[Khan Yunis]]. According to the [[Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics]], the governorate had a population of 426,056 in mid-2022.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.pcbs.gov.ps/statisticsIndicatorsTables.aspx?lang=en&table_id=710 |title=Projected Mid -Year Population for Khan Yunis Governorate by Locality 2017-2026 |date=May 30, 2021 |website=pcbs.gov.ps |publisher=Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics |access-date=December 4, 2023}}</ref> Its land area is 69.61% urban, 12.8% rural, and 17.57% comprising the [[Khan Yunis Camp|Khan Yunis refugee camp]].<ref>[http://www.pcbs.gov.ps/Portals/_pcbs/populati/pop15.aspx Khan Yunis Governorate Small Area Populations] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120207051155/http://www.pcbs.gov.ps/Portals/_pcbs/populati/pop15.aspx |date=2012-02-07 }} Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics</ref>

'''León''' ({{IPA|es|leˈon}}) is a [[Departments of Nicaragua|department]] in [[Nicaragua]]. It covers an area of 5,138&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup> and has a population of 423,012 (2021 estimate). The capital is the city of [[León, Nicaragua|León]].

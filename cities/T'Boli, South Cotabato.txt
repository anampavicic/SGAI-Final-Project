'''TBoli''', officially the '''Municipality of TBoli''' ({{langx|hil|Banwa sang Tboli}}; {{langx|ceb|Lungsod sa Tboli}}; {{langx|tl|Bayan ng Tboli}}), is a 1st class [[municipality of the Philippines|municipality]] in the [[Philippine Province|province]] of [[South Cotabato]], [[Philippines]]. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 101,049 people.{{PH census|current}} The town is named after the indigenous natives themselves, the [[Tboli people]].

Located in the town is '''Lake Holon''' within [[Mount Melibengoy|Mount Parker]]. The lake was declared as the cleanest inland body of water in the entire country in 2003 and 2004.<ref>[https://lifestyle.mb.com.ph/2017/01/06/south-cotabatos-lake-holon-closed-to-visitors-for-rehab/ South Cotabato’s Lake Holon closed to visitors for rehab &ndash; ''Manila Bulletin'']</ref>

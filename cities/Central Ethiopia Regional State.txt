The '''Central Ethiopia Regional State''' is a [[Regions of Ethiopia|regional state]] in [[Ethiopia]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://borkena.com/2023/08/19/ethiopias-two-new-regional-states-formed-central-ethiopia-south-ethiopia/ |title=Archived copy |access-date=2023-09-17 |archive-date=2023-09-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230929062118/https://borkena.com/2023/08/19/ethiopias-two-new-regional-states-formed-central-ethiopia-south-ethiopia/ |url-status=live }}</ref> It was formed from northern part of the then [[Southern Nations, Nationalities, and Peoples' Region]] (SNNPR) on 19 August 2023. Its formation was effected when [[South Ethiopia Regional State]] was established after a successful [[2023 South Ethiopia Region referendum|referendum]] from the former [[SNNPR]]. The largest [[ethnic group]]s in the region are the [[Gurage]] and [[Hadiya people|Hadiya]], constituting 70 percent of the region's population, and the president hails from the [[Gurage]] people.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.ena.et/web/eng/w/eng_3222547#:~:text=The%20Central%20Ethiopia%20region%20constitutes,Chief%20Administrator%20of%20the%20region.|title=Central Ethiopia, Southern Ethiopia Regional States Established|website=www.ena.et|access-date=2023-09-17|archive-date=2023-09-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230918100328/https://www.ena.et/web/eng/w/eng_3222547#:~:text=The%20Central%20Ethiopia%20region%20constitutes,Chief%20Administrator%20of%20the%20region.|url-status=live}}</ref>

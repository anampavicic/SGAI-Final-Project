'''Monastir Governorate''' ({{Langx|ar|ولاية المنستير}} ''{{transl|ar|Wilāyat Munastīr}}'' {{IPA|ar|munæsˈtiːr|pron}}; {{langx|fr|Gouvernorat de Monastir}}) is one of the twenty-four [[Governorates of Tunisia|governorates]] of [[Tunisia]]. It is situated in north-eastern Tunisia. It covers an area of 1,019&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup> (393&nbsp;mi<sup>2</sup>) and has a population of 548,828 (2014 census).<ref>{{in lang|fr}} [http://rgph2014.ins.tn/fr/resultats Census 2014 (National Institute of Statistics)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141029170649/http://rgph2014.ins.tn/fr/resultats |date=2014-10-29 }}</ref> The capital is [[Monastir, Tunisia|Monastir]].

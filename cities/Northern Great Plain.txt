The '''Northern Great Plain''' ({{langx|hu|Észak-Alföld}} {{IPA-hu|'eːsɒk 'ɒlføld|}}) is a statistical ([[Nomenclature of Territorial Units for Statistics|NUTS]] 2) region of [[Hungary]]. It is part of the [[Great Plain and North]] (NUTS 1) region. The Northern Great Plain includes the counties of [[Hajdú-Bihar]], [[Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok]], and [[Szabolcs-Szatmár-Bereg]], with a total area of {{convert|17749|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}} and a population of around 1.5 million.<ref>[http://www.itdh.com/engine.aspx?page=Northern_Great_Plain Northern Great Plain]</ref> The region is in the eastern part of [[Hungary]]. It borders [[northern Hungary]] to the north, [[Ukraine]] to the north-east, [[Romania]] to the south-east, the [[Southern Great Plain]] region of Hungary to the south, and [[central Hungary]] to the west. The region's centre, and capital city is [[Debrecen]], the [[List of cities and towns of Hungary#Largest cities in Hungary|second largest city]] within Hungary.

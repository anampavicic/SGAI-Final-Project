'''San Miguelito''' is a city and [[Districts of Panama|district]] (''distrito'') of [[Panamá Province]] in [[Panama]]. The population according to the [[2000 Panamanian census|2000 census]] was 293,745;<ref name="Statoids">{{cite web|url=http://www.statoids.com/ypa.html|title=Districts of Panama|publisher=Statoids|access-date=April 12, 2009}}</ref> the latest official estimate (for 2019) is 375,409.<ref name="ReferenceA">Instituto Nacional de Estadística y Censo, Panama.</ref> The district covers an area of 50.1&nbsp;km². San Miguelito district is completely [[enclaved]] within Panama District (which completely surrounds it) and it is included in the Panama City Metropolitan Area.<ref name="Statoids"/>

[[Association football|Football]] player [[Luis Tejada]] was born in San Miguelito, and both [[Blas Pérez]] and [[Kevin Kurányi]] were once residents of the district.

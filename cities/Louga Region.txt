'''Louga''' is a city and region of [[Senegal]]. The region is located to the northwest part of the country and [[Louga|Louga city]] is in the northwest of the region - about 50&nbsp;km inland from the Atlantic coast.

==Departments==
Louga region is divided into 3 [[Department (country subdivision)|départements]]:
*[[Kébémer Department|Kébémer Département]]
*[[Linguère Department|Linguère Département]]
*[[Louga Department|Louga Département]]

==Geography==
Louga is traversed by the northwesterly line of equal latitude and longitude.

'''Santa Cruz do Capibaribe''' is a Brazilian [[municipality]] in the [[Pernambuco|State of Pernambuco]]. It is the third largest city in the mesoregion of [[Agreste]] of Pernambuco with a population of 109,897 inhabitants in 2020. It has 335.309&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup> (208.5 square miles) of total area and it is located about 185.7&nbsp;km (115.4 miles) from the capital [[Recife]].

The city is famous for its textile industry together with [[Caruaru]] and [[Toritama]].

==Economy==

{{See also|Largest Cities of Northeast Region, Brazil}}
The main economic activities in Santa Cruz do Capibaribe are related with the textile industry (employs 41%) and general commerce.

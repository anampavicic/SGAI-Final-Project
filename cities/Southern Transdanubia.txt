'''Southern Transdanubia''' ({{langx|hu|Dél-Dunántúl}} {{IPA-hu|'deːl ˈdunaːntuːl|}}) is a statistical ([[Nomenclature of Territorial Units for Statistics|NUTS]] 2) region of [[Hungary]]. It is part of the [[Transdanubia]] (NUTS 1) region. Southern Transdanubia includes the counties of [[Somogy County|Somogy]], [[Tolna (county)|Tolna]], and [[Baranya (county)|Baranya]]. Its capital and largest city is [[Pécs]]. It borders [[Croatia]] to the south, the [[Southern Great Plain]] NUTS 2 region to the east, the [[Central Transdanubia]] NUTS 2 region to the north, and the [[Western Transdanubia]] NUTS 2 region to the north-west.

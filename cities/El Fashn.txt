'''El Fashn''' ({{langx|ar|الفشن}}, from {{Langx|cop|ⲡⲃⲏϣⲛ|lit=the castle}}<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |last=Peust |first=Carsten |title=Die Toponyme vorarabischen Ursprungs im modernen Ägypten |url=http://www.peust.de/ortsnamen_original.pdf |page=40}}</ref>) is a city in [[Egypt]]. It is situated near the southern borders of [[Beni Suef Governorate]].<ref>[http://www.marefa.org/index.php/%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%81%D8%B4%D9%86 الفشن - المعرفة]</ref>

The city was called Phebichis ({{Langx|grc|Φεβιχις}})<ref>{{Cite web |title=TM Places |url=https://www.trismegistos.org/place/1755 |access-date=2022-11-09 |website=www.trismegistos.org}}</ref> in [[Ptolemaic Kingdom|Ptolemaic]] and [[Byzantine Egypt]].

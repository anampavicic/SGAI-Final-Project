'''Akçakale''' ({{langx|ar|أقجة قلعة}}) is a municipality and [[Districts of Turkey|district]] of [[Şanlıurfa Province]], [[Turkey]].<ref>[https://www.e-icisleri.gov.tr/Anasayfa/MulkiIdariBolumleri.aspx Büyükşehir İlçe Belediyesi], Turkey Civil Administration Departments Inventory. Retrieved 19 September 2023.</ref> Its area is 1,038&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.harita.gov.tr/uploads/files-folder/il_ilce_alanlari.xlsx|title=İl ve İlçe Yüz ölçümleri|publisher=General Directorate of Mapping|access-date=19 September 2023}}</ref> and its population is 123,721 (2022).<ref name=tuik>{{Cite web |title=Address-based population registration system (ADNKS) results dated 31 December 2022, Favorite Reports|url=https://biruni.tuik.gov.tr/medas/?kn=95&locale=en |access-date=19 September 2023|publisher=[[TÜİK]]|language=en|format=XLS}}</ref>

Akçakale forms a [[divided city]] with [[Tell Abyad]] in [[Syria]], maintaining a [[border crossing]]. The Mayor is Mehmet Yalçınkaya ([[Justice and Development Party (Turkey)|AKP]]).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.yenisafak.com/yerel-secim-2019/sanliurfa-akcakale-ilcesi-secim-sonuclari|title=Şanlıurfa Akçakale Seçim Sonuçları – Akçakale Yerel Seçim Sonuçları|last=Şafak|first=Yeni|date=2019-07-11|website=Yeni Şafak|language=tr-TR|access-date=2019-11-08}}</ref> The current [[Kaymakam|District Governor]] is Onur Şan.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.akcakale.gov.tr/kaymakam-onur-san|title=Akçakale Kaymakamlığı|website=www.akcakale.gov.tr|access-date=10 October 2023}}</ref>

==Syrian Civil War==
{{Main article|Syrian–Turkish border clashes during the Syrian Civil War}}
On October 3, 2012, Akçakale was hit by [[Shell (projectile)|shells]] from across the [[Syria–Turkey border|Syrian border]] in [[Tell Abyad]], killing five civilians.<ref name="figaro">[http://www.lefigaro.fr/international/2012/10/03/01003-20121003ARTFIG00707-crispation-apres-des-tirs-d-obus-syriens-en-turquie.php La Turquie riposte après des tirs d'obus syriens sur son sol], ''[[Le Figaro]]'', 04/10/2012</ref><ref>{{Cite news|title=Turkey hits targets inside Syria after border deaths|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-19822253|accessdate=3 October 2012|publisher=[[BBC News]]|date=4 October 2012}}</ref> In October 2014, there was an [[Islamic State in Iraq and the Levant]] presence in the town, due to the porous nature of the border at that time between Akçakale and Tell Abyad, which was then part of the Islamic State.<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.heraldscotland.com/news/world-news/no-one-here-trusts-anyone-any-more-inside-an-is-stronghold.25688850|title=No-one here trusts anyone anymore: Inside an Islamic State stronghold|work=[[Sunday Herald]] | author=Khalel, Sheren|author2=Vickery, Matthew|date=25 October 2014}}</ref>

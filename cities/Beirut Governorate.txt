'''Beirut Governorate''' ({{langx|ar|محافظة بيروت}}, {{transl|ar|Muhāfazat Bayrūt}}) is a [[governorates of Lebanon|Lebanese governorate]] that consists of one district and one city, [[Beirut]], which is also its capital, and the capital of [[Lebanon]].

The area of this governorate is 19.8&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup> (without [[suburbs]]); despite its small size, it is considered the most important region in Lebanon because of its economic, political, cultural, and social activity. The governor of the Beirut Governate is [[Greek Orthodox Christianity in Lebanon|Greek Orthodox]] according to tradition, while the mayor of the City of Beirut is [[Sunni Islam in Lebanon|Sunni Muslim]]. Beirut is known to be the most religiously diverse city in the Middle East. There are about 2.5 million people in Beirut and its suburbs ([[Greater Beirut]]).

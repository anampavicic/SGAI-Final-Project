'''Çarşamba''' is a municipality and [[Districts of Turkey|district]] of [[Samsun Province]], [[Turkey]].<ref>[https://www.e-icisleri.gov.tr/Anasayfa/MulkiIdariBolumleri.aspx Büyükşehir İlçe Belediyesi], Turkey Civil Administration Departments Inventory. Retrieved 12 July 2023.</ref> Its area is 774&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.harita.gov.tr/uploads/files-folder/il_ilce_alanlari.xlsx|title=İl ve İlçe Yüz ölçümleri|publisher=General Directorate of Mapping|access-date=12 July 2023}}</ref> and its population is 140,664 (2022).<ref name=tuik>{{Cite web |title=Address-based population registration system (ADNKS) results dated 31 December 2022, Favorite Reports|url=https://biruni.tuik.gov.tr/medas/?kn=95&locale=en |access-date=12 July 2023|publisher=[[TÜİK]]|language=en|format=XLS}}</ref> It is in the center of the [[Çarşamba Plain]]. The mayor is Hüseyin Dündar ([[Independent politician|Independent]]). [[Ferhan Şensoy]] - writer, actor and stage director - was born here.

The town is bisected by the [[Yeşilırmak (river)|Yeşilırmak river]] running south-to-north and by the [[State road D.010 (Turkey)|Black Sea Coastal Road (D010)]] which runs west-to-east. There is a sugar factory in Çarşamba district.<ref>{{Cite news|last=Gazete|first=Banka|date=20 November 2021|title=Aydemir: 'Yeter ki şeker fabrikası çalışsın'|page=https://gazetebanka.com/|work=Gazetebanka.com|url=https://gazetebanka.com/aydemir--yeter-ki-seker-fabrikasi-calissin--/11357/|access-date=20 November 2021}}</ref>

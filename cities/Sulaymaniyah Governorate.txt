'''Sulaymaniyah Governorate''' ({{langx|ckb|پارێزگای سلێمانی}}; {{langx|kmr|Parêzgeha Silêmaniyê}};<ref>{{cite news |title=Li sînorê parêzgeha Silêmaniyê gule li du binkeyên hikûmetê hatin berdan |url=http://www.basnews.com/index.php/kr/news/kurdistan/561002 |access-date=21 December 2019 |language=ku}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=پارێزگای سلێمانی |url=http://slemani.gov.krd/so/ |access-date=21 December 2019 |work=slemani.gov.krd |language=ku}}</ref> {{langx|ar| محافظة السليمانية}}) or '''Sulaymaniyah Province''' is a [[Governorates of Iraq|governorate]] in the [[Kurdistan Region]] of [[Iraq]]. Its largest city is [[Sulaymaniyah]]. [[Halabja Governorate]] was formerly the [[Halabja District, Sulaymaniyah Governorate|Halabja District]] of Sulaymaniyah, until it became a separate governorate in 2014.

== Provincial Government ==
* Governor: [[Haval Abubakir]]
* Deputy Governor: Ahmed Ali Ahmed
* Provincial Council Chairman (PCC): Azad Mohammad Amin

'''Sao José de Ribamar''' is a Brazilian [[Municipalities of Brazil|municipality]] in the [[Maranhão]] state. 179,028 was its estimated population in 2020. It is part of the [[São Luís Island]] together with [[São Luís, Maranhão|São Luís]], [[Raposa]], and [[Paço do Lumiar]].

The municipality contains part of the {{convert|1,535,310|ha}} [[Upaon-Açu/Miritiba/Alto Preguiças Environmental Protection Area]], created in 1992.<ref>{{citation|ref={{harvid|APA Upaon-Açu/Miritiba/Alto Preguiças – ISA}}|language=pt

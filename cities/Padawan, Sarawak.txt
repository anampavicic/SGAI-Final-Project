'''Padawan''' is a sub-district of [[Kuching District]], [[Sarawak]], [[Malaysia]]. The name is also used to refer to the [[local government]] in Sarawak, the [[Padawan Municipal Council]] (Majlis Perbandaran Padawan), and to [[Bidayuh]] Village, which is also known as Kampung Padawan. The Jalan Padawan is the name of a road near mile 23 of Kuching-Serian Highway.

==Transportation==

Padawan is connected with Kuching-Serian Highway, while 10th mile junction has its own Borneo Heights Link, uptown towards [[Borneo Highland Resort]]. Air transportation can be reached by [[Kuching International Airport]], about 4 miles from Kota Padawan (10th mile), hotspot area. Most land transportation are taxis, buses, mini van (known locally as 'Van Sapu') which still popular among the region.

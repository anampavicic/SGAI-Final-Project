'''Ziguinchor''' is a region of [[Senegal]] (regional capitals have the same name as their respective regions).  The region is also referred to historically and popularly as '''Basse [[Casamance]]'''.

==Departments==
Ziguinchor region is divided into 3 [[Departments of Senegal|departments]]:
*[[Bignona Department|Bignona Département]]
*[[Oussouye Department|Oussouye Département]]
*[[Ziguinchor Department|Ziguinchor Département]]

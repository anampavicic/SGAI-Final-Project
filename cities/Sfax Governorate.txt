The '''Sfax Governorate''' ({{Langx|ar|ولاية صفاقس}} ''{{transl|ar|Wilāya Ṣafāqis}}''; {{langx|fr|Gouvernorat de Sfax}}) is one of the [[governorates of Tunisia]]. The governorate has a population of 955,421 (2014)<ref>{{in lang|fr}} [http://rgph2014.ins.tn/fr/resultats Census 2014 (National Institute of Statistics)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141029170649/http://rgph2014.ins.tn/fr/resultats |date=2014-10-29 }}</ref> and an area of 7,545&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>.<ref>[http://www.gouvernorat-sfax.gov.tn/ Official site of the Sfax Governorate]</ref> Its capital is [[Sfax]]. It is along the east coast of Tunisia, and includes the [[Kerkennah Islands]].

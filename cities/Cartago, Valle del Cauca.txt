'''Cartago''' ({{IPA|es|kaɾˈtaɣo}}) is a city in southwestern [[Colombia]], about {{Convert|187|mi|km}} west of [[Bogotá]]. It is in the extreme northern portion of the [[Valle del Cauca Department|Valle del Cauca]]. It is located very close to the city of [[Pereira, Colombia|Pereira]], [[Risaralda Department|Risaralda]] about a 20-minute drive. It is the sixth largest city in Valle after [[Cali]], [[Palmira, Valle del Cauca|Palmira]], [[Buenaventura, Valle del Cauca|Buenaventura]], [[Tuluá]] and [[Jamundí]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Proyecciones de población|url=https://www.dane.gov.co/index.php/estadisticas-por-tema/demografia-y-poblacion/proyecciones-de-poblacion|website=DANE}}</ref> Per the [[2018 Colombian census]], Cartago's population was 142,902.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Telefónicas |first=Telencuestas-Encuestas |title=Cuántos habitantes tiene Cartago, Valle del Cauca en 2023 |url=https://telencuestas.com/censos-de-poblacion/colombia/2023/valle-del-cauca/cartago |access-date=2023-12-30 |website=Telencuestas |language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Vélez Correa |first=Charles Mauricio |last2=Pérez Blandón |first2=Juan David |date=2021 |title=Estudio de Factibilidad Para la Institución Intei en el Desarrollo de un Modelo de Viviendas Colaborativas Tipo Cohousing en la Región Norte Vallecaucana |trans-title=Feasibility Study for the Institución Intei in the Development of a Cohousing Type Collaborative Housing Model in the North Vallecaucana Region |url=https://bibliotecadigital.univalle.edu.co/bitstream/handle/10893/21079/CB-0605564.pdf?sequence=1 |access-date=2022-08-24 |website=[[University of Valle|Universidad del Valle]] |page=71 |language=es |type=PDF}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=2010-09-14 |title=Boletín Censo General 2005 Perfil Cartago Valle del Cauca |trans-title=General Census Bulletin 2005 Cartago Valle del Cauca Profile |url=https://www.dane.gov.co/files/censo2005/PERFIL_PDF_CG2005/76147T7T000.PDF |access-date=2022-08-24 |website=[[National Administrative Department of Statistics (Colombia)|National Administrative Department of Statistics]] |language=es}}</ref>

== Overview ==

Cartago features warm weather with an average of 24°C, Over the course of the year, the temperature typically varies from 20°C (67°F) to 29°C (84°F) and is rarely below 19°C (66°F) or above 31°C (88°F).<ref>{{Cite web |title=Cartago Climate, Weather By Month, Average Temperature (Colombia) – Weather Spark |url=https://weatherspark.com/y/22410/Average-Weather-in-Cartago-Colombia-Year-Round |access-date=2023-12-30 |website=weatherspark.com |language=en}}</ref> The city is home to multiple farmhouses and farm owners, recreational sites, discos and condos frequented by people coming from [[Pereira, Colombia|Pereira]].

== Education ==

The [[University of Valle]] has a branch campus in Cartago. The main Universidad del Valle campus is based in Cali, the department capital. The Cartago branch campus address is located at Calle 10 N° 19–05. Private institutions include the Instituto Técnico Colombiano, INTEC Cartago and Sena Institute.

'''Kotri''' ({{langx|sd|{{naskh|ڪوٽڙي}}}}, {{langx|ur|{{nq|کوٹری}}}}) is a city and the headquarters of the [[Kotri Taluka]] of [[Jamshoro District]] of [[Sindh]] province in Pakistan.<ref name=Kotri>{{cite web|url=http://www.dadu.sdnpk.org/dd5.htm |title=Historical Places of Dadu|website= Population Census Organisation, Government of Pakistan website| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080918085401/http://www.dadu.sdnpk.org/dd5.htm |archive-date=2008-09-18|access-date=9 April 2023|url-status=dead }}</ref> Located on the right bank of the [[Indus River]], it is the 118th [[List of cities in Pakistan by population|largest city in Pakistan by population]].<ref name=city/>

==Name==
The name ''Koṭri'' is the diminutive form of the word ''koṭ'', meaning "fort"; thus, the name means "little fort".<ref name="Gidwani">{{cite journal |last1=Gidwani |first1=Parso J. |title=Place-Names of Sindh |journal=Bulletin of the Deccan College Post-Graduate and Research Institute |date=1990 |volume=49 |pages=153–156 |jstor=42930281 }}</ref>

==Demographics==
According to the [[1998 Pakistan Census]], the population of Kotri city was recorded as 62,085. As per the [[2023 Census of Pakistan]], the population of city was recorded as 106,615.<ref name=city/>

==Economy==
Kotri is a hub for textile production and fishing.<ref name="fishing">{{cite journal|title=Socio-economic well-being through fishing in Kotri downstream: should the river be kept on flowing?|url=https://www.researchgate.net/publication/315662008_Socio-Economic_Well-Being_Through_Fishing_In_Kotri_Downstream_Should_The_River_Be_Kept_On_Flowing|last=Makhdoom|first=Tayyaba Rafique|last2=Shah|first2=Syed Abdul Sattar|last3=Makhdoom|first3=Faiza|year=2016|journal=The Government: Research Journal of Political Science|volume=5|pages=195–209}}</ref>

==Notable people==
*[[Ali Baba (writer)|Ali Muhammad]] (1940 – 8 August 2016), known professionally as Ali Baba (Sindhi: علي بابا, Urdu: علی بابا), was a notable Sindhi-language drama writer and novelist. He was born in Kotri. He died on 8 August 2016 due to a heart attack in his home at Karachi.

'''Santo Antônio de Jesus''' is a [[List of cities in Brazil|city]] in the eastern part of the state of [[Bahia]], [[Brazil]], located west of the state capital [[Salvador, Brazil|Salvador]]. It is part of the mesoregion [[Metropolitana de Salvador]]. The population is 102,380 (2020 est.) in an area of 261.35&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>. It is an important commercial and a service centre in the micro-region.

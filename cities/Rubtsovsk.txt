'''Rubtsovsk''' ({{langx|ru|Рубцо́вск}}, {{IPA|ru|rʊpˈtsofsk|pron}}) is a [[types of inhabited localities in Russia|city]] in [[Altai Krai]], [[Russia]], located on the [[Aley River]] ([[Ob River|Ob]]'s [[tributary]]) {{convert|281|km|sp=us}} southwest of [[Barnaul]]. Population: {{ru-census2010|147,002|;}} {{ru-census2002|163,063|;}} {{su-census1989|171,792|;}} 167,000 (1975); 111,000 (1959); 75,334 (1939).

==History==
It was founded in 1892.{{citation needed|date=April 2010}}

A number of [[anti-Semitic]] incidents took place in the city in 1945.<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=iO1u1qjs61IC&q=Rubtsovsk+1945&pg=RA1-PA228 |title=War, Holocaust and Stalinism: a ... - Google Książki |isbn=9783718657391 |access-date=2010-04-08|last1=Redlich |first1=Shimon |last2=Anderson |first2=Kirill Mikhaĭlovich |last3=Altman |first3=I. |year=1995 |publisher=Psychology Press }}</ref>

During the [[2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine]], the city was the home city of several soldiers associated with the [[Bucha massacre]]<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-61442387 | title=Ukraine: The children's camp that became an execution ground | work=BBC News | date=May 15, 2022 }}</ref> and also a major destination for Ukrainian war loot.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://meduza.io/feature/2022/04/15/poltonny-trofeev | title=Полтонны "трофеев" "Медуза" разыскала российских военных, отправлявших огромные посылки от границы с Украиной в Рубцовск. Именно в этот город идет большинство таких грузов }}</ref>

==Administrative and municipal status==
Within the [[subdivisions of Russia#Administrative divisions|framework of administrative divisions]], Rubtsovsk serves as the [[administrative center]] of [[Rubtsovsky District]], even though it is not a part of it.<ref name="Ref5">Law #28-ZS</ref> As an administrative division, it is incorporated separately as the '''[[city of federal subject significance|city of krai significance]] of Rubtsovsk'''—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the [[administrative divisions of Altai Krai|districts]].<ref name="Ref5">Law #28-ZS</ref> As a [[subdivisions of Russia#Municipal divisions|municipal division]], the city of krai significance of Rubtsovsk is incorporated as '''Rubtsovsk Urban Okrug'''.<ref name="Ref546">Law #83-ZS</ref>

==Transportation==
The [[Rubtsovsk Airport]] is a derelict airfield which is used most commonly by local youth for drag racing.{{citation needed|date=September 2010}}

==Geography==
The city is located on the [[Aley River]] 281 kilometers (175&nbsp;mi) southwest of Barnaul, the largest city and administrative centre of Altai Krai.

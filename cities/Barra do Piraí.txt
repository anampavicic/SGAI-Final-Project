'''Barra do Piraí''' ({{IPA|pt|ˈbaʁɐ du piɾɐˈi}}) is a [[List of municipalities in Rio de Janeiro|municipality]] of the [[Brazil]]ian state of [[Rio de Janeiro (state)|Rio de Janeiro]]. It is located at latitude 22º28'12" South and longitude 43º49'32" East. Its population is 100,764 (2020) and its area is 578.471&nbsp;km².<ref>[[Brazilian Institute of Geography and Statistics|IBGE]] - {{cite web|url=http://www.ibge.gov.br/cidadesat/default.php |title=Archived copy |access-date=2014-08-03 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070109005631/http://www.ibge.gov.br/cidadesat/default.php |archive-date=2007-01-09 }}</ref> It is 114&nbsp;km from [[Rio de Janeiro]].

The municipality contains part of the {{convert|5952|ha}} [[Serra da Concórdia State Park]], created in 2002.<ref>{{citation|language=pt|ref={{harvid|Parque Estadual da Serra da Concórdia – Inea}}

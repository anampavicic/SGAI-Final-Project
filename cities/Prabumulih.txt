'''Prabumulih''' is a city in [[South Sumatra]], [[Indonesia]]. It was originally part of [[Muara Enim Regency]], from which it was separated to become an independent city on 21 June 2001. It has an area of 447.31&nbsp;km² and had a population of 161,814 at the 2010 Census<ref name="Biro Pusat Statistik 2011">Biro Pusat Statistik, Jakarta, 2011.</ref> and 193,196 at the 2020 Census;<ref name="Badan Pusat Statistik 2021">Badan Pusat Statistik, Jakarta, 2021.</ref> the official estimate as at mid 2023 was 200,673 (comprising 100,908 males and 99,765 females).<ref name="Badan Pusat Statistik 2024">Badan Pusat Statistik, Jakarta, 28 February 2024, ''Kota Prabumulih Dalam Angka 2024'' (Katalog-BPS 1102001.1673)</ref> Geographically, this city is located at 3°25′58″S 104°14′8″E in the heart of the province, and it is surrounded on all sides by Muara Enim Regency, apart from a short stretch bordering [[Penukal Abab Lematang Ilir Regency]].

== Economy ==
Prabumulih is one of main producers of [[crude oil]] and [[natural gas]] in South Sumatra. The city is known as "''kota nanas"'' (Pineapple City) because this is known as a centre for pineapple production in the region.

== Airports ==
There used to be an airport in Prabumulih, which is no longer active.

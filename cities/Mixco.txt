'''Mixco''' ({{IPA|es|ˈmisko}}) is a [[city]] and municipality in the [[Guatemala Department|Guatemala department]] of [[Guatemala]]. It is next to the main Guatemala City municipality and has become part of the [[Guatemala City Metropolitan Area]].  Most of Mixco is separated from the City by [[canyons]], for which a multitude of bridges have been created.  It is the second largest city in Guatemala after [[Guatemala City]], with a population of 475,777.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Antecedentes Históricos – Municipalidad de Mixco |url=https://www.munimixco.gob.gt/antecedentes/ |access-date=2023-03-25 |language=es}}</ref> [[Ciudad San Cristóbal]] is located within Mixco.

== Administrative division ==
[[File:Guatemala - Mixco.png|thumb|200px|[[Guatemala Department]] map, showing Mixco municipality area.]]

The municipality is divided into zones with residential neighborhoods, villages, settlements and the municipal capital.  Due to its close proximity to [[Guatemala City]], several villages were turned into residential neighborhoods.

==Sports==
[[Deportivo Mixco]] football club play in the [[Primera División de Ascenso|Guatemalan second division]]. They have been playing their home games at different locations and plan to build the new Estadio de La Tierra de Campeones.<ref>[http://deportivomixco.com/desplieganews.php?codigo=49 Estadio del Deportivo Mixco] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110709013518/http://deportivomixco.com/desplieganews.php?codigo=49 |date=2011-07-09 }} - Deportivo Mixco {{in lang|es}}</ref>

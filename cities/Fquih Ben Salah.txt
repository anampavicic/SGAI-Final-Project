'''Fquih Ben Salah''' ({{Langx|ar|الفقيه بن صالح}}) is a city in central [[Morocco]], located in the region of [[Béni Mellal-Khénifra]]. According to the [[2014 Moroccan census]], it has a population of 205,720,<ref name=census2014/> up from 128,446 in 2004.<ref>{{cite web|title=Province de Fquih Ben Salah |url=http://www.benimellalkhenifra.ma/fr/province/fquih-ben-salah|date=July 2021|access-date=September 15, 2022}}</ref> Its main economic activity is agriculture.

== History ==
The city is located in the territory of the [[Arabs|Arab]] tribe of [[Beni ʿAmir|Beni Amir]], which is part of the [[Tadla]] tribal confederation.

'''Gudivada''' is a city in [[Krishna district]] of the Indian state of [[Andhra Pradesh]]. It is a municipality and the headquarters of [[Gudivada mandal]] in [[Gudivada revenue division]].<ref name=census /><ref>{{cite web|title=Krishna District Mandals |url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/maps/atlas/28part31.pdf|publisher=Census of India |access-date=17 July 2015|pages=504, 528–529}}</ref> It is one of the cities in the state to be a part of [[Andhra Pradesh Capital Region]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Declaration of A.P. Capital Region |url=https://andhranation.files.wordpress.com/2014/12/new-mandals-added-in-crda.pdf |website=Andhra Nation |publisher=Municipal Administration and Urban Development Department |access-date=23 September 2015 |date=22 September 2015 |archive-date=10 August 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210810085549/https://andhranation.files.wordpress.com/2014/12/new-mandals-added-in-crda.pdf |url-status=dead }}</ref> It is the [[List of cities in Andhra Pradesh|twenty-seventh most populous]] city in [[Andhra Pradesh]] and the [[List of cities in India by population|three-hundredth most populous]] city in [[India]] with a population of 118,167 according to the 2011 [[Census of India]].

== History ==
[[File:Holy relic sites map of Andhra Pradesh.jpg|thumb|Holy relic sites map of Andhra Pradesh]][[File:Gudiwada Reliquaries (BM).JPG|thumb|Reliquaries from the stupa at Gudiwada in the [[British Museum]]]]
The name Gudivada was derived from ''Gudi'' meaning Temple and ''Vada'' means Settlement or town in [[Telugu language]]. The presence of [[Human settlement|Habitation]] at this city may be dated back to 3rd Century BCE or 2nd Century BCE since the time of Sathavahanas as per some inscriptions.<ref>{{cite web|last=Spectrum |first=Digital |url=http://livegudivada.blogspot.in/2008/06/gudivada-history.html |title=Gudivada: Gudivada History |publisher=Livegudivada.blogspot.in |date=2008-06-05 |access-date=2013-05-01}}</ref> Archaeological excavations<ref>{{cite web |url=http://asi.nic.in/ |title=Archaeological Survey of India |publisher=Asihyd.ap.nic.in |access-date=2013-05-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110629155409/http://asi.nic.in/ |archive-date=29 June 2011 |url-status=dead }}</ref> conducted at Gudivada yielded an ancient [[Buddhist]] stupa mound and Jain [[relics]]. Gudivada is religiously diversified city with many [[Hindu]] Temples and important [[Jain]] [[Tirtha (Jainism)|Tirth]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.jinalaya.com/india/gudivada.htm |title=Shri Gudivada Tirth – Jain Temples in Rest of India |publisher=www.Jinalaya.com |access-date=2013-05-01}}</ref> of Bhagawan [[Parshvanath]] located at market road. ''Vidarbhapuri'' was one of its earlier names.<ref>{{cite book|last1=Gupta|first1=Om|title=Encyclopaedia of India, Pakistan and Bangladesh|date=April 2006|publisher=Gyan Publishing House|isbn=9788182053892|page=842|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=eENU_a8c79MC&dq=vidarbhapuri+gudivada&pg=PA842|language=en}}</ref>

== Geography ==
It is located in the [[Coastal Andhra]] region of the state. Gudivada is located 41 kilometres (25.5&nbsp;mi) east of the district headquarters, [[Machilipatnam]], and 45 kilometres (27.9&nbsp;mi) south of  by road to [[Amaravati (state capital)]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://gudivada.cdma.ap.gov.in/|title=Gudivada Municipality|access-date=5 November 2016|archive-date=19 August 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180819023912/http://gudivada.cdma.ap.gov.in/|url-status=dead}}</ref> It lies on the [[Eastern coastal plains]]. The city is located in zone 3 as per [[Earthquake zones of India]].

== Climate ==
Gudivada falls in the hot, humid region of the country, and it is less than 35 miles from [[Bay of Bengal]]. The climate of the town is very hot in summer and is pleasant during the winter. The hottest day falls in the month of May with shift to June during some years.

== Demographics ==
{{As of|2011}} [[census of India]], Gudivada had a population of 118,167 with 30,834 households. The total population constitute 59,062 males, 59,105 females and 10,509 in the age group of 0–6 years. It had an average literacy rate of 81.64% with 87,887 literates, significantly higher than the district average of 73.7% and the state average of 67.41%.<ref name=census /><ref>{{cite web

== Civic administration ==
[[Gudivada municipality]] was constituted in the year 1937 as a "third grade municipality".<ref name="info">{{cite web|title=About Us {{!}} Gudivada Municipality|url=http://gudivada.cdma.ap.gov.in/en/about-us-3|website=gudivada.cdma.ap.gov.in|access-date=5 November 2016|archive-date=5 November 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161105160845/http://gudivada.cdma.ap.gov.in/en/about-us-3|url-status=dead}}</ref> It was upgraded to "special grade municipality" and is spread over an area of {{convert|12.67|km2|mi2|abbr=on}} with 36 election wards.<ref name=civicbody /> [[YSR Congress Party]] won the Gudivada municipal elections in 2014 and the present [[Member of the Legislative Assembly (India)|MLA]] is [[Venigandla Ramu]] whereas the present chairman of the municipality is TBA. The present municipal commissioner is TBA.

== Economy ==

Agri based economy is the traditional base, consisting of Rice Mills, Tractor Trailer building and other supporting industries. Gudivada is a centre for service industries for the many villages around. The service industries range from Medical, Entertainment and General stores. For the last several decades, Gudivada has transformed into a good educational hub for primary, secondary and higher schools.

== Transport ==
[[File:17050 SC-MTM Machilipatnam Express at GDV.jpg|thumb|Old photo of Gudivada Junction Railway station sign board and Machilipatnam Express halted at Gudivada Junction Railway station]]

=== Public transport ===
The primary modes of intra-city public transport are [[auto rickshaw]]s and [[taxi]]s. Apart from these, other means of transport are [[car]]s, [[motorcycle]]s, [[cycle rickshaw]]s, and [[bicycle]]s. The [[Gudivada bus station]] and [[Gudivada Junction railway station]] are the major transport infrastructure for road and rail transport.<ref>{{cite web|title=Bus Stations in Districts |url=http://www.apsrtc.gov.in/Bus%20Stations.aspx |website=Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation |access-date=15 March 2016 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160322233330/http://apsrtc.gov.in/Bus%20Stations.aspx |archive-date=22 March 2016 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/cities/Vijayawada/apsrtc-strike-security-forces-deployed-at-bus-stations/article7175963.ece|title=Security Forced deployed at Bus Stations in AP|website=[[The Hindu]]}}</ref>

=== Road ===
The city has a total road length of {{Convert|164.00|km|mi|abbr=on}}.<ref>{{cite web |title=Details of Roads in Each ULB of Andhra Pradesh |url=http://centralapp.cdma.ap.gov.in:8080/CDMAAPTaxesInfo/RoadDetails.jsp |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160801101300/http://centralapp.cdma.ap.gov.in:8080/CDMAAPTaxesInfo/RoadDetails.jsp |archive-date=1 August 2016 |access-date=28 March 2017 |website=Commissioner and Directorate of Municipal Administration |publisher=Municipal Administration and Urban Development Department - Government of Andhra Pradesh}}</ref> The [[National Highway (India)|national]] and [[state highways]] that pass through the city include, [[National Highway 165 (India)|NH 165]]–connecting [[Kathipudi]] and [[Pamarru, Krishna district|Pamarru]];.<ref>{{cite web |title=Rationalisation of Numbering Systems of National Highways |url=http://dorth.gov.in/writereaddata/sublinkimages/finaldoc6143316640.pdf |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160201124738/http://dorth.gov.in/writereaddata/sublinkimages/finaldoc6143316640.pdf |archive-date=1 February 2016 |access-date=3 April 2012 |publisher=[[Department of Road Transport and Highways]] |location=New Delhi}}</ref>

=== Rail ===
[[Gudivada Junction railway station]] is the major railway junction in [[Krishna district]] and one of the fourteen ''B–category'' stations in the [[Vijayawada railway division]] of [[South Coast Railway zone]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=About District {{!}} Krishna District, Government of Andhra Pradesh {{!}} India |url=https://krishna.ap.gov.in/new_about-district/ |access-date=2022-06-26 |language=en-US}}</ref>

=== Air ===
Gudivada which falls under [[Andhra Pradesh Capital Region]] is served by [[Vijayawada Airport|NTR Amaravati International airport]] at [[Gannavaram]], which is just 37&nbsp;km away.<ref>{{Cite web |title=NTR Amaravati International Airport {{!}} AAI |url=https://www.aai.aero/en/airports/vijayawada}}</ref>

== Education ==
The primary and secondary school education is imparted by government, aided and private schools, under the ''School Education Department'' of the state.<ref>{{cite web|title=School Education Department |url=http://rmsaap.nic.in/Notification_TSG_2015.pdf |publisher=School Education Department, Government of Andhra Pradesh |access-date=7 November 2016 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151227185018/http://rmsaap.nic.in/Notification_TSG_2015.pdf |archive-date=27 December 2015 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=The Department of School Education - Official AP State Government Portal {{!}} AP State Portal |url=http://www.ap.gov.in/department/organizations/school-education/ |website=www.ap.gov.in |access-date=7 November 2016 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161107155331/http://www.ap.gov.in/department/organizations/school-education/ |archive-date= 7 November 2016 }}</ref> The medium of instruction followed by different schools are English, Telugu.

The private aided colleges are, ANR college and KT Reddy women's college. There are eleven other unaided private colleges for [[undergraduate education|undergraduate]] and [[postgraduate education]].<ref name="colleges">{{cite web|title=List of colleges in Krishna district|url=http://bieap.gov.in/pdf/krishna.pdf|website=Board of Intermediate Education|access-date=27 September 2016}}</ref> One-fourth of the passed out school children will enroll for college education.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kqpBg02R-50C&q=gudivada+education&pg=PA71|title=Gudivada Education|year=1989}}</ref> There are colleges for vocational courses such as, ''Gudivada Vocational Junior College'' and ''St.Johns Vocational Junior College'';<ref>{{cite web|title=List of colleges offering vocational courses in Guntur district|url=http://www.sakshieducation.com/(S(bjoccknv3uzg34nkfz3ecnjk))/colleges/VC/krishna-vocational%20colleges.pdf|website=Sakshi Education|access-date=5 October 2016}}</ref> industrial training centres include, ''Gudivada Industrial Training Centre'', ''Government Industrial Training Institute for Girls''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Government ITI|url=http://sakshieducation.com/Colleges/ITIColleges/KRISHNA%20ITI.pdf|website=Sakshi Education|access-date=5 October 2016}}</ref> It is home for Regional Research Institute for Homeopathy.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ccrhindia.org/CRIGUDIVADA.asp|title=REGIONAL RESEARCH INSTITUTE}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ibtimes.co.in/pilot-project-integration-homoeopathy-yoga-launched-andhra-pradesh-667241|title=Pilot project on 'Integration of Homoeopathy/Yoga' launched in Andhra Pradesh|website=[[International Business Times]]}}</ref>

== Sports ==

[[N. T. Rama Rao|N.T.R]] Stadium in the city was built in partnership with ''Sports Authority of Andhra Pradesh'', the ''Sports Authority of India'' and the ''[[Gudivada Municipality]]''. It is the multi-purpose stadium for several sports like, athletics, [[volleyball]], [[cricket]], [[kho kho]], [[kabaddi]], [[badminton]], [[tennikoit]] and [[basketball]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-national/tp-andhrapradesh/ntr-stadium-all-set-to-become-more-sporty/article1851121.ece|title=NTR Stadium all set to become more sporty|work=The Hindu|access-date=11 April 2016}}</ref> In January 2016, the stadium hosted the ''National Rural Sports and Games'' competition, under the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Abhiyan.<ref>{{cite news|title=National Rural Sports and Games off to a colourful start|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/andhra-pradesh/national-rural-sports-and-games-off-to-a-colourful-start/article8087170.ece|access-date=18 January 2016|work=The Hindu|date=10 January 2016|location=Gudivada|language=en-IN}}</ref>

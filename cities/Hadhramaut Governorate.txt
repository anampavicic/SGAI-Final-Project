'''Hadhramaut Governorate''' {{efn|also spelled '''Hadramawt''' or '''Hadramout'''}}  ({{langx|ar|حضرموت}} ''Ḥaḍramawt'') is a [[governorates of Yemen|governorate]] of [[Yemen]]. Lying within the large historical region of [[Hadhramout Region|Hadhramaut]], it is the country's largest governorate. The capital of Hadhramaut is the city of [[Mukalla]]. Other cities in Hadhramaut include the historical towns of [[Shibam]], [[Sena, Yemen|Sena]], [[Seiyun]], [[Tarim, Yemen|Tarim]], and [[Ash Shihr]].

The [[Socotra]] Archipelago was transferred from the [[Aden Governorate]] to the Hadhramaut Governorate in 2004.<ref>{{cite news |date=2014-02-10 |title=Yemen to become federation of six regions |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-26125721 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140210183408/http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-26125721 |archive-date=10 February 2014 |access-date=11 February 2014 |work=BBC News}}</ref> It was subsequently separated to create [[Socotra Governorate]] in December 2013.<ref>{{cite news |date=2014-02-10 |title=Yemen to become federation of six regions |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-26125721 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140210183408/http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-26125721 |archive-date=10 February 2014 |access-date=11 February 2014 |work=BBC News}}</ref>

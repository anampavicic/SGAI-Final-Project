'''Kankan Region''' ({{lang-nqo|ߞߊ߲ߞߊ߲߫ ߕߌ߲߬ߞߎߘߎ߲}}) is a [[Regions of Guinea|region]] of [[Guinea]] located in the east of the country. It is the largest region of Guinea by area. The region has five (5) [[Prefectures of Guinea|prefectures]] (Kankan, Kérouané, Kouroussa, Mandiana and Siguiri).

It is bordered by the countries of [[Mali]] and [[Côte d'Ivoire]] and the Guinean regions of [[Nzérékoré Region|Nzérékoré]] and [[Faranah Region|Faranah]].

'''Novy Urengoy''' ({{langx|ru|Но́вый Уренго́й}}, lit: “New Urengoy”) is a [[types of inhabited localities in Russia|city]] in [[Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug]], [[Russia]]. Population: {{ru-census|p2010=104,107|p2002=94,456|p1989=93,235|p2021=107,251}} It is the largest city in the [[autonomous okrug]].

It was founded in 1975 after the discovery of the [[Urengoy gas field]], one of the largest in Russia.{{citation needed|date=June 2010}} Town status was granted to it in 1980.{{citation needed|date=June 2010}}

==Administrative and municipal status==
Within the [[subdivisions of Russia#Administrative divisions|framework of administrative divisions]], it is incorporated as the '''[[city of federal subject significance|city of okrug significance]] of Novy Urengoy'''—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the [[administrative divisions of Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug|districts]].<ref name="Ref928">Law #42-ZAO</ref> As a [[subdivisions of Russia#Municipal divisions|municipal division]], the city of okrug significance of Novy Urengoy is incorporated as '''Novy Urengoy Urban Okrug'''.<ref name="Ref931">Law #102-ZAO</ref>

Novy Urengoy has a [[subarctic climate]] ([[Köppen climate classification]] ''Dfc''). Winters are severely cold and long with average temperatures from {{convert|-30|C}} to {{convert|-22|C}} in January, while summers are mild and brief with average temperatures from {{convert|+10|C}} to {{convert|+18|C}} in July. [[Precipitation]] is moderate, and is somewhat higher in summer than at other times of the year.

==Economy==
A major industry of the city is oil and gas production, with one of the largest [[Urengoy gas field|gas fields]] in the world in the area and substantial prospects for further exploration. The [[Government owned corporation|government-owned]] company [[Gazprom]] is the main local employer.

===Transportation===
The city is situated on the [[Tyumen]]–Novy Urengoy railway line. Traveling north from Tyumen, Novy Urengoy is the last station of significance. [[Noyabrsk]] is the previous station of significance.

Novy Urengoy is along the [[Salekhard–Igarka Railway]], "Dead Road". Extending from Novy Urengoy to [[Nadym|Stary Nadym]] is one section of the "Railway of Death" that serves as an important freight railway.

The city is served by the [[Novy Urengoy Airport]].

'''East Darfur State''' ({{Langx|ar|ولاية شرق دارفور}} Wilāyat Šarq Dārfūr; Sharq Darfur) is one of the [[States of Sudan|states]] of [[Sudan]], and one of five comprising the [[Darfur]] region. It was created in January 2012 as a result of the ongoing peace process for the wider Darfur region.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sudantribune.com/Bashir-establishes-two-states-in,41256 |title=Bashir establishes two states in Darfur, reshuffles governors - Sudan Tribune: Plural news and views on Sudan |access-date=March 7, 2012 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120229084948/http://www.sudantribune.com/Bashir-establishes-two-states-in,41256 |archive-date=February 29, 2012 }}</ref> The state capital is [[Ed Daein]]. The state was formed from land that was part of the state of [[South Darfur]].

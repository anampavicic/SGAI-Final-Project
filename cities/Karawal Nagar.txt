{{As of|2011}} India [[census]],<ref name=census2011>{{cite web|url=http://www.census2011.co.in/data/town/63982-mandoli-delhi.html|title=Census 2011, Mandoli town Data}}</ref> Karawal Nagar had a population of 224,281. Karawal Nagar, was also in centre of the [[2020 Delhi riots]].
. 

Karawal Nagar is well connected to other parts of Delhi through Delhi Metro, DTC buses, Cabs, shared and hired auto rickshaws, and Gramin Sewa.
The nearest metro station to Karawal Nagar is Johri Enclave Metro and Shiv Vihar Metro Station. The distance between Karawal Nagar and Johri Enclave metro station is just 1.8&nbsp;km.
Majority of people living here use Delhi Metro, shared auto and DTC buses.

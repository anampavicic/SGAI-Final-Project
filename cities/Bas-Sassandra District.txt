'''Bas-Sassandra District''' ({{langx|fr|District du Bas-Sassandra}}; {{IPA|fr|ba.sa.sɑ̃d.ʁa|}}, "Lower [[Sassandra River|Sassandra]]") is one of fourteen [[Districts of Ivory Coast|administrative districts]] of [[Ivory Coast]]. The district is located in the southwest part of the country. The capital of the district is [[San Pédro, Ivory Coast|San-Pédro]].

==Creation==
The district was created in a 2011 administrative reorganisation of the [[subdivisions of Ivory Coast]].<ref>Décret n° 2011-263 du 28 septembre 2011 portant organisation du territoire national en Districts et en Régions.</ref> Its territory is composed of the former region of [[Bas-Sassandra Region|Bas-Sassandra]] with the addition of the department of [[Fresco Department|Fresco]], which was transferred from the region of [[Sud-Bandama]].<ref>{{Cite web|author=Law, Gwillim|title=Districts of Côte d'Ivoire (Ivory Coast)|work=Administrative Divisions of Countries ("Statoids")|url=http://www.statoids.com/uci.html}}</ref>

==Administrative divisions==
Bas-Sassandra District is currently subdivided into three [[Regions of Ivory Coast|regions]] and the following [[departments of Ivory Coast|departments]]: 
* '''[[Gbôklé Region]]''' (region seat in [[Sassandra]])
** [[Fresco Department]]
** [[Sassandra Department]]
* '''[[Nawa Region]]''' (region seat in [[Soubré]])
** [[Buyo Department]]
** [[Guéyo Department]]
** [[Méagui Department]]
** [[Soubré Department]]
* '''[[San-Pédro Region]]''' (region seat also in [[San Pédro, Ivory Coast|San-Pédro]]) 
** [[San-Pédro Department]]
** [[Tabou Department]]

==Population==
According to the 2021 census, Bas-Sassandra District has a population of 2,687,176.<ref name="pop"/>

'''Banjarbaru''' is the capital city of [[South Kalimantan]], one of the provinces in [[Indonesia]]. It is located {{cvt|35|km|mi}} southeast of [[Banjarmasin]], the largest city of the province. The city had a population of 199,627 as of the 2010 Census,<ref name="Biro Pusat Statistik 2011">Biro Pusat Statistik, Jakarta, 2011.</ref> and 253,442 at the 2020 Census,<ref name="Badan Pusat Statistik 2021">Badan Pusat Statistik, Jakarta, 2021.</ref> and the official population estimate (as at mid 2023) was 272,763 (comprising 136,867 males and 135,896 females).<ref name="Badan Pusat Statistik 2024">Badan Pusat Statistik, Jakarta, 28 February 2024, ''Kota Banjarbaru Dalam Angka 2024'' (Katalog-BPS 1102001.6372)</ref> The large town of [[Martapura, South Kalimantan|Martapura]] lies immediately to the north of Banjarbaru, and in effect constitutes an extension of the city. The second largest city in the province after [[Banjarmasin]], it is also part of [[List of metropolitan areas in Indonesia|Banjarbakula metropolitan area]].<ref name=":9">{{Cite web |title=PU-net |url=http://perkotaan.bpiw.pu.go.id/n/metropolitan/9 |access-date=2021-04-09 |website=perkotaan.bpiw.pu.go.id |archive-date=10 May 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210510142514/http://perkotaan.bpiw.pu.go.id/n/metropolitan/9 |url-status=live }}</ref>

== History ==

=== Etymology ===
Banjarbaru was previously only a temporary name used by governor Dr. Murdjani to differentiate it from Banjarmasin, as "baru" means "new" in [[Indonesian language|Indonesian]]. However, the name stuck between people living there and gradually became official name of the city.<ref name=":2" /> Banjarbaru previously consisted of a series of hills known as Mount Apam.<ref name=":2" />

=== Early history ===
[[File:COLLECTIE TROPENMUSEUM Diamantwinning bij Tjempaka bij Martapoera Zuidoost-Borneo TMnr 10007447.jpg|left|thumb|Diamond mining in Cempaka during colonial era]]
Diamond mines in Cempaka have existed since the 15th century under the Hindu-Buddhist kingdom [[:id:Kerajaan Negara Dipa|Negara Dipa]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=KARAKTERISTIK KAWASAN PERMUKIMAN PENAMBANG INTAN CEMPAKA |url=https://e-journal.upr.ac.id/index.php/JT/article/download/1332/1116/2493 |url-status=live |format=pdf |access-date=16 April 2021 |archive-date=16 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210416002402/https://e-journal.upr.ac.id/index.php/JT/article/download/1332/1116/2493 }}</ref> In the era of the [[Sultanate of Banjar|Banjar Sultanate]], there was a royal edict stating that diamonds of four [[Carat (mass)|carats]] or higher should be sold only to the sultan.<ref>{{Cite web |title=EKSPLOITASI SOMBER DAYA ALAM VERSUS PERLINDUNGAN SUMBER DAYA BUDAYA Dl PEGUNUNGAN MERATUS |url=https://naditirawidya.kemdikbud.go.id/index.php/nw/article/download/367/304 |url-status=live |format=pdf |access-date=16 April 2021 |archive-date=16 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210416002403/https://naditirawidya.kemdikbud.go.id/index.php/nw/article/download/367/304 }}</ref> The place was mostly uninhabited except for resting places of diamond mine laborers from Cempaka, now also part of the city.<ref name=":3">{{Cite web |last=Khairally |first=Elmy Tasya |title=Sudah Pernah ke Banjarbaru? Ini 5 Objek Wisata Menariknya |url=https://travel.detik.com/domestic-destination/d-5255422/sudah-pernah-ke-banjarbaru-ini-5-objek-wisata-menariknya |access-date=2021-04-09 |website=detikTravel |language=id |archive-date=10 May 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210510023310/https://travel.detik.com/domestic-destination/d-5255422/sudah-pernah-ke-banjarbaru-ini-5-objek-wisata-menariknya |url-status=live }}</ref> Cempaka diamond mines under the [[Dutch East Indies|colonial era]] were regulated under ''Ordonantie 25 Nopember 1923 Staatblast 1923'' No. 174 together with Pelaihari and [[Martapura, South Kalimantan|Martapura]].<ref name=":5">{{Cite web |title=ANALISIS SOSIOLOGI EKONOMI PADA TAMBANG RAKYAT |url=http://jurnal.unpad.ac.id/sosioglobal/article/download/19550/pdf |url-status=live |format=pdf |access-date=15 April 2021 |archive-date=28 July 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200728080640/http://jurnal.unpad.ac.id/sosioglobal/article/download/19550/pdf }}</ref>

On 20 April 1999, Banjarbaru was separated from [[Banjar Regency]] (of which it had previously been a part) and gained its status as an independent city, although it was still recognized as part of the [[List of metropolitan areas in Indonesia|Banjarbakula metropolitan area]].<ref name=":9" /><ref>{{Cite web |title=UU No. 9 Tahun 1999 tentang Pembentukan Kotamadya Daerah Tingkat II Banjarbaru [JDIH BPK RI] |url=https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/Home/Details/45289/uu-no-9-tahun-1999 |access-date=2021-04-20 |website=peraturan.bpk.go.id |archive-date=20 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210420204904/https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/Home/Details/45289/uu-no-9-tahun-1999 |url-status=live }}</ref> The city legally became capital of the South Kalimantan province on 15 February 2022 following updates of laws concerning legal basis of several provinces were passed by [[People's Representative Council]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Agency|first=ANTARA News|title=Banjarbaru resmi ibukota Provinsi Kalimantan Selatan|url=https://kalsel.antaranews.com/berita/312989/banjarbaru-resmi-ibukota-provinsi-kalimantan-selatan|access-date=2022-02-19|website=ANTARA News Kalimantan Selatan}}</ref>

==Geography==
Banjarbaru is located at the foot of the [[Meratus Mountains]], with 80% of the city area elevated between 0 and {{cvt|25|m|ft}} above sea level, but it also includes areas up to {{cvt|500|m|ft}} high. In the North, East, and West it borders [[Banjar Regency]], while in the South it borders [[Tanah Laut Regency]]. [[Podzol]] dominates the city soil, with a concentration of as much as 63.82%. However, Landasan Ulin's soil is instead dominated by [[peat]] and [[alluvium]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Profil Kota Banjarbaru 2014 |url=https://sippa.ciptakarya.pu.go.id/sippa_online/ws_file/dokumen/rpi2jm/DOCRPIJM_d4e17563b3_BAB%20IVBab%204%20bjb.pdf |url-status=live |access-date=15 April 2021 |archive-date=15 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210415235440/https://sippa.ciptakarya.pu.go.id/sippa_online/ws_file/dokumen/rpi2jm/DOCRPIJM_d4e17563b3_BAB%20IVBab%204%20bjb.pdf }}</ref>

Compared to other regencies and cities in the province, Banjarbaru is also the second smallest second-level administrative division in the province after Banjarmasin city, covering around 0.88% of province territory.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Geografis – Humas Kota Banjarbaru |url=https://humas.banjarbarukota.go.id/tentang-banjarbaru/geografis/ |access-date=2021-04-09 |language=en-US |archive-date=8 May 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210508131045/https://humas.banjarbarukota.go.id/tentang-banjarbaru/geografis/ |url-status=live }}</ref>

===Climate===
Banjarbaru has a [[tropical savanna climate]] (''Aw'' according to the [[Köppen climate classification]]) with abundant rainfall in all months, although June to October is comparatively drier. Banjarbaru receives {{convert|2627|mm}} of rain annually. Temperatures are uniform in all months due to its equatorial location, with the average annual temperature being {{convert|27.2|C|F}}. On 16 August 1997, Banjarbaru recorded a temperature of {{convert|40.6|C|F}}, which is the highest temperature that has ever been recorded in Indonesia.<ref name="CEWS - BMKG">{{Cite web|url=https://cews.bmkg.go.id/Informasi_Iklim_Ekstrim/Historis_Kejadian_Iklim_Ekstrim.bmkg|title=Historis Kejadian Iklim Ekstrim|publisher=[[Meteorology, Climatology, and Geophysical Agency|Meteorology, Climatology, and Geophysical Agency of Indonesia (BMKG)]]|date=2011|language=id|access-date=2 May 2021|archive-date=2 May 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210502044557/https://cews.bmkg.go.id/Informasi_Iklim_Ekstrim/Historis_Kejadian_Iklim_Ekstrim.bmkg|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref name="Sains Kompas">{{cite news|last=Putri|first=Gloria Setyvani|url=https://sains.kompas.com/read/2019/10/24/190300723/cuaca-panas-masih-9-hari-ini-daftar-hari-terpanas-di-indonesia|title=Cuaca Panas Masih 9 Hari, Ini Daftar Hari Terpanas di Indonesia|publisher=[[Kompas]]|date=24 October 2019|language=id|access-date=2 May 2021}}</ref>

== Demographics ==
Around 94% of the city population are [[Muslims|Muslim]], 4.6% are [[Christianity|Christian]], 0.21% [[Balinese Hinduism|Hindu]], and 0.16% [[Buddhist]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |title=BPS Kota Banjarbaru |url=https://banjarbarukota.bps.go.id/publication/2020/04/27/88cb462db9a1a248de838d38/kota-banjarbaru-dalam-angka-2020.html |access-date=2021-04-09 |website=banjarbarukota.bps.go.id |archive-date=19 January 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220119042201/https://banjarbarukota.bps.go.id/publication/2020/04/27/88cb462db9a1a248de838d38/kota-banjarbaru-dalam-angka-2020.html |url-status=live }}</ref> There were 136,867 males and 135,896 females in the city as of mid 2023, with a [[sex ratio]] of 100 females to 100.7 males.<ref name="Badan Pusat Statistik 2024">Badan Pusat Statistik, Jakarta, 28 February 2024, ''Kota Banjarbaru Dalam Angka 2024'' (Katalog-BPS 1102001.6372)</ref> South Banjarbaru is the most densely populated district with 3,211 people per square kilometre in mid 2023 and Cempaka is the least densely populated district with 324 people per square kilometre.<ref name=":8">{{Cite web |title=BPS Kota Banjarbaru |url=https://banjarbarukota.bps.go.id/publication/2020/12/25/ee56c8df86b52e9a281cb153/statistik-daerah-kota-banjarbaru-2020.html |access-date=2021-04-18 |website=banjarbarukota.bps.go.id |archive-date=18 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210418223123/https://banjarbarukota.bps.go.id/publication/2020/12/25/ee56c8df86b52e9a281cb153/statistik-daerah-kota-banjarbaru-2020.html |url-status=live }}</ref>

The city population is dominated by young adults with an age range of 20 to 39 years old. The population within reproductive age (15 – 64 years old) make up to 70% of city's population as of 2019.<ref name=":8" /> Life expectancy of the city is 71.87 years as of 2019, which is slightly above the national figure and significantly higher than the provincial figure.<ref name=":8" />

=== Local government ===
[[File:Banjarbaru city hall.jpg|left|thumb|Banjarbaru city hall, across Dr. Murdjani field]]
As with all of [[City status in Indonesia|Indonesian cities]], the local government is a second-level administrative division run by a mayor and vice mayor together with the city parliament, and it is equivalent to regency.<ref>{{Cite web |title=UU 22 1999 |url=https://www.dpr.go.id/dokjdih/document/uu/UU_1999_22.pdf |url-status=live |access-date=16 April 2021 |archive-date=16 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210416012225/https://www.dpr.go.id/dokjdih/document/uu/UU_1999_22.pdf }}</ref> Executive power lies in the mayor and vice mayor, while legislation duties are carried by local parliament. Mayor, vice mayor, and parliament members are democratically elected by people of the city in an election.<ref>{{Cite web |title=UU 8 2015 |url=https://www.dpr.go.id/dokjdih/document/uu/1627.pdf |url-status=live |access-date=16 April 2021 |archive-date=26 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210226055828/https://www.dpr.go.id/dokjdih/document/uu/1627.pdf }}</ref> Meanwhile, head of districts are appointed directly by city mayor with recommendation by the city secretary.<ref>{{Cite web |title=PP No. 17 Tahun 2018 tentang Kecamatan [JDIH BPK RI] |url=https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/Home/Details/77921/pp-no-17-tahun-2018 |access-date=2021-04-16 |website=peraturan.bpk.go.id |archive-date=17 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210417073426/https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/Home/Details/77921/pp-no-17-tahun-2018 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>Government Law No.19 1998</ref>

== Education ==
In Banjarbaru, there are 164 kindergartens, 83 elementary schools, 37 junior high schools, 23 senior high schools, and 16 vocational high schools (SMK) both public and private.<ref name=":0" /> In addition, there are 14 higher education institutions; the most notable being [[Lambung Mangkurat University]].<ref name=":0" /> Lambung Mangkurat University is also the only public university in the city, while the rest of higher education institutions are private.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2020-08-05 |title=√ Universitas Terbaik di Kalimantan Selatan 2020/2021 |url=https://kampusimpian.com/universitas-terbaik-di-kalimantan-selatan/ |access-date=2021-04-16 |website=Kampusimpian.com – Platform Pendidikan Indonesia |language=id-ID |archive-date=16 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210416081233/https://kampusimpian.com/universitas-terbaik-di-kalimantan-selatan/ |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=6+ Kampus Terbaik di Kota Banjarbaru |url=https://www.faktakampus.com/2020/04/kampus-di-banjarbaru.html |access-date=2021-04-16 |website=Fakta Kampus |archive-date=16 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210416081232/https://www.faktakampus.com/2020/04/kampus-di-banjarbaru.html |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Universitas Lambung Mangkurat |url=https://campus.quipper.com/directory/universitas-lambung-mangkurat |url-status=live |access-date=2021-04-16 |website=campus.quipper.com |archive-date=16 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210416081233/https://campus.quipper.com/directory/universitas-lambung-mangkurat }}</ref>

== Media ==
According to the [[Press Council (Indonesia)|Press Council]], there are four media companies registered in Banjarbaru, consisting of two cyber media, one printed, and one television. They are KanalKalimantan, Teras7, [[List of newspapers in Indonesia|Radar Banjarmasin]], and Amaco Media.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Dewan Pers |url=https://dewanpers.or.id/data/perusahaanpers |access-date=2021-04-20 |website=dewanpers.or.id |archive-date=19 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210419131851/https://dewanpers.or.id/data/perusahaanpers |url-status=live }}</ref> However, due to lack of registration & verification, the numbers could be higher according to the Indonesian Cyber Media Union.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Studio |first=Aldiskatel |date=2019-08-03 |title=Masih Banyak Media Online di Kalsel Belum Terverifikasi |url=https://www.kanalkalimantan.com/masih-banyak-media-online-di-kalsel-belum-terverifikasi/ |access-date=2021-04-20 |website=Kanal Kalimantan |language=id-ID |archive-date=20 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210420212049/https://www.kanalkalimantan.com/masih-banyak-media-online-di-kalsel-belum-terverifikasi/ |url-status=live }}</ref> There are two known radio stations, Nirwana FM Banjarbaru and Abdi Persada FM. Nirwana FM Banjarbaru is licensed by the [[Ministry of Communication and Information Technology (Indonesia)|Ministry of Communication and Information Technology]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Daftar Izin Radio Yang Diterima 2017 |url=https://e-penyiaran.kominfo.go.id/uploads/informasi/08ceeb262a53bc0caf9a64b2171a2f5b.pdf |url-status=live |access-date=20 April 2021 |archive-date=20 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210420212709/https://e-penyiaran.kominfo.go.id/uploads/informasi/08ceeb262a53bc0caf9a64b2171a2f5b.pdf }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=ANALISIS PRODUK DAN PROMOSI DALAM MENINGKATKAN MINAT PENDENGAR PADA RADIO ABDI PERSADA 104,7 FM BANJARBARU |url=http://repository.uniska-bjm.ac.id/659/2/15310157.pdf |url-status=live |access-date=20 April 2021 |archive-date=20 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210420214153/http://repository.uniska-bjm.ac.id/659/2/15310157.pdf }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Hairiyadi |date=2021-04-15 |title=Banjarbaru Juara Dialog Interaktif Program Kerja |url=https://koranbanjar.net/banjarbaru-juara-dialog-interaktif-program-kerja/ |access-date=2021-04-20 |website=KoranBanjar.Net |language=id-ID |archive-date=19 January 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220119042124/https://koranbanjar.net/cgi-sys/suspendedpage.cgi |url-status=live }}</ref>

'''Durrës County''' ({{lang-sq|Qarku i Durrësit}}), officially the '''County of Durrës''' ({{lang-sq|Qarku i Durrësit|italic=unset}}), is a [[Counties of Albania|county]] in the [[Northern Albania|Northern Region]] of the [[Republic of Albania]]. It is the smallest by area and the [[List of counties of Albania by population|second most populous]] of the twelve counties, with 292,029 people within an area of {{convert|766|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}}.<ref name=pop21/> The county borders on the [[Adriatic Sea]] to the west, the counties of [[Lezhë County|Lezhë]] to the north, [[Dibër County|Dibër]] to the east and [[Tirana County|Tirana]] to the south. It is divided into three [[Municipalities of Albania|municipalities]], [[Durrës]], [[Krujë]] and [[Shijak]], with all of whom incorporate sixteen [[Villages of Durrës County|administrative units]].

The county has geographically a coastline on the [[Albanian Adriatic Sea Coast]] and extends in the [[Western Lowlands]] on a flat [[alluvial plain|alluvial]] and [[coastal plain]]. The east is characterised by the [[Skanderbeg Mountains]] which splits the Western Lowlands from the [[Central Mountain Range (Albania)|Central Mountain Range]]. Its [[climate]] is profoundly determined by its proximity to the [[Mediterranean Sea]] and its considerable change in terrain.

The central city [[Durrës]] is one of largest cities on the Adriatic Sea and ranks 5th with a population of 175,110 and one of the world's [[List of cities by time of continuous habitation|oldest continuously inhabited cities]]. Durrës is home to Albania's main port, the [[Port of Durrës]]. The [[Port of Durrës|port]] is also the 10th largest cargo port on the [[Adriatic Sea]] that handle more than 3.4 million tonnes of cargo per year. The historical city of [[Krujë]] is the second largest city in Durrës County.

== Geography ==

Durrës County is defined within an area of {{convert|766|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}} and is located in the [[Northern Albania|Northern Region]] of the [[Republic of Albania]] bordering the [[Adriatic Sea]] to the west, the counties of [[Lezhë County|Lezhë]] to the north, [[Dibër County|Dibër]] to the east and [[Tirana County|Tirana]] to the south.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Institute of Statistics (Albania)|Instituti i Statistikës]] (INSTAT) |title=A new Urban–Rural Classification of Albanian Population |url=https://www.instat.gov.al/media/2919/a_new_urban-rural_classification_of_albanian_population.pdf |access-date=28 September 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191114101641/https://www.instat.gov.al/media/2919/a_new_urban-rural_classification_of_albanian_population.pdf |archive-date=14 November 2019 |page=15 |date=May 2014}}</ref> The county lies between [[latitude]]s [[41st parallel north|41°]] and [[23rd parallel north|23° N]] and [[longitude]]s [[19th meridian east|19°]] and [[30th meridian east|30° E]].

Because of its proximity to the [[Mediterranean Sea]], several rivers, large and small, flow throughout the county.<ref name="Geography">{{cite web |last=Kiri |first=Lindita |publisher=[[University of Tirana]] |title=Evolucioni dhe pasojat gjeografike të proceseve gjeopopullative në qarkun e Durrësit pas vitit 1990 |url=http://www.doktoratura.unitir.edu.al/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/Lindita_Kiri.pdf |access-date=16 October 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201016133635/http://www.doktoratura.unitir.edu.al/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/Lindita_Kiri.pdf |archive-date=16 October 2020 |location=Tirana |pages=17–59 |language=sq}}</ref> The [[Ishëm (river)|Ishëm]] is the longest river in the county originating at the [[Skanderbeg Mountains]] in [[Tirana County]] and flowing through the county until it reaches the Adriatic Sea at the [[Cape of Rodon]] along the [[Gulf of Drin]].<ref name="Geography"/> The [[Erzen]], the second longest river in the county, begins nearby the village of [[Shëngjergj]], flows into northwest and empties into the sea at the [[Gjiri i Lalzit]].

The city of Krujë is found at an altitude of 600&nbsp;m (1,969&nbsp;ft) on the foot of Mount Krujë ({{lang-sq|Mali i Krujës}}) of the Krujë Gorge, while south and west of the town is found the plain of the [[Ishëm River]]. The town is located in the northern part of the outer Albanides tectonic unit, which consists of anticlines of Mesozoic [[carbonate platforms]].

=== Climate ===

The climate is generally Mediterranean but varies by local topography. There are diverse microclimates in the county. Summers are dry while heavy rains are experienced during the winter.

== Demography ==

{{Historical populations|9=1989|10=218530|11=2001|12=245179|13=2011|14=262785|15=2023|16=226863|align=right|source=<ref>{{cite web |title=Census of Population and Housing|url=https://www.instat.gov.al/en/themes/censuses/census-of-population-and-housing/|publisher=Institute of Statistics Albania}}</ref>}}Durrës County is the [[List of counties of Albania by population|second most populous]] and [[List of counties of Albania by population density|densely populated]] county of the [[Republic of Albania]], with an increasing number of population.<ref name="Population 2020">{{cite web |publisher=[[Institute of Statistics (Albania)|Instituti i Statistikës]] (INSTAT) |title=Popullsia e Shqipërisë 2020 |url=http://www.instat.gov.al/media/6849/popullsia_me-_1_janar_2020.pdf |access-date=6 July 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200706140605/http://www.instat.gov.al/media/6849/popullsia_me-_1_janar_2020.pdf |archive-date=6 July 2020 |page=6 |language=sq |date=26 March 2020}}</ref> The [[Institute of Statistics (Albania)|Institute of Statistics]] (INSTAT) estimated the county's population in January 2021 at 292,029.<ref name="pop21" /> At the [[2011 Census of Albania|2011 census]], there were 263,711 people living in the county with a population density of 343 people per square kilometres.<ref name="Census 2011">{{cite web |last=Nurja |first=Ines |publisher=[[Institute of Statistics (Albania)|Institute of Statistics]] (INSTAT) |title=Censusi i popullsisë dhe banesave/ Population and Housing Census–Durrës (2011) |url=http://www.instat.gov.al/media/3061/3__durres.pdf |access-date=14 October 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200327145006/http://www.instat.gov.al/media/3061/3__durres.pdf |archive-date=27 March 2020 |location=Tirana}}</ref>

Ethnically, Durrës County is predominantly Albanian but small minorities of [[Bosniaks in Albania|Bosniaks]] (near [[Shijak]]), [[Aromanians in Albania|Aromanians]] (in [[Fllakë]]), Romani & Balkan Egyptians are present in the ethnic composition as well. By language, 99.5% spoke [[Albanian language|Albanian]] as a first language. Other spoken languages were [[Romani language|Romani]] (0.12%), [[Greek language|Greek]] (0.05%), [[Macedonian language|Macedonian]] (0.05%), [[Italian language|Italian]] (0.02%), [[Turkish language|Turkish]] (0.02%) and [[Aromanian language|Aromanian]] (0.02%).<ref name="Census 2011"/> By religion, there were 177,274 (67.46%) [[Islam|Muslim]]s, 19,322 (7.35%) [[Roman Catholicism|Roman Catholic]]s, 8,675 (3.30%) [[Eastern orthodoxy|Eastern Orthodox]], 4,215 (1.60%) [[Bektashi Order|Bektashi]]s, 325 (0.12%) [[Evangelism|Evangelist]]s, 167 (0.06%) other [[Christianity|Christians]], 9,131 (3.47%) believers without denomination and 41 (0.02%) of other religions.<ref name="Census 2011"/> Nonetheless, 2,901 people (1.10%) were [[Irreligion|irreligious]] while 33,754 (12.84%) did not declare their religion.

=== Settlements ===

There are three [[Municipalities of Albania|municipalities]] (''bashkia'') and 16 [[Villages of Durrës County|administrative units]] (''njësi administrative'') incorporated in Durrës County.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.vendime.al/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/137-2014.pdf |title=Law nr. 115/2014 |language=sq |page=6368|access-date=25 February 2022 }}</ref> The majority of its population is predominantly concentrated in the county's capital city of [[Durrës]]. As of the [[2011 Census of Albania|2011 Census]], the population of the administrative unit Durrës stands at 113,249 with the adjacent eponymous municipality having a population of 175,110.<ref name="Census 2011"/> Other units with a population over 10,000 include [[Fushë-Krujë]], [[Katund i Ri]], [[Kodër-Thumanë]], [[Krujë]], [[Rrashbull]], [[Sukth]] and [[Xhafzotaj]].

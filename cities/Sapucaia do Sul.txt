'''Sapucaia do Sul''' is a municipality in the state of [[Rio Grande do Sul]], [[Brazil]]. It is located in the south of Brazil, in the region of "[[:pt:Vale do Rio dos Sinos|Vale dos Sinos]]".

Sapucaia do Sul is a commercial city, and some industries have been there since the 1960s. The city is crossed by BR-116, one of the most important roads in Brazil.
The coordinates of this place are -29.829, -51.144. As of 2020, the city has a population of 141,808.

One of its main touristic attractions is the Zoo Park.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-12-28|title=Parque Zoológico|url=https://sema.rs.gov.br/parque-zoologico-6107f0bea9d13|access-date=2022-02-02|website=Sema - Secretaria do Meio Ambiente e Infraestrutura|language=pt}}</ref>

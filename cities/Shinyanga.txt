'''Shinyanga''', also known as '''Mji wa Shinyanga''' in the national language, is a city in northern [[Tanzania]]. The city is the location of the regional headquarters of [[Shinyanga Region]] as well as the district headquarters of [[Shinyanga Urban|Shinyanga Urban District]]. The region and district are named after the town.

==Location==
Shinyanga is located in [[Shinyanga District]], in [[Shinyanga Region]], in northern [[Tanzania]]. The city is located approximately {{convert|175|km|mi}}, by road, southeast of [[Mwanza]], the nearest large city.<ref>[http://distancecalculator.globefeed.com/Tanzania_Distance_Result.asp?fromplace=Mwanza%20(Mwanza)&toplace=Shinyanga%20(Shinyanga)&fromlat=-2.5166667&tolat=-3.6666667&fromlng=32.9&tolng=33.4333333 Map showing Mwanza and Shinyanga with distance marker]</ref>  This location lies approximately {{convert|475|km|mi}}, by road, northwest of [[Dodoma]], the capital city of Tanzania.<ref>[http://distancecalculator.globefeed.com/Tanzania_Distance_Result.asp?fromplace=Dodoma%20(Dodoma)&toplace=Shinyanga%20(Shinyanga)&fromlat=-6.1833333&tolat=-3.6666667&fromlng=35.75&tolng=33.4333333 Road distance between Dodoma and Shinyanga with map]</ref> The coordinates of the city are: 3°39′43″S 33°25′23″E / 3.661945°S 33.423056°E / -3.661945; 33.423056.

==Population==
The 2002 national census estimated the population of Shinyanga at about 93,000.  The 2012 national census listed the population of Shinyanga Municipal Council at 161,391. <ref>[http://www.tzdpg.or.tz/fileadmin/documents/dpg_internal/dpg_working_groups_clusters/cluster_2/water/WSDP/Background_information/2012_Census_General_Report.pdf Tanzania 2012 Population and Housing Census]</ref> According to a 21 June 2022 news article in [https://www.afrik21.africa/en/ Afrik21] the population now exceeds 200,000. <ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-06-21 |title=TANZANIA: AFD finances water and sanitation in Shinyanga with €75 million |url=https://www.afrik21.africa/en/tanzania-afd-finances-water-and-sanitation-in-shinyanga-with-e75-million/ |access-date=2022-06-22 |website=Afrik 21 |language=en-US}}</ref>

==Transport== 
===Railway===
Shinyanga is served by the Shinyanga Railway Station as a branch of the [[Central Line (Tanzania)|Central Railway]] of Tanzania.

===Airport===
[[Shinyanga Airport]] is located {{convert|8|km|mi|abbr=in}} along Mwanza road in Ibadakuli Area.

===Road===
Shinyanga is served by the [[Tabora]]-[[Mwanza]] Highway. The highway passes conveniently through the center of Shinyanga.

==Reforestation==
The region has a [[forest restoration]] program which is well documented by the [[Food and Agriculture Organization]], [[United Nations Environment Programme]] and the Global Partnership on Forest Landscape Restoration. The programme is featured in the film ''Forests for the 21st Century''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.fao.org/DOCREP/ARTICLE/WFC/XII/0164-B3.HTM|title = Forest landscape restoration in Shinyanga, the United Republic of Tanzania}}</ref>

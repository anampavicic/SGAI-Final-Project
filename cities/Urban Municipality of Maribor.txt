The '''Urban Municipality of Maribor''' ({{IPA|sl|ˈmaːɾibɔɾ|pron}}), also the '''City of Maribor''' ({{langx|sl|Mestna občina Maribor}}, acronym '''MOM'''), is one of twelve [[urban municipality|urban municipalities]] in [[Slovenia]]. Its seat is [[Maribor]], the second-largest city in Slovenia. The population of the municipality was 113,747 in January 2024.<ref name="SURS"/> It borders [[Austria]].

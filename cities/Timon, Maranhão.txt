'''Timon''' is a Brazilian [[Municipalities of Brazil|municipality]] in the State of [[Maranhão]]. The population is 170,222 (estimation 2020) and the total area is 1765&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>.<ref name="IBGE2" /><ref name="IBGE2Area" />
==Geography==
Timon is located on the [[Parnaíba]] river which forms a natural border with the neighboring city of [[Teresina]], capital of the [[Piauí]] state.

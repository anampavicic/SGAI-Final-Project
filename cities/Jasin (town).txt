'''Jasin''' is a town and the seat of [[Jasin District]] in the [[Malaysia]]n state of [[Melaka]]. It is governed by '''Jasin Municipal Council''' ({{lang-ms|Majlis Perbandaran Jasin}}), which was formerly known as '''Jasin District Council'''  ({{lang-ms|Majlis Daerah Jasin}}) from 1 July 1978 until 1 January 2007 and the '''Jasin Rural District Council''' ({{lang-ms|Majlis Daerah Luar Bandar Jasin}}) from 23 August 1957 until 1 July 1978.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mpjasin.gov.my/ms/jasin/profil/latar-belakang|title=Latar Belakang|access-date=2022-08-22|archive-date=2021-02-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210209003323/http://www.mpjasin.gov.my/ms/jasin/profil/latar-belakang|url-status=dead}}</ref>

'''Taboão da Serra''' is a municipality in the state of [[São Paulo (state)|São Paulo]] in Brazil. It is part of the [[Metropolitan Region of São Paulo]].<ref>[http://governo-sp.jusbrasil.com.br/legislacao/1028148/lei-complementar-1139-11 Lei Complementar nº 1.139, de 16 de junho de 2011]</ref> The population is 293,652 (2020 est.) in an area of 20.39&nbsp;km².<ref name=ibge>[http://cod.ibge.gov.br/232U5 Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatística]</ref> It is the second smallest city in area in the São Paulo Metropolitan Area (RMSP).

Without significant geographical boundaries with the capital of São Paulo, Taboão da Serra is indistinguishable from the neighborhoods of São Paulo that it borders, such as [[Butantã (district of São Paulo)|Butantã]] and [[Campo Limpo (district of São Paulo)|Campo Limpo]]. In other words, it is a city [[Conurbation|conurbated]] with the capital of São Paulo, a phenomenon that has become increasingly common in recent decades in the cities of the [[Metropolitan Region of São Paulo]]. For many years, the city was divided between the profile of a [[Commuter town|dormitory town]] and an industrial location.<ref>{{Cite web |title=EncontraTaboão.com {{!}} Guia de Taboão da Serra » EncontraTaboao.com |url=https://www.encontrataboao.com/ |access-date=2024-10-16 |website=EncontraTaboão.com |language=pt-BR}}</ref>

== Geography ==

=== Climate ===
The city's climate, as in the whole [[Greater São Paulo]], has a monsoon-influenced [[humid subtropical climate]] (''Cfa''), according to the [[Köppen climate classification|Köppen classification]]. Summers are rainy and not too warm. The winters are rainless and not too cold.
The year average temperature is 63.5 (17.5 Cº), and the coldest month is July (Average 55.4 [13&nbsp;°C]). The hottest month is February with an average temperature of 71.6 (22&nbsp;°C). Rainfall is abundant, amounting to an annual average of 1,400 millimetres (57.2&nbsp;in).

== Media ==
In telecommunications, the city was served by [[:pt:Companhia_Telefônica_Brasileira|Companhia Telefônica Brasileira]] until 1973, when it began to be served by [[:pt:Telecomunicações_de_São_Paulo|Telecomunicações de São Paulo]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.imprensaoficial.com.br/DO/BuscaDO2001Documento_11_4.aspx?link=/1973/ineditoriais/abril/24/p1/pag_0054_DUI6O3SN7V5CNe7E7ANSRTTG04F.pdf&pagina=54&data=24/04/1973&caderno=Ineditoriais&paginaordenacao=100054|title=Creation of Telesp - April 12, 1973|access-date=2024-05-27|website=www.imprensaoficial.com.br|language=Portuguese}}</ref> In July 1998, this company was acquired by Telefónica, which adopted the [[Vivo (telecommunications company)|Vivo]] brand in 2012.<ref name=":vivo" />

The company is currently an operator of cell phones, fixed lines, internet (fiber optics/4G) and television (satellite and cable).<ref name=":vivo">{{Cite web|url=https://ri.telefonica.com.br/en/the-company/our-history/|title=Our History - Telefônica RI|access-date=2024-05-27|website=Telefônica}}</ref>

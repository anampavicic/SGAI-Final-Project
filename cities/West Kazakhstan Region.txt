'''West Kazakhstan Region''' ({{langx|kk|Батыс Қазақстан облысы|translit=Batys Qazaqstan oblysy}}; {{langx|ru|Западно-Казахстанская область|translit=Zapadno-Kazakhstanskaya oblast}}) is a [[Regions of Kazakhstan|region]] of [[Kazakhstan]]. The region has a population of 695,000 as of July 1st, 2024.<ref name=":01">{{Cite web|script-title=ru:Западно-Казахстанская область - Статистика регионов РК|url=https://stat.gov.kz/ru/region/zko/|access-date=2023-07-18|publisher=Committee on Statistics of the Ministry of National Economy of the Republic of Kazakhstan|language=ru, kk|title=}}</ref> Its administrative centre is [[Oral, Kazakhstan|Oral]] (also known as Uralsk or Ural'sk), a city of about 250,000 inhabitants.

The Region was created as ''West Kazakhstan Oblast'' in [[Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic|Kazakh SSR]] of [[Soviet Union]]. Between 1962 and 1992 it was named ''Ural’sk Oblast'' and was renamed in 1993 to the old name following the independence of Kazakhstan from the Soviet Union.

==Geography==
The region borders [[Russia]] and is near the [[Ural Mountains]]. The [[Ural River]] is the border between [[Asia]] and [[Europe]] and flows from Russia to the [[Caspian Sea]] through the region, meaning the extreme west of Kazakhstan is in Eastern Europe. West Kazakhstan region borders [[Aktobe Region]] to the east and [[Atyrau Region]] to the south, in addition to the following Russian [[Oblasts of Russia|Oblasts]]: [[Astrakhan Oblast]] to the south-west, [[Volgograd Oblast]] to the west, [[Saratov Oblast]] to the north-west, [[Samara Oblast]] to the north and [[Orenburg Oblast]] to the north-east.

It is traversed by the northeasterly line of equal latitude and longitude. There are numerous lakes in the region, including [[Shalkar (Terekti District)|Shalkar]], [[Balykty Sarkyl]], [[Botkul]] and [[Aralsor]].<ref name="STMa">{{cite web|url=https://maps.vlasenko.net/smtm1000/m-39.jpg|title=M-39 Topographic Chart (in Russian)|access-date=8 August 2022}}</ref>

== Economy ==
In the first half of 2019, the gross regional product amounted to ₸1.4 trillion, an increase of ₸145 million from the previous year.{{Citation needed|date=August 2020}} For 9 months of this year,{{Clarify|reason=|date=August 2020}} industrial output amounted to almost ₸1.8 trillion.{{Citation needed|date=August 2020}} For the first quarter of 2020, the West Kazakhstan Region reported an [[Unemployment Rate|unemployment rate]] of 4.8%, and an average monthly salary of ₸186,857.<ref>{{Cite web|script-title=ru:ОСНОВНЫЕ СОЦИАЛЬНО-ЭКОНОМИЧЕСКИЕ ПОКАЗАТЕЛИ|url=https://stat.gov.kz/region/253160?lang=ru|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200804003557/https://stat.gov.kz/region/253160?lang=ru|archive-date=2020-08-04|access-date=2020-08-03|publisher=Committee on Statistics of the Ministry of National Economy of the Republic of Kazakhstan|language=ru}}</ref>

The development of the agro-industrial complex is also one of the drivers of the region’s economy. During the first half of 2020, the gross agricultural production of the region totaled ₸59.8252 billion, of which ₸59.6761 billion came from livestock farming, and the remaining amount coming from crop production and various agricultural services.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|script-title=ru:Сельское хозяйство|trans-title=Agriculture|url=https://www.gov.kz/memleket/entities/bko/activities/446?lang=ru|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200804005739/https://www.gov.kz/memleket/entities/bko/activities/446?lang=ru|archive-date=2020-08-04|access-date=2020-08-03|publisher=Akimat of the West Kazakhstan Region|language=ru}}</ref> The main crops grown in the region include wheat, barley, sunflower, safflower, and potatoes.<ref name=":2" /> As of July 1, 2020, the region has approximately 691,800 heads of cattle, 227,100 horses, 1.63 million heads of poultry, 1.21 million heads of sheep, 243,900 goats, 16,200 pigs, and 2,300 camels.<ref name=":2" /> There are 196 agricultural cooperatives registered in the region.<ref name=":2" />

In general, today 42 large new investment projects worth over ₸1 trillion are being implemented in the region, creating about 5,000 jobs.{{Citation needed|date=August 2020}} As of 2020, the region has 7 large-scale investment projects with foreign participation, totaling about ₸1.9 trillion, and projected by the regional government to create more than 500 jobs.<ref>{{Cite web|script-title=ru:Инвестиции|trans-title=Investment|url=https://www.gov.kz/memleket/entities/bko/activities/443?lang=ru|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200804004720/https://www.gov.kz/memleket/entities/bko/activities/443?lang=ru|archive-date=2020-08-04|access-date=2020-08-03|publisher=Akimat of the West Kazakhstan Region|language=ru}}</ref>

== Culture ==
The West Kazakhstan Region has a rich historical and cultural heritage. In the region, there are more than 6,000 historical and archaeological sites of different eras and more than 500 historical, architectural and art monuments, 14 of which are monuments of national significance.{{Citation needed|date=August 2020}} 
In the field of culture and art of the region there are 717 state-run organizations.{{Citation needed|date=August 2020}} In order to study, preserve and popularize the historical and cultural heritage in the region there are 24 museums in the region, including 2 private ones, and 13 in rural areas.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|script-title=ru:Культура|trans-title=Culture|url=https://www.gov.kz/memleket/entities/bko/activities/230?lang=ru|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200803165456/https://www.gov.kz/memleket/entities/bko/activities/230?lang=ru|archive-date=2020-08-03|access-date=2020-08-03|publisher=Akimat of the West Kazakhstan region|language=ru}}</ref> At the end of 2019, the region was home to 364 libraries, of which, 340 were in rural areas, hosting 1,303,352 visitors and 5,819,524 works.<ref name=":1" /> The region's libraries are in a process of [[Digitization|digitalizing]] their works, with 4,642 books and 175 other works being digitalized in the first half of 2020.<ref name=":1" /> The region is also home to 263 cultural centers, 237 of which are located in rural areas.<ref name=":1" />

==Sport==
The capital Oral is home to Kazakhstan's only professional [[bandy]] club, [[Akzhaiyk]]. In 2016 the national championship was won by the regional team<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sports.kz/news/sbornaya-zko-stala-chempionom-kazahstana-po-hokkeyu-s-myachom |title=Сборная ЗКО стала чемпионом Казахстана по хоккею с мячом |publisher=Sports.kz |access-date=2018-08-18}}</ref> and in the variety [[rink bandy]], a national title was obtained in 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://timeskz.kz/3670-na-chempionate-rk-po-rink-bendi-komanda-zko-zanyala-pervoe-mesto.html|title=На чемпионате РК по ринк-бенди команда ЗКО заняла первое место}}</ref>

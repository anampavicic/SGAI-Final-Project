The '''Capital Governorate''' ({{Langx|ar|محافظة العاصمة|Muḥāfaẓat al-ʿĀṣimah}}) is one of the four [[governorates of Bahrain]]. The governorate includes [[Manama]], the capital of Bahrain. It is the most populous administrative region in the country, with a population of over half million people in 2020.

==Formation==
The Capital Governorate was formed by royal decree on 3 July 2002. The present governorate incorporates the municipalities of [[Manama|Al Manamah]], [[Jid Ali]], [[Ras Rumman]] and parts of [[Jidd Haffs]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Decree Law No. (17) of 2002 |url=http://www.capital.gov.bh/pages/pdf/govlawe.pdf|publisher=Capital Governorate|accessdate=10 August 2012}}</ref>

==Demographics==
According to a [[census]] conducted in 2010, there are 329,510 people living in the Capital Governorate, which is the highest of the four governorates; 261,921 non-Bahraini citizens and 67,589 Bahraini nationals.<ref>{{cite web|title=Population by Governorate|url=http://www.cio.gov.bh/cio_ara/English/Publications/Census/Population/2.pdf|publisher=Census 2010|accessdate=10 August 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131025002821/http://www.cio.gov.bh/cio_ara/English/Publications/Census/Population/2.pdf#|archive-date=2013-10-25|url-status=dead}}</ref>  The vast majority of housing in the Capital Governorate are [[Apartment|flats]], with around 34,000 flats.<ref name=Cen/> Private [[villa]]s were the second most-common form of housing, accounting for 7,284 of housing in the governorate.<ref name=Cen>{{cite web|title=Census results|url=http://www.census2010.gov.bh/results_en.php|publisher=Census2010|accessdate=10 August 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120320104234/http://www.census2010.gov.bh/results_en.php#|archive-date=2012-03-20|url-status=dead}}</ref> 

As at the 2020 Census, the governorate's total population stands at 548,345 people in a land area of 78.71 km<sup>2</sup>, with a population density of 6,967 per km<sup>2</sup>.<ref name="CG">{{cite web |title=Annual Report 2020 |url=https://www.capital.gov.bh/Media/Pdf/5191735b-e95d-4002-ac13-d971422a2239CG%20Annual%20Report%20English%202020.pdf |website=Capital Governorate |access-date=14 July 2021}}</ref>

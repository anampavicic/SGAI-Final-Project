'''Modinagar''' is a town and a [[municipal board]] in [[Ghaziabad district, Uttar Pradesh|Ghaziabad district]] in the [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Uttar Pradesh]]. It's just 24&nbsp;km from [[Ghaziabad|Ghaziabad city]], 48&nbsp;km from [[New Delhi]]. It is a part of [[National Capital Region (India)|National Capital Region]].

The city was founded in 1933 by [[Gujarmal Modi]] who established the [[Modi Group|Modi Group of Industries]] here along with his brother, Kedar Nath Modi. It is approximately 45 [[kilometres]] north-east of [[New Delhi]]. It is situated on Ghaziabad Meerut [[National Highway 58 (India)|National Highway No. 58]], approximately equally distant from [[Meerut]] and [[Ghaziabad, India|Ghaziabad]]. It is best known as the home of business conglomerate [[Modi Enterprises]]. In recent years, Modinagar has been developed as an [[educational]] hub as many new institutions are being established in and around the city due to its location in [[National Capital Region]]. Modinagar is also a part of [[Delhi–Meerut Regional Rapid Transit System]]. It is connected to recently constructed Delhi - Meerut Expressway (NE-3) via Bhojpur exit.

==Boundaries==

It lies in Lat. 280 50' north and Long. 770 35' east, 25&nbsp;km north-east of [[Ghaziabad, India|Ghaziabad]].

Modinagar lies on the [[Delhi]]-[[Mussoorie]] National Highway ([[National Highway 58 (India)|NH-58]]). Parallel to this road runs the [[Delhi]]-[[Saharanpur]] section of the [[Northern Railway (India)|Northern Railway]]. A metalled road going to [[Hapur]] originates from the city. [[Modinagar railway station]] is in between [[Meerut]] and [[Ghaziabad, India|Ghaziabad]] stations. Most of the major express trains as well as all general passenger [[trains]] from [[Delhi]] to Meerut-[[Haridwar]]-[[Dehradun]] route stop at this railway station.

== Economics ==
Modinagar is known as the home of Modi Enterprises. The area has long been associated with sugar mills. The climate and good irrigation are ideal for growing fruit and vegetables. In recent years, Modinagar has grown as a bedroom community to [[Delhi]] and hosts a growing technology industry.

==Government and politics==
In 2022', Assembly elections [[Bhartiya Janta Party]] candidate Manju Shiwach won the election.

The city administration is based at Tehsil, Modinagar and both the top chairs of the city namely, Sub Divisional Magistrate and The Circle Officer of Police have office at Tehsil.

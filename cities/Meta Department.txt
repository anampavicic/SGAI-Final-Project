'''Meta''' ({{IPA|es|ˈmeta}}) is a [[departments of Colombia|department]] of [[Colombia]]. It is close to the geographic center of the country, to the east of the [[Andean]] mountains. A large portion of the department, which is also crossed by the [[Meta River]], is covered by a grassland plain known as the [[Llanos]]. Its capital is [[Villavicencio]]. The department has a monument placed in the very geographic centre of Colombia, at a place known as Alto de Menegua, a few kilometers from Puerto López.

[[Achagua language|Achagua]], which is similar to Piapoco, is an [[indigenous languages of the Americas|Indigenous language]] spoken by a minority in the department.

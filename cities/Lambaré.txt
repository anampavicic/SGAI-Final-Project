'''Lambaré''' ({{IPA|es|lambaˈɾe}}) is a city in [[Central Department]], [[Paraguay]], part of the [[Gran Asunción]] metropolitan area. With a population of approximately 140,000 inhabitants, it is the [[List of cities in Paraguay by population|sixth-most populous city in the country]].

==Toponymy==

The name of the town derive from the [[Guarani language]] ''Ambaré'', "the land of shadows". The people were known as ''avambaré'', citizens of El Ambaré.

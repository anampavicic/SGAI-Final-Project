The '''Capital District''' ({{langx|es|Distrito Capital}}) is a [[federal district]] of [[Venezuela]]. It has an area of {{Convert|433|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}} and there is only one administrative division (''municipio''), [[Libertador Municipality, Caracas|Libertador]], which contains about half of [[Caracas]], the Venezuelan [[capital city]], which is also the seat of the three branches of the federal government of Venezuela. The population in 2004 was 2,073,768. The District borders on the states of [[Vargas (state)|Vargas]] and [[Miranda (state)|Miranda]].

==Government==
{{main|Head of the Government of the Distrito Capital}}
Formerly it had its own [[local government]] with a governor, but the [[Constitution of Venezuela|constitutional reform of 1999]] abolished the district government and created instead the [[Caracas|Metropolitan District of Caracas]], with jurisdiction over the territory of the District and also four adjacent ''municipios'' (Baruta, Chacao, el Hatillo and Sucre) in [[Miranda (state)|Miranda]] which all together form the city.

On 13 April 2009, the [[National Assembly (Venezuela)|National Assembly]] passed a law creating the figure of a head of government for the district which would be designated by the President.<ref>Official Gazette, 14 April 2009, {{in lang|es}} [http://150.188.8.226/cgi-win/be_alex.exe?Documento=T020700036341/1&Nombrebd=bibconsulta&term_termino_2=\192.168.215.23/pgr/alexandr/db/bibpgr/edocs/2009/39157.pdf&term_termino_3=&term_termino_5=pdf&term_termino_4=%201&ForReg=http://150.188.8.226/recursos/gaceta.asp&TiposDoc=S Official announcement]{{dead link|date=November 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> On 14 April 2009, [[Jacqueline Faría]] became the first Head of Government of the Capital District.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.abn.info.ve/noticia.php?articulo=177609&lee=10 |title = The World of Supernatural Beings - Alternative Belief News| date=June 2015 }}</ref> During the [[2017 Venezuelan protests]], General [[Antonio José Benavides|Antonio José Benavides Torres]] of the National Guard was appointed to be the Head of Government of the Capital District by disputed President Maduro.<ref>{{cite news|title=Himno Nacional, Alma Llanera y sana PAZ sin órganos de represión: Marcha nocturna honró a los caídos en sede OEA|url=https://www.lapatilla.com/site/2017/06/21/himno-nacional-alma-llanera-y-sana-paz-sin-organos-de-represion-marcha-nocturna-honro-a-los-caidos-en-sede-oea/|access-date=22 June 2017|work=[[La Patilla]]|date=21 June 2017|language=es-ES}}</ref>

'''Mukono Town''' is a municipality in [[Mukono District]] in the [[Central Region, Uganda|Central Region]] of  [[Uganda]]. The town is administered by the Mukono Town Council. The district headquarters are located in this town.<ref name="2R">{{cite web
|url=https://www.monitor.co.ug/uganda/special-reports/mukono-the-king-s-hand-1562996 |title=Mukono: The King's Hand |date=12 January 2014 |newspaper=[[Daily Monitor]] |author=Johnson Mayamba |access-date=7 March 2021 |location=Kampala}}</ref> 

==Location==
Mukono Municipality is 21&nbsp;km east of Kampala along the Kampala-Jinja Highway.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Location & Size {{!}} Mukono DIstrict |url=https://mukono.go.ug/lg/location-size |access-date=2024-05-20 |website=mukono.go.ug}}</ref> It is bordered by [[Kalagi, Mukono, Uganda|Kalagi]] to the north, [[Kira Town]] to the west, [[Lake Victoria]] to the south, and [[Lugazi]] to the east. The town is about {{convert|24.5|km}} east of the central business district of [[Kampala]], Uganda's capital and largest city.<ref name="3R">{{cite web |title=Map Showing Kampala And Mukono With Distance Marker |publisher=Globefeed.com |date=15 April 2014 |access-date=6 March 2021 |author=Globefeed.com
|url=https://distancecalculator.globefeed.com/Uganda_Distance_Result.asp?fromplace=Kampala%20()&toplace=Mukono%20(Mukono)&fromlat=0.3155556&tolat=0.3533333&fromlng=32.5655556&tolng=32.7552778}}</ref> This is approximately {{convert|25|km}}, west of the town of [[Lugazi]], in neighboring [[Buikwe District]].<ref name="4R">{{google maps| url=https://www.google.com/maps/dir/Mukono/Lugazi/@0.388052,32.705377,11z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m14!4m13!1m5!1m1!1s0x177dc7b71409b0a5:0xdddaf82b549ec570!2m2!1d32.7520139!2d0.3548655!1m5!1m1!1s0x177dd073b20aada5:0xacb1dc220540be1e!2m2!1d32.9381321!2d0.3739085!3e0 | title=Road Distance Between Mukono, Uganda And Lugazi, Uganda | access-date=7 March 2021}}</ref>

==Organizational structure==
Mukono Municipality administration is divided into (a) the political arm headed by the [[Mayor]], (b) the technical arm headed by the [[Town Clerk]] and the legislative arm, headed by the Speaker of the [[town council]]. The council is the supreme policy-making organ in the town. It is composed of 26 elected councilors. Members of the town council serve four-year terms.
The mayor of the town is elected to a five-year term by universal adult suffrage using a secret ballot. The technical staff of the town council are headed by the town clerk under whom there are four municipal departments: the Management Department, the Treasury Department, the Public Health Department, and the Engineering Department.<ref name="OrgR">{{cite web| url=https://mukonomunicipalcouncil.go.ug/about-Municipal.html | title=Leadership composition of Mukono Municipality | publisher=Mukono Municipal Council |date=8 March 2021 | author=Mukono Municipality |access-date=8 March 2021}}</ref>

'''Ise Ekiti''' ({{lang-yo|Ìṣẹ̀-Èkìtì}}, also '''Ise''') is a city in [[Ekiti State]], [[Nigeria]], It is the traditional home of Akinluaduse, also known as Akinluse by the inhabitants of the city. Akinluse was a great warrior in the ancient Oyo empire. Ise - Ekiti is the headquarters of <ref name="nigeria congress">{{cite web|url= http://www.nigeriacongress.org/fgn/administrative/lgadetails.asp?lg=Ise/Orun|title= Ise/Orun Local Government Area|access-date= 2007-01-11|year= 2004|work= www.nigeriacongress.org|publisher= West African Non-Governmental Organisation Network|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20040109165327/http://www.nigeriacongress.org/fgn/administrative/lgadetails.asp?lg=Ise%2FOrun|archive-date= 2004-01-09|url-status= dead}}</ref> the [[Ise/Orun]] [[Local Government Areas in Nigeria|Local Government Area]], along with [[Ise/Orun|Orun]].<ref name="sunnews">{{cite news|first= Maurice|last= Archibong|title= Ise Ekiti: Mirror on Nigeria's fountain of knowledge|url= http://www.sunnewsonline.com/webpages/features/travels/2006/july/06/travels-06-07-2006-001.htm|publisher= Sun News Online|date= 2006-07-06|access-date= 2007-01-11|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20060822152254/http://www.sunnewsonline.com/webpages/features/travels/2006/july/06/travels-06-07-2006-001.htm|archive-date= 2006-08-22|url-status= dead}}</ref>
Its geographic coordinates are {{coord|7|27|36|N|5|25|12|E|region:NG_type:city(204022)|display=inline,title}}.<ref name="gaisma">{{cite web |url= http://www.gaisma.com/en/location/ise.html|title= Ise, Nigeria - Sunrise, sunset, dawn and dusk times, table|access-date=2007-01-11 |last= Tukiainen|first=Matti |work=Gaisma }}</ref> Ise ekiti is divided into three quarters namely; Oraye, Odo Ise and Erinwa. The Arinjale of Ise-Ekiti is Oba Ayodele Ajayi Aweloye II. .<ref name="sunnews"/>

As of 2007 Ise local government had an estimated population of 204,022.<ref name="world gazetteer">{{cite web|url=http://www.world-gazetteer.com/wg.php?men=gpro&des=gamelan&geo=350874542 |title="The World Gazetteer" |access-date=2007-04-06 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20130209121150/http://www.world-gazetteer.com/wg.php?men=gpro&des=gamelan&geo=350874542 |archive-date=2013-02-09 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

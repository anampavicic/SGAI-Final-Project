'''South Governorate''' ({{langx|ar|محافظة الجنوب|muḥāfaẓat al-Janūb}}, or simply {{lang|ar|الجنوب}}) is one of the [[governorates of Lebanon]].  South Lebanon has a population of 590,000 inhabitants and an area of 929.6&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>. The capital is [[Sidon]]. The lowest elevation is sea-level; the highest is 1,000 meters. The local population is religiously diverse and includes [[Shia Islam in Lebanon|Shia]] and [[Sunni Islam in Lebanon|Sunni]] Muslims, [[Druze]], [[Eastern Orthodox Christianity in Lebanon|Eastern Orthodox]], [[Maronites in Lebanon|Maronite]], [[Protestantism in Lebanon|Protestant]], and Greek Catholic Christians. Temperatures can drop to 4&nbsp;°C during winter with much rain and snow on the higher ground. In the humid summer, temperatures can rise to 30&nbsp;°C in the coastal areas. The governorate has several rivers: the [[Litani River|Litani]], Deir El Zahrani, [[Naqoura]], [[Awali (river)|Awali]], Qasmiye, and [[Hasbani River|Hasbani]]. The area is famous for its citrus and banana farms. Its main cities are [[Sidon]], [[Tyre, Lebanon|Tyre]] and [[Jezzine]].

==Local attractions and events==
The area offers a great number of attractions, including pristine white sandy beaches south of [[Tyre (Lebanon)|Tyre]], and the opportunity to snorkel or dive among submerged Phoenician and Roman ruins near the ancient cities of [[Sidon]] and [[Tyre (Lebanon)|Tyre]]. Culture-lovers will enjoy the bustling Ottoman-era Pazars, and one can relax after a long day at a seafood restaurant overlooking the Mediterranean. [[Southern Lebanon]] also hosts the [[Tyre (Lebanon)|Tyre]] festival, which attracts thousands of tourists each year.

Shias make up the majority of the governorate and are the main residents of the Zahrani Tyre districts. Sunnis are the main residents of Saida the capital of the governorate and the third largest city in Lebanon after Beirut and Tripoli. Christians are mainly residents of the Jezzine district and form a small community in the city of Tyre, which is the fourth-most-populous city in Lebanon.

'''Qarchak''' ({{langx|fa|قرچک}}){{efn|Also [[romanize]]d as '''Qar Chak'''; also known as '''Qareh Chak'''<ref>{{GEOnet3|-3782659}}</ref>}} is a city in the [[Central District (Qarchak County)|Central District]] of [[Qarchak County]], [[Tehran province|Tehran]] province, [[Iran]], serving as capital of both the county and the district.<ref name="Qarchak County Establishment">{{cite report|title=Approval letter regarding the establishment of Qarchak County under the citizenship of Tehran province|language=fa|website=rc.majlis.ir|via=Research Center of the System of Laws of the Islamic Council of Farabi Mobile Library|url=https://rc.majlis.ir/fa/law/show/826690|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130110180800/https://rc.majlis.ir/fa/law/show/826690|publisher=Ministry of the Interior, Council of Ministers|last=Rahimi|first=Mohammad Reza|archive-date=10 January 2013|date=c. 2013|orig-date=Approved 21 October 1391|id=Notification 200678/T47140H|access-date=24 November 2023}}</ref>

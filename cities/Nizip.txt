'''Nizip''' ({{langx|grc-x-medieval|Nisibis or Nisibina}}; {{langx|ota|نزيب}}<ref>Tahir Sezen ''Osmanlı Yer Adları (Alfabetik Sırayla)'', T.C. Başbakanlık Devlet Arşivleri Genel Müdürlüğü, Yayın Nu 21, Ankara, p. 385.</ref>) is a municipality and [[Districts of Turkey|district]] of [[Gaziantep Province]], [[Turkey]].<ref>[https://www.e-icisleri.gov.tr/Anasayfa/MulkiIdariBolumleri.aspx Büyükşehir İlçe Belediyesi], Turkey Civil Administration Departments Inventory. Retrieved 12 July 2023.</ref> Its area is 944&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.harita.gov.tr/uploads/files-folder/il_ilce_alanlari.xlsx|title=İl ve İlçe Yüz ölçümleri|publisher=General Directorate of Mapping|access-date=12 July 2023}}</ref> and its population is 148,386 (2022).<ref name=tuik>{{Cite web |title=Address-based population registration system (ADNKS) results dated 31 December 2022, Favorite Reports|url=https://biruni.tuik.gov.tr/medas/?kn=95&locale=en |access-date=12 July 2023|publisher=[[TÜİK]]|language=en|format=XLS}}</ref>

It is located 45&nbsp;km from the city of [[Gaziantep]], 95&nbsp;km from [[Şanlıurfa]] ([[Edessa, Mesopotamia|Edessa]]), and 35&nbsp;km from [[Karkamış]].

==Demographics==
In early 20th century, the town housed 15,000 people and was mostly inhabited by Turks.<ref>{{cite book |last1=Banse |first1=Ewald |author1-link=Ewald Banse |title=Ewald Banse's Lexikon der Geographie L bis Z |date=1923 |publisher=G. Westermann |page=198 |quote=Nisib, nö Syrien, Stadt von 15000, meist türk E.}}</ref>

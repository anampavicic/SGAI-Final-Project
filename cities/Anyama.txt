'''Anyama''' is a city in south-eastern [[Ivory Coast]]. It is a suburb of [[Abidjan]] and is one of four [[sub-prefectures of Ivory Coast|sub-prefectures]] of [[Abidjan Autonomous District]]. Anyama is also a [[Communes of Ivory Coast|commune]]. The city is located about 20 kilometres north of Abidjan.

Villages in the sub-prefecture include [[Akoupé-Zeudji]], [[Attinguié]], and [[M'Bonoua]].

==Sport==
Anyama is represented in football by [[Rio Sport d'Anyama]].

{{infobox Chinese|psp=Tengchow|c=滕州|w=T'eng<sup>2</sup>-chou<sup>1</sup>|p=Téngzhōu}}
'''Tengzhou''' ({{zh|s=[[:zh:滕州|滕州]]|p=Téngzhōu}}) is a [[county-level city]] of [[Zaozhuang]], [[Shandong]] province of the [[People's Republic of China]], and is the site of the feudal vassal [[Teng (state)|State of Teng]] during the [[Spring and Autumn period]].

Tengzhou was likely the birthplace of the philosopher [[Mozi]], and the city hosts the [[Mozi Memorial Hall]] ({{Lang-zh|c=|s=墨子纪念馆|t=墨子紀念館|p=}}) to commemorate him.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|date=2010-07-19|script-title=zh:滕州市概况|url=http://xzqh.org/html/show/sd/12164.html|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201207195410/http://xzqh.org/html/show/sd/12164.html|archive-date=2020-12-07|access-date=2020-12-07|website=xzqh.org|language=zh}}</ref>

The [[mayor]] of Tengzhou is [[Liu Wenqiang]], and the Tengzhou [[Party Committee Secretary]] is [[Shao Shiguan]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-02-02|script-title=zh:邵士官、刘文强到龙泉街道走访慰问老干部|url=https://www.sohu.com/a/293067248_120060731|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201207204852/https://www.sohu.com/a/293067248_120060731|archive-date=2020-12-07|access-date=2020-12-07|website=[[Sohu]]|language=zh}}</ref>

Tengzhou has an area of {{Convert|1485|km2|mi2|abbr=}},<ref name=":0" /> and a population of 1,756,300 as of 2019.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|script-title=zh:人口民族|url=http://www.tengzhou.gov.cn/xtzgk/201407/t20140729_2713876.html|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201207192022/http://www.tengzhou.gov.cn/xtzgk/201407/t20140729_2713876.html|archive-date=2020-12-07|access-date=2020-12-07|publisher=Tengzhou People's Government|language=zh}}</ref> As of the end of 2022, the resident population of Tengzhou was 1,553,200.<ref name=":5">{{Cite web |last=People's Government of Tengzhou |date=2023-05-16 |title=2022年滕州市国民经济和社会发展统计公报 |trans-title=Statistical Bulletin on National Economic and Social Development of Tengzhou City, 2022 |url=http://xxgk.tengzhou.gov.cn/xxgkml/szbm/tjj/202308/t20230803_1731971.html |access-date=2024-04-30 |website=xxgk.tengzhou.gov.cn}}</ref>

== History ==
The settlement of Tengzhou is said to date back to the [[Yellow Emperor]].<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|date=2010-07-19|script-title=zh:滕州市沿革|url=http://xzqh.org/html/show/sd/12163.html|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201207192844/http://xzqh.org/html/show/sd/12163.html|archive-date=2020-12-07|access-date=2020-12-07|website=xzqh.org|language=zh}}</ref>

After the collapse of the [[Shang dynasty]], present-day Tengzhou became the center of the independent [[Teng (state)|Teng state]].<ref name=":2" /> Eventually, it was conquered by the [[Qin (state)|Qin state]].<ref name=":2" />

Upon the reunification of the region, the region became incorporated as Teng County ({{Lang-zh|c=|s=滕县|t=滕縣|p=}}).<ref name=":2" /> It remained as Teng County until the [[Jin dynasty (1115–1234)|Jin Dynasty]], when it became the Tengyang Prefecture ({{Lang-zh|c=|s=滕阳州|t=滕陽州|p=}}).<ref name=":2" /> The prefecture was abolished at the start of the [[Ming dynasty|Ming Dynasty]].<ref name=":2" />

From 1945 to 1953, the county changed administration numerous times, before finally becoming Teng County.<ref name=":2" />

On March 7, 1988, Teng County became the [[county-level city]] of Tengzhou.<ref name=":2" />

== Geography ==
Tengzhou is Located on the [[Huanghuai]] [[Plain]], it borders the Taiyi Mountain to the east and [[Weishan Lake]] to the west, and has a minimum elevation of {{Convert|33.5|m|ft|abbr=}}, and a maximum elevation of {{Convert|596.6|m|ft|abbr=}}.<ref name=":0" />

=== Climate ===
Tengzhou is located in the southern edge of the warm temperate zone, the average temperature of the coldest month is -0.2&nbsp;°C. Tengzhou experiences an average annual temperature of {{Convert|13.6|°C|°F|abbr=}}, and an average precipitation of {{Convert|773.1|mm|in|abbr=}}.There are four distinct seasons with abundant rainfall and plenty of light.<ref name=":0" />

== Demographics ==
As of 2019, Tengzhou is home to 1,756,300 people, living in 518,500 households.<ref name=":1" /> As of the end of 2022, the resident population of Tengzhou was 1,553,200.<ref name=":5" /> The city has about 927,900 males and 828,400 females.<ref name=":1" /> The city has a [[birth rate]] is 10.65 per thousand, a [[Death Rate|death rate]] of 6.15 per thousand, and a [[rate of natural increase]] of 4.49 per thousand.<ref name=":1" />

In addition to the city's [[Han Chinese]] population, 30 [[Ethnic minorities in China|ethnic minorities]] live in Tengzhou, the largest of which is the [[Hui people]].<ref name=":1" /> As of 2009, the city's ethnic minority population totaled 4,050, with the Hui people comprising 2,280 of this population.<ref name=":2" />

== Economy ==

=== Agriculture ===
In 2019, 788,100 tons of agricultural produce was made, a 2.1% increase from 2018.<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|script-title=zh:产业结构|url=http://www.tengzhou.gov.cn/xtzgk/201407/t20140729_2713880.html|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201030133654/http://www.tengzhou.gov.cn/xtzgk/201407/t20140729_2713880.html|archive-date=2020-10-30|access-date=2020-12-07|publisher=Tengzhou People's Government|language=zh}}</ref> 56,200 hectares of vegetables were harvested, representing a 0.7% increase from 2018.<ref name=":4" /> The city's pigs were afflicted by [[African swine fever virus|African swine fever]], resulting in a 27.4% drop in live pigs from 2018 to 2019.<ref name=":4" /> The city slaughtered 27.08 million heads of poultry in 2019, a 5.7% increase from the previous year.<ref name=":4" /> Tengzhou's aquaculture output totaled 41.2 thousand tons in 2019, a decline of 8.9% from the previous year.<ref name=":4" />

=== Industry ===
As of 2019, Tengzhou had 254 industrial enterprises above designated size.<ref name=":4" /> The value added of industrial enterprises above designated size increased by 3.2% from 2019, with light industry increasing 1.3%, and heavy industry increasing 3.8%.<ref name=":4" /> Major industries in Tengzhou include chemicals, minerals, petroleum, coal, food processing, Chinese medicine, electronic components, and cement.<ref name=":4" />

In the early 2010s, Tengzhou, which had been a major manufacturing area, was experiencing an economic downturn which closed many local factories.<ref name="WSJ07202015">Magnier, Mark, [https://www.wsj.com/articles/for-many-firms-chinas-new-normal-spells-doom-1437355230 "For Many Firms, China’s ‘New Normal’ Spells Doom : Industrial city’s struggle illustrates how slower economic growth is squeezing manufacturers"], ''[[The Wall Street Journal]]'', July 20, 2015</ref> However, ''[[The Wall Street Journal]]'' reported in July 2015 that innovation, particularly in manufacturing, had revitalized the local economy.<ref name="WSJ07202015" />

=== Retail ===
In 2019, the city's total retail sales of consumer goods increased by 4.9%, with urban retail sales increasing 3.6%, and rural retail sales increasing by 9.9%.<ref name=":4" /> In 2019, Tengzhou's auto sector grew by 10.7%; its books, newspapers, and magazines retail sales increased by 16.5%; Tengzhou's sports and entertainment goods retail sales increased by 27.7%.<ref name=":4" />

=== Foreign investment and trade ===
In 2019, Tengzhou received $45.59 million of foreign investment, which was utilized for 19 different projects.<ref name=":4" /> In 2019, ¥328 million worth of goods was imported into Tengzhou, and ¥3.445 billion worth of goods was exported from Tengzhou.<ref name=":4" />

== Main tourist attractions ==
'''Mozi Memorial Museum，''' – set academic seminars, library collection, science and technology education, visits in one of the comprehensive courtyard building groups. The east courtyard is the Mozi Memorial Hall, which mainly exhibits Mozi's biography, scientific and technological military achievements, results, Mozi statues, etc. The west courtyard is the office area of Tengzhou Mozi Research Center of Shandong University. The west courtyard is the office area of Mozi Research Center of Tengzhou City, Shandong University, which is equipped with a gallery of celebrities' inscriptions and a collection of domestic and foreign Mozi research books and materials.<ref name=":6">{{Cite web |last=Tengzhou Culture and Tourism Network |date=2017-06-14 |title=滕州市旅游景点 |trans-title=Tengzhou Tourist Attractions |url=http://www.tengzhou.gov.cn/zzq/zfbm/lyhfwyfzj |access-date=2024-04-30 |website=www.tengzhou.gov.cn}}</ref>

'''Lu Ban Memorial Hall''' – there are the Hall of Sacred Ancestors, the Hall of Public Ceremonies, the Hall of Woodworking Instruments, the Hall of Stone Weapons, the Hall of Weapons, the Hall of Architecture, the Hall of Boats and Bridges, the Hall of Science and Technology Education for Youths, and the Hall of Achievements of Luban's Successors.<ref name=":6" />

'''Weishan Lake Wetland Red Lotus Scenic Area''' – There are more than 50 attractions in the scenic area, such as Panlong Island, Xiaolizhuang, Aquatic Plant Garden, Wetland Rafting Garden, Lotus Boutique Garden, Wetland Museum and so on. Weishan Lake Wetland Red Lotus Tourist Scenic Area is the largest and most primitive preservation state in East China, with the best wetland landscape and the largest lotus ornamental place in China.It is known as the "Lotus Capital of China".<ref name=":6" />

'''Wang Xuezhong Art Gallery''' – Inside the hall, there are Strider Garden, Strider Academy, Poetry Monument Gallery, Double Pond Lotus Cui, Stone Bridge Maple Forest, and Academy Corridor.There are exhibition halls, cultural relics exhibition rooms, ancient painting and calligraphy exhibition rooms, lecture halls, data rooms, and reception rooms. It is a collection, exhibition, research, and exchange in one of the cultural and boogey units, the main collection of Mr. Wang Xuezhong's calligraphy, Chinese painting, oil painting, watercolor paintings created over the years.<ref name=":6" />

'''Longshan Scenic Area''' – with Longshan Mountain as the main body, it belongs to Taiyishan Mountain Range together with Taishan Mountain and Yishan Mountain.In the north, it is connected with Tai Mountain and Yishan Mountain, and in the east and west, it is adjacent to the scenic spots of Weishan Lake Red Lotus Wetland and Lianqing Mountain."Longling Clear Clouds" is listed as the top of the eight scenic spots in Guteng.<ref name=":6" />

'''Tengguo Old Town''' – It was the political, economic and cultural center of Tengguo at that time. In the scenic spot, there are mainly Wengong Ancient Platform, Teng Wengong Building, Ancient City Wall, Li Bai Spectacular Stone Carving, Lv Zu Pavilion and other historical landscapes. Tengguo Old Town is a famous tourist attraction in Zaozhuang, and Wengong Ancient Platform is one of the "Eight Scenic Spots of Ancient Teng" and one of the top ten scenic spots in Zaozhuang.<ref name=":6" />

=== Rail ===
The [[Beijing–Shanghai railway]], the [[Beijing–Shanghai high-speed railway]] both run through the city.<ref name=":0" />

=== Maritime ===
The city's two ports, Xin'an Port ({{Lang-zh|c=|s=辛安港|t=|p=}}) and Tengzhou Port ({{Lang-zh|c=|s=滕州港|t=|p=}}) are connected to the [[Beijing-Hangzhou Grand Canal]].<ref name=":0" />

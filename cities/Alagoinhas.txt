'''Alagoinhas''' is a city in the [[Brazilian state]] of [[Bahia]]. It is located at around {{Coord|12|8|9|S|38|25|8|W|region:BR_type:city(139723)}}. It was founded in 1852. In 1974, the city was made the seat of the [[Roman Catholic Diocese of Alagoinhas]].

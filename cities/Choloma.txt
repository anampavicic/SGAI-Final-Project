'''Choloma''' is a city and municipality in the Honduran [[Departments of Honduras|department]] of [[Cortés department|Cortés]]. It is the third-largest city in [[Honduras]] with a population of 220,300 (2023 calculation).<ref>[https://www.citypopulation.de/en/honduras/cities/ Citypopulation.de] Population of the major cities in Honduras</ref>
Many factories are stationed in Choloma due to its cheap labour which has boosted the municipality's economy.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.quesomahon-dalrit.com/the-major-attractions-of-choloma/|title=The Major Attractions Of Choloma|last=queso17|date=2019-02-22|website=The Queso Traveler from south Texas|language=en-US|access-date=2019-04-05}}</ref> Choloma is just south of [[Puerto Cortes]] and north of [[San Pedro Sula]], with transportation to other municipalities provided by private bus companies. Many transnational companies are stationed here for production as well as in various tax-free zones around the country.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://micholoma.com/our-hotel.html|title=History|website=Mi Choloma|language=en|access-date=2019-04-05}}</ref>

==Sports==
[[Atlético Choloma]] is the professional football club from Choloma, who have played in the [[Liga Nacional de Fútbol Profesional de Honduras|Honduran national football league]] from 2011 through 2013. They play their home games at the [[Estadio Rubén Deras]].

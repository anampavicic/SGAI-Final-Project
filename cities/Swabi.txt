'''Swabi''' ({{langx|ps|صوابۍ}}; {{langx|ur|{{nq|صوابی}}}}) is a city in the [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]] province of Pakistan.<ref name="NRB">{{cite web|url=http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=63&dn=Swabi|title=Tehsils & Unions in the District of Swabi|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120209043517/http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=63&dn=Swabi |archive-date=2012-02-09 }}</ref> It is located near the bank of the [[Indus River]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/03/Swabi.html|title=redirect to /world/PK/03/Swabi.html|website=www.fallingrain.com}}</ref> It is the [[List of most populous cities in Pakistan|73rd largest city of Pakistan]] and [[List of cities in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa by population|eighth largest in the province]] in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.{{Citation needed|date=August 2023}} Swabi is also a major city in the [[Mardan Division]], where it is the second-largest city.{{Citation needed|date=August 2023}}

According to the [[2023 Census of Pakistan]], the city of Swabi had 1,894,600 inhabitants, making it the [[List of cities in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa by population|eighth-largest]] city in [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]]. These inhabitants were distributed across 16,212 households, for an average household size of 7.61 in Swabi.<ref name="SwabiBlockwise">{{cite web |title=POPULATION AND HOUSEHOLD DETAIL FROM BLOCK TO DISTRICT LEVEL KHYBER PAKHTUNKHWA (SWABI DISTRICT) |url=http://www.pbs.gov.pk/sites/default/files/bwpsr/kp/SWABI_BLOCKWISE.pdf |website=SWABI_BLOCKWISE.pdf |publisher=Pakistan Bureau of Statistics |access-date=23 June 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200623111254/http://www.pbs.gov.pk/sites/default/files/bwpsr/kp/SWABI_BLOCKWISE.pdf |archive-date=23 June 2020 |date=3 January 2018}}</ref> Swabi experienced quick growth throughout the latter half of the twentieth century, with its population nearly quintupling over a mere 40 years. However, this growth trajectory has gradually decelerated over time. Between 1998 and 2017, the city's population exhibited an increase of only 2.29% annually.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pbs.gov.pk/census-2017-district-wise/results/015|title=Pakistan Bureau of Statistics|website=www.pbs.gov.pk}}</ref> The residents historically are of the [[Yusufzai]] tribe of [[Pashtuns]] which are predominant in the area.

== Climate ==
Swabi has a warm and temperate climate. With hot and humid summers and mild winters, Swabi features a [[humid subtropical climate]] ([[Köppen climate classification|Köppen]] ''Cwa''). The average temperature in Swabi is 22.2&nbsp;°C, while the annual precipitation averages 639&nbsp;mm. November is the driest month with an average rainfall of 12&nbsp;mm, while August is the wettest month with an average of 137&nbsp;mm of precipitation.

June is the hottest month of the year, with an average temperature of 32.9&nbsp;°C. January ''is'' the coldest month, with an average temperature of 10.2&nbsp;°C.

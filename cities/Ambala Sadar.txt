'''Ambala Sadar''' is a city and a [[municipal council]] in [[Ambala district]]  in the state of [[Haryana]], [[India]].<ref name="2011 Census">{{cite web |title=Ambala Sadar (M Cl) |url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/pca/SearchDetails.aspx?Id=64175 |website=[[2011 Census of India]] |publisher=[[Government of India]] |accessdate=19 September 2017 |archiveurl=https://archive.today/20170919165504/http://www.censusindia.gov.in/pca/SearchDetails.aspx?Id=64175 |archivedate=19 September 2017 |url-status=live |df=dmy-all }}</ref>

==Demographics==
{{As of|2001}} India [[census]], Ambala Sadar had a population of 106,378. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Ambala Sadar has an average literacy rate of 77%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 54% of the males and 46% of females literate. 11% of the population is under 6 years of age.

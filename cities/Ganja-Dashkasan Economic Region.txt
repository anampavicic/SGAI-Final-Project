'''Ganja-Dashkasan Economic Region''' ({{langx|az|Gəncə-Daşkəsən iqtisadi rayonu}}) is one of the 14 [[Economic regions of Azerbaijan|economic regions]] of [[Azerbaijan]]. It borders [[Georgia (country)|Georgia]] to the north and [[Armenia]] to the south, as well as the economic regions of [[Shaki-Zagatala Economic Region|Shaki-Zagatala]], [[Central Aran Economic Region|Central Aran]], [[Karabakh Economic Region|Karabakh]], [[East Zangezur Economic Region|East Zangezur]], and [[Gazakh-Tovuz Economic Region|Gazakh-Tovuz]]. The region consists of the districts of [[Dashkasan District|Dashkasan]], [[Goranboy District|Goranboy]], [[Goygol District|Goygol]], [[Samukh District|Samukh]], as well as the cities of [[Ganja, Azerbaijan|Ganja]] and [[Naftalan, Azerbaijan|Naftalan]].<ref>{{cite web |title=New economic regions of Azerbaijan – LIST |url=https://report.az/en/domestic-politics/new-economic-regions-of-azerbaijan-list/ |website=Report News Agency |date=7 July 2021 |access-date=24 April 2022 }}</ref> It has an area of {{convert|5,270|km2|sqmi}}. Its population was estimated to be at 611.3 thousand people in January 2021.<ref name="statgov" />

== History ==
Ganja-Dashkasan Economic Region was established on 7 July 2021 as part of a reform of the economic region system of Azerbaijan. Its territory was part of the larger [[Ganja-Qazakh Economic Region]] before 2021.<ref>{{cite web |title=Azərbaycan Respublikasında iqtisadi rayonların yeni bölgüsü haqqında Azərbaycan Respublikası Prezidentinin Fərmanı |url=https://president.az/articles/52389 |website=president.az |language=az}}</ref>

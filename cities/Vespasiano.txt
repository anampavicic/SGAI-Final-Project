'''Vespasiano''' is a municipality in the [[Metropolitana de Belo Horizonte (mesoregion)|Belo Horizonte metropolitan region]] in the Brazilian state of [[Minas Gerais]], located {{convert|27|km|mi|0}} north of [[Belo Horizonte]].

Vespasiano is home to [[Cidade do Galo]], the training grounds of [[Campeonato Brasileiro Série A]] team [[Clube Atlético Mineiro|Atlético Mineiro]]. FASEH, a higher learning institution, is also located in the city.

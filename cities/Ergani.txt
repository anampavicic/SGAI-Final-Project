'''Ergani''' ({{langx|ota|عثمانيه|translit=Osmaniye}},<ref>Tahir Sezen, ''Osmanlı Yer Adları (Alfabetik Sırayla)'', T.C. Başbakanlık Devlet Arşivleri Genel Müdürlüğü, Yayın Nu 21, Ankara, p. 393.</ref> {{langx|ku|Erxenî}}<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://tirsik.net/danegeh/pirtuk/ismail%20bulbul/anamneza%20bi%20kurmancî.pdf|title=Kürtçe Anamnez, Anamneza bi Kurmancî|editor-last=Avcýkýran|editor-first=Dr. Adem|website=Tirsik|page=55|access-date=17 December 2019}}</ref>), formerly known as Arghni or Arghana, is a municipality and [[Districts of Turkey|district]] of [[Diyarbakır Province]], [[Turkey]].<ref>[https://www.e-icisleri.gov.tr/Anasayfa/MulkiIdariBolumleri.aspx Büyükşehir İlçe Belediyesi], Turkey Civil Administration Departments Inventory. Retrieved 12 July 2023.</ref> Its area is 1,510&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.harita.gov.tr/uploads/files-folder/il_ilce_alanlari.xlsx|title=İl ve İlçe Yüz ölçümleri|publisher=General Directorate of Mapping|access-date=12 July 2023}}</ref> and its population is 136,099 (2022).<ref name=tuik>{{Cite web |title=Address-based population registration system (ADNKS) results dated 31 December 2022, Favorite Reports|url=https://biruni.tuik.gov.tr/medas/?kn=95&locale=en |access-date=12 July 2023|publisher=[[TÜİK]]|language=en|format=XLS}}</ref> Ergani District is located in the administrative as the Southeastern Anatolia Region, but as a geographical region it is located in Eastern Anatolia. The mayor is Ahmet Kaya ([[Peoples' Democratic Party (Turkey)|HDP]]) since 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://secim.haberler.com/2019/yerel-secimler/diyarbakir-ergani-secim-sonuclari/|title=Diyarbakır Ergani Seçim Sonuçları - 31 Mart Diyarbakır Ergani Yerel Seçim Sonuçları|website=secim.haberler.com|language=tr-TR|access-date=2019-11-08}}</ref> Ahmet Karaaslan was appointed as the [[Kaymakam|District Governor]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ergani.gov.tr/kaymakamimiz-sayin-ahmet-karaaslan|title=Kaymakamımız Sayın Ahmet Karaaslan|publisher=Ergani Kaymakamlığı|access-date=28 August 2023}}</ref>

==Çayönü==
Near the village of Sesverenpınar, among the Hilar rocks [[Çayönü]] hill was first settled in c. 7500 BC, and was continuously inhabited until 5000 BC (but only occasionally since then). Remains have been found from the earlier habitation, one of the most important archaeological finds of its era.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tf.uni-kiel.de/matwis/amat/iss/kap_a/advanced/ta_1_2c.html|title=Cayönü Tepesi|website=www.tf.uni-kiel.de|access-date=2019-11-08}}</ref> 

==History==
Trade has flourished between Ergani and [[Mesopotamia]] during the third millennium BC. Ergani has a rich history of being a mining region.<ref>{{Cite book|title=The Socio-Economic Organisation of the Urartian Kingdom|last=Çiftçi|first=Ali|publisher=Brill|year=2017|isbn=9789004347588|pages=122}}</ref> 

== Education ==
There is 107 elementary schools, and 3 Anatolian high schools in Ergani. There is also a Dicle University Vocational School in Ergani.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.dicle.edu.tr/en/academic/vocational-high-schools/ergani-vocational-high-school/index|title=Ergani Vocational High School Dicle University|website=www.dicle.edu.tr|access-date=2019-11-10}}</ref>

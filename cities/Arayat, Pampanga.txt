'''Arayat''', officially the '''Municipality of Arayat''' ({{lang-pam|Balen ning Arayat}}; {{lang-tl|Bayan ng Arayat}}), is a 1st class [[municipality of the Philippines|municipality]] in the [[Philippine Province|province]] of [[Pampanga]] in the [[Philippines]]. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 144,875 people.{{PH census|current}}

==Geography==
Arayat is bordered with [[Candaba, Pampanga|Candaba]], [[Mexico, Pampanga|Mexico]], [[Magalang, Pampanga|Magalang]], [[Santa Ana, Pampanga|Santa Ana]] and [[Cabiao]] in [[Nueva Ecija]]. A large portion of [[Mount Arayat]] is located within this municipality.

Arayat is {{convert|22|km}} from [[San Fernando, Pampanga|San Fernando]] and {{convert|88|km}} from [[Manila]].

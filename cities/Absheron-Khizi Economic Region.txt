'''Absheron-Khizi Economic Region''' ({{langx|az|Abşeron-Xızı iqtisadi rayonu}}) is one of the 14 [[Economic regions of Azerbaijan|economic regions]] of [[Azerbaijan]]. It borders the economic regions of [[Shirvan-Salyan Economic Region|Shirvan-Salyan]], [[Mountainous Shirvan Economic Region|Mountainous Shirvan]], [[Guba-Khachmaz Economic Region|Guba-Khachmaz]], and [[Baku Economic Region|Baku]]. The region consists of the districts of [[Absheron District|Absheron]], [[Khizi District|Khizi]] and the city of [[Sumgait]].<ref>{{cite web |title=New economic regions of Azerbaijan – LIST |url=https://report.az/en/domestic-politics/new-economic-regions-of-azerbaijan-list/ |website=Report News Agency |date=7 July 2021 |access-date=24 April 2022 }}</ref> It has an area of {{convert|3730|km2|sqmi}}. Its population was estimated to be at 578.8 thousand people in January 2021.<ref name="statgov" />

== History ==
Absheron-Khizi Economic Region was established on 7 July 2021 as part of a reform of the economic region system of Azerbaijan. Its territory was part of the larger [[Absheron Economic Region]] prior to 2021.<ref>{{cite web |title=Azərbaycan Respublikasında iqtisadi rayonların yeni bölgüsü haqqında Azərbaycan Respublikası Prezidentinin Fərmanı |url=https://president.az/articles/52389 |website=president.az |language=az}}</ref>

'''Kasaï-Central''' is one of the 21 [[provinces of the Democratic Republic of the Congo]] created in the [[Subdivisions of the DR Congo#New provinces|2015 repartitioning]].  Kasaï-Central and [[Kasaï Province|Kasaï]] provinces are the result of the dismemberment of the former [[Kasaï-Occidental]] province.<ref>{{cite news |title=RDC : le Kasaï-Occidental démembré |url=https://www.radiookapi.net/actualite/2015/07/18/rdc-le-kasai-occidental-demembre |access-date=2 June 2020 |work=[[Radio Okapi]] |date=18 July 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150813105351/http://radiookapi.net/actualite/2015/07/18/rdc-le-kasai-occidental-demembre/ |archive-date=13 Aug 2015 |url-status=live |language=fr}}</ref>  Kasaï-Central was formed from the [[Lulua District|Lulua district]] and the independently administered city of [[Kananga]] which retained its status as a provincial capital. The 2020 population was estimated to be 4,045,300.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Congo (Dem. Rep.): Provinces, Major Cities & Towns - Population Statistics, Maps, Charts, Weather and Web Information |url=https://www.citypopulation.de/en/drcongo/cities/ |access-date=2024-02-11 |website=www.citypopulation.de |archive-date=2022-06-26 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220626152516/https://www.citypopulation.de/en/drcongo/cities/ |url-status=live }}</ref>

The new province's territory corresponds to the historic [[Luluabourg Province]] which existed in the early period after [[History of the Democratic Republic of the Congo#Independence and the Congo Crisis (1960–65)|independence]] between 1963 and 1966.

'''Nevinnomyssk''' ({{langx|ru|Невинномысск}}) is a [[types of inhabited localities in Russia|city]] in [[Stavropol Krai]], [[Russia]], located on both banks of the [[Kuban River]] at its [[confluence]] with the [[Bolshoy Zelenchuk River]], {{convert|54|km|sp=us}} south of [[Stavropol]].

The only single-industry town in [[Stavropol Krai]]. Since December 22, 2017 year — the territory of advanced socio-economic development.

On October 29, 2020, the Duma of the Stavropol territory decided to award it the honorary title of City of military valor.<ref>{{Cite web|language=ru-ru|url=http://www.dumask.ru/info/smipublic/sobytiya/item/24422-gorodam-nevinnomyssku-i-pyatigorsku-prisvoeny-pochetnye-zvaniya.html|website=www.dumask.ru|access-date=2020-10-29|title=Дума Ставропольского края - Городам Невинномысску и Пятигорску присвоены почетные звания}}</ref>

On December 14, 2019, residents of the city on the bridge set a [[world record]] for "The Largest number of people simultaneously standing in the plank in the world". 4,573 people participated in the 1-minute [[Plank (exercise)|plank]].<ref>{{cite web |url = https://knigarekordovrossii.ru/rekordy/1831-naibolshee-kolichestvo-chelovek-odnovremenno-stoyashhih-v-planke-v-mire |title = Наибольшее количество человек, одновременно стоящих в планке в мире |language = ru |website = knigarekordovrossii.ru |access-date = 2019-12-23 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = https://stv24.tv/novosti/mirovoj-rekord-po-planke-ustanovili-v-nevinnomysske/ |title = Мировой рекорд по планке установили в Невинномысске |website = stv24.tv |date = December 14, 2019 |access-date = 2019-12-23 }}</ref>

==History==
Nevinnomyssk was founded in 1825 as a ''[[stanitsa]]'' near a small fort. It was granted town status in 1939.{{citation needed|date=June 2010}} Nevinnomyssk was occupied by the Germans from August 1942-January/February 1943 in Operation Case Blue, connected to Operation Edelweiss.

==Administrative and municipal status==
Within the [[subdivisions of Russia#Administrative divisions|framework of administrative divisions]], it is incorporated as the '''[[city of federal subject significance|city of krai significance]] of Nevinnomyssk'''— an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the [[administrative divisions of Stavropol Krai|districts]].<ref name="Ref372">Resolution #63-p</ref> As a [[subdivisions of Russia#Municipal divisions|municipal division]], the city of krai significance of Nevinnomyssk is incorporated as '''Nevinnomyssk Urban Okrug'''.<ref name="Ref893">Law #88-kz</ref>

== Modern technology ==
Nevinnomyssk is actively implementing the [[Smart city|Smart city system]]. To date, several modules from the "smart city"  system are successfully operating in the city. Among them-the program complex "Urbanix", which provides the cartographic basis of Nevinnomyssk, as well as "smart lighting", created under the energy service contract, and the video surveillance system "Safe city".<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://nevadm.ru/news/media/2020/7/9/v-nevinnomyisske-prodolzhayut-vnedryat-sistemu-umnyij-gorod/|title=В Невинномысске продолжают внедрять систему "Умный город"}}</ref>

There are plans to install "smart stops" and a number of other innovations.

Also on the outskirts of Nevinnomyssk, a huge [[Wind power|wind Park]] started operating.

This is 84 wind power plants generating 2.5 MW each. Thus, the total capacity of the wind farm is 210 MW. Similar modern technologies are used in [[EU]] countries.

== Transport ==
[[File:DJI 0013 MP4-0-00-30-731.jpg|thumb|left|260px|
New overpass was built]]
Nevinnomyssk railway station accepts commuter and long-distance passenger trains. The city moved modern buses and Marcucci. Public transport costs 28 rubles.

In 2019 in Nevinnomyssk [[:ru:Путепровод в ТОСЭР «Невинномысск»|a new overpass was built]], the length of the bridge is 1.1 kilometers, and the entire interchange reaches 3 kilometers. The overpass has four lanes for vehicles and two sidewalks, and it crosses five railway tracks, the largest overpass in the Stavropol territory. The overpass was built across the Nevinnomyssk-Pyatigorsk and Nevinnomyssk-Moscow railway, in the area of Stepnaya and Revolyutsionnaya streets. The opening took place on December 27, 2019.

== Status of the territory of advanced development ==
Nevinnomyssk is a single-industry municipality of the Russian Federation with a stable socio-economic situation.

22 December 2017, in accordance with the Federal law "On territories of advancing socio-economic development of the Russian Federation", the Russian Government decided to establish within the company towns of the territory of advancing socio-economic development "Nevinnomyssk" with the aim of diversifying the economy, reducing dependence on core enterprises, increase of investment attractiveness of the city, the creation of new jobs, attracting investment.<ref>http://static.government.ru/media/files/LJI1o1TfVFQXorNLKncADzG4NILnW7RA.pdf {{Bare URL PDF|date=March 2022}}</ref>

At the end of 2018, nevinnomisk monotown entered the Top 10 leaders of the annual rating of monotowns. The rating comprehensively assesses the activity and efficiency of local governments, the level of development of SMEs, the urban economy and the urban environment. The annual rating of single-industry towns was formed for the first time as part of the implementation of the program "Integrated development of single-industry towns" (2016–2018).

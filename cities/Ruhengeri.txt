'''Ruhengeri''', also known as '''Musanze''' or '''Muhoza,''' is the third largest city in [[Rwanda]] and the capital of [[Musanze District]] in the [[Northern Province, Rwanda|Northern Province]] of Rwanda. The city has a population of 153,368 as of the 2022 census.<ref name="pop"/>

== Name ==
Some sources now refer to the city itself as Musanze, after the district in which it lies within.<ref>{{cite book |last1=Briggs |first1=Philip |last2=Booth |first2=Janet |title=Rwanda |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=NVTLZ41XyX8C |accessdate=2010-06-14 |year=2010 |publisher=Bradt Travel Guides |isbn=1-84162-306-7 |page=22 }}</ref> This has to do with the adopted policy of renaming Rwanda cities, which was done to eliminate memories of the past and to install new administrations in the country.{{Citation needed|date=December 2020}}
[[File:The streets of Ruhengeri (8209006497).jpg|thumb|Photo of Ruhengeri in 2012 with volcanoes visible in the background]]

== Geography ==
Ruhengeri lies near the twin lakes of [[Lake Burera]] and [[Lake Ruhondo]] and is the gateway city to [[Volcanoes National Park]] and the famous [[mountain gorilla]]s in the north-western part of the country. 

The city's proximity to the [[Volcanoes National Park]] has made it a popular tourist destination with several restaurants and hotels mainly aimed at visitors to the national park.

== Notable institutions ==
Ruhengeri is also home to the [[Institute of Applied Sciences Ruhengeri|Institute of Applied Sciences]] (INES-Ruhengeri).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ines.ac.rw/|title=INES-Ruhengeri|website=INES-Ruhengeri|language=en-US|access-date=2019-06-02}}</ref> INES-Ruhengeri was established in 2003 in collaboration with the  Catholic Diocese of Ruhengeri.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Educational & Research Institutions |url=https://www.statistics.gov.rw/about-us/partnerships/educational-research-institutions |access-date=February 12, 2023 |website=NISR}}</ref>

The city is served by the [[Ruhengeri Airport]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=RHG - Ruhengeri [Ruhengeri Airport], 03, RW - Airport - Great Circle Mapper |url=http://www.gcmap.com/airport/HRYU |access-date=2018-08-21 |website=www.gcmap.com}}</ref>

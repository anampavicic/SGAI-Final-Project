'''Berrechid''' ({{langx|ar|برشيد}}) is a town and [[List of municipalities, communes, and arrondissements of Morocco|municipality]] in [[Berrechid Province]] of the [[Casablanca-Settat]] region of [[Morocco]]. It recorded a population of 136,634 inhabitants in the [[2014 Moroccan census]].<ref name=census2014/> At the time of the [[2004 Moroccan census|2004 census]], the commune had a total population of 89,830 people living in 18,808 households.<ref name="Pop">{{cite web |url=http://www.lavieeco.com/documents_officiels/Recensement%20population.pdf |title=Recensement général de la population et de l'habitat de 2004 |publisher=Haut-commissariat au Plan, Lavieeco.com |accessdate=21 June 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120724191316/http://www.lavieeco.com/documents_officiels/Recensement%20population.pdf |archivedate=24 July 2012 }}</ref>

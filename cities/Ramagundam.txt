'''Ramagundam''' is a city under  [[Ramagundam Municipal Corporation|municipal corporation]] in [[Peddapalli district]] of the [[States and union territories of India|Indian state]] of [[Telangana]]. It is the most populous city in the district and falls under the Ramagundam [[revenue division]].<ref name="census">{{cite web|title=District Census Handbook – Adilabad|url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/dchb/2803_PART_B_DCHB_KARIMNAGAR.pdf|website=Census of India|access-date=19 September 2015|page=12}}</ref> It is located on the banks of the [[Godavari River]]. As of the 2011 [[Census of India|census]], the city had a population of 229,644, making it the [[List of cities in Telangana by population|6th most populous]] in the state, and an [[urban agglomeration]] population of 242,979.<ref name="profile" /> It is located about 225&nbsp;km from the state capital [[Hyderabad]].

== Etymology ==
The city's name derives from a combination of two words, ''Rama'' and ''Gundam''. A famous temple of the [[Hindu]] god [[Rama]] is situated in the old part of the city, where as ''Gundam'' means "water springs" are present.

== Geography ==
Ramagundam is located at {{Coord|18.7638|N|79.4750|E|}}.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/02/Ramgundam.html|title=redirect to /world/IN/02/Ramgundam.html}}</ref> It has an average elevation of 179&nbsp;meters (590&nbsp;feet). It is about 225&nbsp;kilometres (140&nbsp;mi) northeast of the state capital, [[Hyderabad]], 65&nbsp;km from [[Karimnagar]], 0&nbsp;km from [[Peddapalli district|Ramagundam district]] headquarters and 0.1&nbsp;km from [[Godavarikhani]].

== Climate ==
Ramagundam experiences dry inland climatic conditions with hot summers and cool winters. The city of Ramagundam gets most of its rainfall from the Southwest [[monsoon]]. The summer season is extremely hot, but temperatures decline with the onset of the monsoons, and the winter season is generally cool. The most popular season for tourism is from November to February.

The summer season starts in March and can continue through early June. During this period temperatures range from a minimum of 25&nbsp;°C to a maximum of 40&nbsp;°C. The highest recorded temperature in the area is around 47.3&nbsp;°C.<ref name="weather">{{cite web|url=http://www.imd.gov.in/section/climate/extreme/ramagundam2.htm|title=CLIMATOLOGICAL TABLE PERIOD: 1981–2010 |access-date=16 June 2016 |publisher=Indian Meteorological Department}}</ref> Nights are much cooler, and the humidity is around 50%. The city experiences the most rainfall in July and August, from the Southwest monsoon, with the highest rainfall being 112mm and an average annual rainfall of 1129.2mm. During this time, daytime temperatures average around 32&nbsp;°C. There is additional rainfall in September and October due to Northeast rainfall. The winter season starts in November and lasts through February. During this time, temperatures range from a minimum of 14&nbsp;°C to a maximum of 32&nbsp;°C.

== Government and politics ==
[[File:Ramagundam Municipal Corporation.jpg|thumb|[[Ramagundam Municipal Corporation]]]]
The [[Ramagundam Municipal Corporation]] is the [[Local government|local governing body]], administering the city. The [[municipal corporation]] consists of democratically elected members, is headed by a [[mayor]] and administers the city's governance, infrastructure and administration. This city is selected under central government scheme of [[AMRUT]].<ref>{{cite web|title=List of cities covered by AMRUT|url=http://amrut.gov.in/list_of_cities.aspx|publisher=Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation(AMRUT)|access-date=8 July 2018}}</ref>

=== Law and order ===
[[Ramagundam Police Commissionerate]] is a city [[police force]] with primary responsibilities in law enforcement and investigation within districts of [[Peddapalli district|Ramagundam]], and [[Mancherial]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/telangana/minister-reviews-arrangements-for-police-commissionerates/article9207123.ece|title=Minister reviews arrangements for police commissionerates|work=The Hindu|access-date=19 June 2017|language=en}}</ref>

=== Politics ===
Makkan singh Raj Thakur is elected as MLA for Ramagundam constituency from Indian national congress in the elections held in December 2023.Korukanti Chandar is MLA from Ramagundam constituency who contested as an independent candidate in Telangana general assembly elections held in the year 2018 and later he joined ruling party Telangana Rashtra Samithi [TRS]. Somarapu Satyanarayana, who won as MLA for consecutive two times in a row in 2009 and 2014 elections from Ramagundam constituency, has been appointed as Telangana's first [[TSRTC]] Public Transport chairman in 2016.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.metroindia.com/cities/article/29/04/2016/somarapu-to-take-charge-as-rtc-chief-today/30560|title=Somarapu to take charge as RTC chief today|work=Metro India News}}</ref>

== Economy ==

Ramagundam city is situated in the [[Godavari]] valley coalfields and has one of the India's largest [[thermal power station]]s in south region under [[NTPC Ramagundam|NTPC]].  

Mostly industries are connected with [[Godavarikhani]]- [[NTPC Ramagundam]]. Some of them are [[Singareni Collieries Company|Singareni]], [[Fertilizer Corporation of India]], Kesoram Cement, Basanth Nagar, [[National Fertilizers]] Limited, [[Engineers India]] Limited, [[TSGENCO]] thermal power station, Kesoram (Birla) cement factories, NTPC (RSTPS – Ramagundam Super Thermal Power Station) sourcing 2600 MW of power 24/7 and the TSSEB (Telangana State Electricity Board) unit at Ramagundam. Another 1600 MW (2x800 MW) is proposed as RDM-II as per expansion plan by NTPC Limited. NTPC's first SPV or Solar photovoltaic unit of capacity 25 MW (Phase-I 1x10 MW, Phase-II 1x15 MW) has been proposed at Ramagundam. This would help in earning carbon credits against use of solar power as renewable energy source. 

Also, [[Sripada Yellampalli project]], an irrigation project, is located near Ramagundam. Singareni's first Solar Power Plant of capacity 50 MW has been proposed at Ramagundam.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thehansindia.com/posts/index/Telangana/2017-10-14/Setting-up-of-solar-plant-in-Ramagundam-reviewed/333034|title=Setting up Solar Plant in Ramagundam reviewed|work=The Hans India|access-date=13 October 2017|language=en}}</ref>

[[Telangana Super Thermal Power Project]] was inaugurated in 2023 in the town.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://thesouthfirst.com/telangana/prime-minister-modi-to-dedicate-to-nation-development-projects-worth-₹8000-crore-at-nizamabad-on-3-oct/ | title=Prime Minister Modi to dedicate to nation development projects worth ₹8,000 crore at Nizamabad on 3 Oct | date=2 October 2023 }}</ref>

== Transport ==
[[file: Ramagundam railway station.jpg|thumb|Ramagundam railway station]]
=== Road ===
[[State Highway 1 (Telangana)]] is connected with [[Hyderabad]]-[[Karimnagar]]-Ramagundam [[Godavarikhani]] Highway. [[TSRTC]] [[Godavarikhani]] and [[MSRTC]](ST) operates buses and connects Ramagundam (Godavarikhani) to major parts of the state.<ref>{{cite news|title=City bus services flagged off in Karimnagar|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/telangana/city-bus-services-flagged-off-in-karimnagar/article8095231.ece|access-date=30 June 2016|work=The Hindu|date=12 January 2016|language=en-IN}}</ref> [[Godavarikhani]] depot runs City buses in Ramagundam Corporation limits.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.worldtvnews.co.in/?p=53463|title=Transport Minister P. Mahender Reddy and Finance Minister Etala Rajender flagging off JNNURM City bus service, in Godavarikhani coal belt area in Karimnagar – worldtvnews|date=26 June 2016}}</ref> This city is directly connected by road to capital city Hyderabad by [[State Highway 1 (Telangana)|SH 1]] which starts from this city. [[National Highway 63 (India)|National Highway 63]] is close by.

=== Rail ===
[[Ramagundam railway station]] falls under the jurisdiction of [[Secunderabad railway division]] of [[South Central Railway zone]] is B category station.<ref>{{cite web|title=Jurisdiction Map of Secunderabad Division|url=http://www.scr.indianrailways.gov.in/view_section.jsp?lang=0&id=0,1,291,357,806|website=South Central Railway|access-date=30 June 2016}}</ref>

=== Air ===
[[Ramagundam Airport]] is the airport situated in Basanthnagar. It is not functional. It is located in centre of 4 districts so state government proposed to develop this airport. Central government plans to run flight services to this city under [[UDAN|UDAN plan]]. The nearest airport is [[Rajiv Gandhi International Airport]] in Hyderabad which is around 270 km from Ramagundam.

'''Arifwala'''  ([[Punjabi language|Punjabi]] and {{langx|ur|{{Nastaliq|عارفوالا}}}}) is a city and headquarters of [[Arif Wala Tehsil]] of [[Pakpattan District]] in the [[Punjab, Pakistan|Punjab]] province of [[Pakistan]].<ref name="NRB">{{cite web|url=http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=25&dn=Pakpattan|title=Tehsils & Unions in the District of Pakpattan|publisher=Government of Pakistan website|accessdate=17 November 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120209043343/http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=25&dn=Pakpattan|archive-date=9 February 2012|url-status=dead}}</ref>

It is the [[List of largest cities in Pakistan|69th largest city in Pakistan]].
Arifwala is located to the southwest of [[Pakpattan]].

==Demographics==
The population of city in 1998 was 74,174 but according to the [[2017 Census of Pakistan]], the population has risen to 111,403 with a growth of about 50.19% in 19 years.<ref>[https://www.citypopulation.de/en/pakistan/distr/admin/ All Pakistan Districts and Tehsils (scroll down to Pakpattan District for Arif Wala)] Citypopulation.de website, Retrieved 17 November 2021</ref>

== Road projects ==
The Provincial Development Working Party approved a scheme to rehabilitate a 17&nbsp;km stretch of road from Bahawalnagar to Arifwala for Rs631.246 million in its 7th meeting. The PDWP, chaired by Abdullah Khan Sumbal, also approved seven other road schemes worth a total of Rs9 billion.<ref>{{Cite news|date=2022-08-31 |title=Rs9bn road projects approved for Punjab |url=https://www.dawn.com/news/1707605 |access-date=2023-03-15 |newspaper=Dawn newspaper |language=en}}</ref>

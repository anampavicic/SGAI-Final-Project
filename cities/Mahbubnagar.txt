'''Mahbubnagar''', formerly known as '''Rukmammapeta''' and '''Palamooru'''<ref>{{Cite web |title=About Mahbubnagar District {{!}} Mahbubnagar District,Telangana {{!}} India |url=https://mahbubnagar.telangana.gov.in/about-district-2-2/ |access-date=2023-06-21 |language=en-US}}</ref> is a city<ref>{{cite web |title=Cities having population 1 lakh and above, Census 2011 |url=http://censusindia.gov.in/2011-prov-results/paper2/data_files/India2/Table_2_PR_Cities_1Lakh_and_Above.pdf }}{{dead link|date=April 2024}}</ref>{{Dead link|date=April 2024}} in [[Mahbubnagar District]] of the [[Indian state]] of [[Telangana]] named after the [[6th Nizam]] of [[Hyderabad State|Hyderabad]], [[Mahboob Ali Khan]]. It is the headquarters of Mahbubnagar [[Tehsil|mandal]] in Mahbubnagar revenue division.<ref name=census>{{cite web |title=District Census Handbook – Mahbubnagar |url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/dchb/2807_PART_B_DCHB_MAHBUB NAGAR.pdf |website=Census of India |access-date=25 December 2015 |pages=12–13}}</ref> The city is also the largest in the district with an area of {{convert|39.64|km2|abbr=on}} and [[List of cities in Telangana by population|7th most populous]] in the state.<ref name=info>{{cite web |title=Mahbubnagar Municipality |url=http://www.cdma.telangana.gov.in/MAHBUBNAGAR/ |website=Municipal Administration & Urban Development Department |access-date=26 December 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151125214418/http://www.cdma.telangana.gov.in/MAHBUBNAGAR/ |archive-date=25 November 2015}}</ref>

== Geography ==
Mahbubnagar is located at {{Coord|16.73|N|77.98|E|}}.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/02/MahbubNagar.html |title=redirect to /world/IN/02/Mahbubnagar.html |work=fallingrain.com}}</ref> It has an average elevation of 498&nbsp;metres (1633&nbsp;feet). The city of Mahbubnagar is located at a distance of 98&nbsp;km from [[Hyderabad, India|Hyderabad]], 130&nbsp;km from [[Kurnool]] and 151&nbsp;km from [[Gulbarga]].{{citation needed|date=February 2023}}

In 2011, Mahbubnagar had a population of 222,573<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/ |title=Census of India Website : Office of the Registrar General & Census Commissioner, India |website=www.censusindia.gov.in |access-date=2019-01-08}}</ref> It is the 9th largest city of south India by number of auto rickshaws, and 2nd in Telangana after Hyderabad.{{citation needed|date=February 2020}}

== Governance ==
''Mahbub Nagar municipality'' is the civic body of the city, which was constituted in 1942 as a ''third grade municipality''. It was upgraded to ''second grade'' in 1959, to ''first grade'' in 1983 and finally to ''Special grade municipality'' in 2004.<ref name=info /> It is spread over an area of {{Convert|98.64|km2|abbr=on}}.<ref>{{cite web |title=Basic Information of Municipality |url=http://cdma.telangana.gov.in/MAHABUBNAGAR/Basic_information_Municipality.html |website=Municipal Administration & Urban Development Department |publisher=Government of Telangana |access-date=24 December 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150617190326/http://cdma.telangana.gov.in/MAHABUBNAGAR/Basic_information_Municipality.html |archive-date=17 June 2015}}</ref> Mahbub Nagar urban agglomeration consists of Mahbub Nagar municipality, [[census towns]] of Boyapalle, Yenugonda and the [[out growth]]s of Mahbub Nagar (rural) (full), Yedira (part) village.<ref name=census />

== Transport ==
[[File:Mahabubnagar Railway Station.JPG|thumbnail|Mahabubnagar railway station]]
The city is connected to major cities and towns by means of road, railway and airway. [[National Highway (India)|National]] and [[state highways]] that pass through the city are [[NH 44]] and [[National Highway 167 (India)|NH 167]], State highway 20 and 23. [[TSRTC]] operates buses to various destinations from Mahbub Nagar bus station of the city.<ref>{{cite web |title=Bus Stations |url=http://www.tsrtc.telangana.gov.in/BusStations.php |website=[[TSRTC]] |access-date=20 May 2016}}</ref>

===Air===
[[Rajiv Gandhi International Airport]] is only 75&nbsp;km from the city.

===Railway===
The city has rail connectivity with the presence of [[Mahbubnagar railway station|MahbubNagar Main Station]], Mahbub Nagar Town, Yenugonda Station and Diviti Pally Stations.

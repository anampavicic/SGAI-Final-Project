'''Umuarama''' is a [[municipality]] in the state of [[Paraná (state)|Paraná]] in Brazil. Its population was 112,500 inhabitants in 2020.
Umuarama is one of the most important cities in [[Paraná (state)|Paraná]], one of the three states of southern [[Brazil]]. The city elevation is 430 m (1,300 feet) above sea level and the rainfall is about 1600mm/year (63&nbsp;inches/yr). Umuarama is known as "The Capital of Friendship".

==History==

The municipality was founded in 1955, and colonized by Companhia Melhoramentos Norte do Paraná, a company that settled an important number of cities in Northern [[Paraná (state)|Paraná]].

==Tourism==
There are a lot of places to visit in Umuarama, the most important are:
* Expo Umuarama
* Aratimbó Lake
* Uirapuru Park
* Park of Xetas (also known as Indian's Park)
* Streetmarkets
* The replica of Eiffel Tower at the Estância Paris 
* Tucuruvi Lake

Umuarama is served by [[Umuarama Airport|Orlando de Carvalho Airport]].

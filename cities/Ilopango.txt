'''Ilopango''' is a [[town]] and district in the [[San Salvador Department|San Salvador department]] of [[El Salvador]]. It is a few miles east of the nation's capital, [[San Salvador]] and part of the [[San Salvador metropolitan area]]. It is located near [[Lake Ilopango]], the country's largest lake at 72 square kilometers.

==Overview==
[[File:Ilopango_El_Salvador_calle_2011.jpg|thumb|250px|Street in Ilopango]]
El Salvador's [[Ilopango International Airport|second airport]] is in Ilopango. It is currently used for charter flights and by the Salvadoran military but plans are underway to increase its use as tourism and travel in El Salvador increase and [[Comalapa International Airport]] cannot handle the future influx alone.{{citation needed|date=April 2012}} The [[Civil Aviation Authority (El Salvador)|Civil Aviation Authority]] has its headquarters on the airport property,<ref>[http://www.aac.gob.sv Home].

'''Talkha''' ({{langx|ar|طلخا}}&nbsp; {{IPA|ar|ˈtˤɑlxɑ|pron}}) is a city in [[Dakahlia Governorate]], [[Egypt]]. 
The city is located on the west bank of the [[Damietta]],<ref name="Map on Talkha">{{cite web |title=Map of Talkha |url=http://www.dakahliya.gov.eg/DocLib/talkha.jpg |website=Dakahliya Governorate site}}</ref> a [[distributary]] of the [[Nile]] in the [[Nile Delta|Delta]] region. Is about 120&nbsp;km northeast of [[Cairo]]. Talkha is situated opposite the city of [[Mansoura, Egypt|Mansoura]] on the Damietta. Together, the two cities form an [[urban agglomeration|agglomeration]].

Talkha contains the neighborhoods of Old Market, El Maalamein, Ar Rouda, El Sharifa, and El Muhandisin.

'''Atsimo-Andrefana''' (South West) is a [[Regions of Madagascar|region]] of [[Madagascar]]. It borders [[Menabe]] in north, [[Amoron'i Mania]] and [[Haute Matsiatra]] in northeast, [[Ihorombe]] and [[Anosy]] in east and [[Androy]] in southeast. The capital is [[Toliara]] and the population was 1,799,088 in 2018.<ref name=recensement2018>{{cite web|url=https://www.instat.mg/wp-content/uploads/Rapport-Prelim-2019_ver_final.pdf|title=Troisieme Recensement General de la Population et de L'Habitation (RGPH-3) Resultats Provisoires|publisher=Institut National de la Statistique Madagascar|access-date=May 23, 2020|archive-date=June 24, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210624195743/https://www.instat.mg/wp-content/uploads/Rapport-Prelim-2019_ver_final.pdf|url-status=dead}}</ref> Atsimo Andrefana is geographically the largest of all Malagasy regions with an area of {{convert|66236|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on}}.<ref name="doc">{{cite web

==Administrative divisions==
Atsimo-Andrefana Region is divided into nine districts, which are sub-divided into 105 communes.

* [[Ampanihy District]] – 16 communes
* [[Ankazoabo District]] – 5 communes
* [[Benenitra District]] – 5 communes
* [[Betioky-Atsimo District|Betioky Sud District]] – 5 communes
* [[Beroroha District]] – 19 communes
* [[Morombe District]] – 8 communes
* [[Sakaraha District]] – 11 communes
* [[Toliara|Toliara I District]] – 1 commune
* [[Toliara II District]] – 19 communes

==Rivers==
Mayor river in Atsimo-Andrefana is the [[Onilahy River]] and in the north the [[Mangoky River]].
Also the [[Fiherenana River]] and [[Linta River]] flows in that region while the [[Menarandra River]] forms the border with region of [[Androy]] in the south.

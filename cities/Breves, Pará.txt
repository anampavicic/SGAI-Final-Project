'''Breves''' is a Brazilian municipality located in the state of [[Pará]], on the island of [[Marajó]]. Its population {{As of|2020|lc=y}} is estimated to be 103,497 people. The area of the municipality is 9,550.454&nbsp;km². The city belongs to the mesoregion [[Marajó]] and to the microregion of ''Furos de Breves''.

The municipality is contained in the {{convert|59985|km2}} [[Marajó Archipelago Environmental Protection Area]], a sustainable use conservation unit established in 1989 to protect the environment of the delta region.<ref>{{citation|ref={{harvid|APA Arquipélago do Marajó – ISA}}|language=pt
 |title=APA Arquipélago do Marajó|publisher=ISA: Instituto Socioambiental
 |url=https://uc.socioambiental.org/uc/951|access-date=2016-06-27}}</ref>
It contains the {{convert|93746|ha}} [[Mapuá Extractive Reserve]], a sustainable use conservation unit created in 2005.<ref>{{citation|ref={{harvid|RESEX Mapuá – ISA}}|language=pt
 |title=RESEX Mapuá|publisher=ISA: Instituto Socioambiental
 |url=https://uc.socioambiental.org/uc/4249|access-date=2016-06-30}}</ref>

The city is served by [[Breves Airport]].

== Climate ==
The climate is [[Tropical rainforest climate|tropical rainforest]] or [[Tropical rainforest climate|equatorial]] ([[Köppen climate classification|Köppen]]: ''Af'') with high rainfall and high temperature all year round.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://pt.climate-data.org/america-do-sul/brasil/para/breves-43947/|title=Clima Breves: Temperatura, Tempo e Dados climatológicos Breves - Climate-Data.org|website=pt.climate-data.org|access-date=2019-03-30}}</ref>

'''Singrauli''' is a city in [[Singrauli district]] in the [[Indian state]] of [[Madhya Pradesh]] and Commissionaire of Rewa. It lies about {{convert|26|km}} from the district headquarters of [[Waidhan]].

==Demographics==
{{update after|2022}}
As of the [[2011 Census of India]],<ref>{{cite web |url=https://censusindia.gov.in/pca/SearchDetails.aspx?Id=565416 |title=Census of India: Search Details - Singrauli|access-date= 11 November 2021}}</ref> Singrauli had a population of {{formatnum:220257}}, of which males were 53.1% and females were 46.9%. Child population (0-6) in urban region was 30,079. Average literacy rate in Singrauli was 75.51%.

==Administration==
Singrauli Municipal Corporation is the local body governing the city, and is the second richest municipal corporation in Madhya Pradesh.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Dwary |first1=Anurag |title=AAP Makes Madhya Pradesh Debut With Singrauli Mayoral Win |url=https://www.ndtv.com/india-news/aap-makes-madhya-pradesh-debut-with-singrauli-mayoral-win-3166398 |website=NDTV.com |access-date=16 August 2022}}</ref> Rani Agrawal is the mayor of the city.<ref name="July 2022">{{cite news |title=AAP Makes Madhya Pradesh Debut With Singrauli Mayoral Win |url=https://www.ndtv.com/india-news/aap-makes-madhya-pradesh-debut-with-singrauli-mayoral-win-3166398 |access-date=19 July 2022 |work=NDTV.com |date=17 July 2022}}</ref> Singrauli is in [[Rewa Division]].

==Politics ==
The city is part of the [[Singrauli Assembly constituency]].

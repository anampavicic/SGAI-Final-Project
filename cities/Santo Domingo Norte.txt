'''Santo Domingo Norte''' is a [[Municipalities of the Dominican Republic|municipality]] of the [[Santo Domingo Province]] in the [[Dominican Republic]]. There is one [[municipal district]] (''distrito municipal'') within the municipality: [[La Victoria, Dominican Republic|La Victoria]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.one.gob.do/index.php?option=com_docman&task=cat_view&gid=113 |title=Listado de Códigos de Provincias, Municipios y Distritos Municipales, Actualizada a Junio 20 del 2006 |author=Oficina Nacional de Estadística, Departamento de Cartografia, División de Límites y Linderos |language=es |access-date=2007-01-24 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070314215735/http://www.one.gob.do/index.php?option=com_docman&task=cat_view&gid=113 |archive-date=2007-03-14 }}</ref>

==Overview==
Santo Domingo Norte was created as a municipality in 2001 by Law 163-01 splitting the Santo Domingo province from the Distrito Nacional including those parts of metropolitan [[Santo Domingo]] north of the [[Isabela River]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.informejudicial.com/leyes/Division%20Territorial/Ley%20163-01,%20crea%20Provincia%20Santo%20Domingo%20y%20Modifica%20Articulos%201%20y%202%20Ley%205220.htm |title=Ley No. 163-01 que crea la provincia de Santo Domingo, y modifica los Artículos 1 y 2 de la Ley No. 5220, sobre División Territorial de la República Dominicana. |author=EL CONGRESO NACIONAL |language=es |access-date=2007-03-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070518042944/http://www.informejudicial.com/leyes/Division+Territorial/Ley+163-01,+crea+Provincia+Santo+Domingo+y+Modifica+Articulos+1+y+2+Ley+5220.htm |archive-date=2007-05-18 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=2019-10-16 |title=Municipio SDN, rico en historia, pero con un pobre desarrollo |url=https://eldia.com.do/municipio-sdn-rico-en-historia-pero-con-un-pobre-desarrollo/ |access-date=2024-04-21 |website=El Día |language=en}}</ref>

'''Nandyal''', commonly called as '''Nandyala''', is a city and the district headquarters of [[Nandyal district]] of the [[India]]n state of the [[Andhra Pradesh]]. It is a [[municipality]] and the headquarters of Nandyal [[mandal]] in [[Nandyal revenue division]].<ref name="census" /><ref>{{cite web|title=Kurnool District Mandals |url=http://censusindia.gov.in/2011census/maps/atlas/28part32.pdf|publisher=Census of India |access-date=19 January 2015|page=339|format=PDF}}</ref>

== Demographics ==
In the 2011 [[census of India]], Nandyal had a population of 211,424 making it the [[List of cities in Andhra Pradesh by population|13th]] most populous town in the state.<ref name="population" />

== Governance ==
=== Civic administration ===
Nandyal municipality was constituted in the year 1899. The jurisdictional area is spread over an area of {{Convert|19|km2|mi2|abbr=on}}.<ref name=municipality /> Its urban agglomeration is spread over an area of {{convert|33.67|km2|abbr=on}} which includes constituents of Nandyal municipality, [[out growth]]s of Moolasagaram, Noonepalle and partial outgrowths of Udumalpuram, Ayyalur.<ref name=census />

== Culture and tourism ==
[[File:Mahanandi aerial view.jpg|thumb|Mahanandi]]

Nandyal is surrounded by nine sacred temples known as the ''Nava Nandi''. ''Sri [[Yaganti]] Uma Maheswara Temple'' near Nandyal has one of the largest Nandi idols of the world. As per the [[Archaeological Survey of India]], the rock grows at a rate of {{Convert|1|in|cm}} per 20 years. Nandyal District also consists of [[Belum Caves]], [[Mahanandi]] and [[Srisailam]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://dsal.uchicago.edu/reference/gazetteer/pager.html?objectid=DS405.1.I34_V18_369.gif|title=Imperial Gazetteer2 of India, Volume 18, page 363 -- Imperial Gazetteer of India -- Digital South Asia Library|work=uchicago.edu}}</ref>

== Economy ==
The city is a major hub of industry and agriculture. It is rich in [[natural resources]] like [[marble]]. There are many [[rice mill]]s and [[oil mill]]s and [[Cotton mill]]s and industries which produce [[milk]], [[soft drinks]], [[sugar]], [[Poly-vinyl chloride|PVC pipes]], etc. Some of the renowned industries in the City are the Vijaya Dairy, Nandi Dairy, Nandi Pipes, Nandi Polymers, S. P. Y. Agro, Nandi Steels, etc.<ref>{{cite web|title=Brief Industrial Profile of Kurnool District |url=http://dcmsme.gov.in/dips/Kurnool%20dip%20%20amended%20march.pdf|website=Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises|publisher=Government of India|access-date=28 June 2015|pages=11–12}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Basic Information of Municipality|url=http://cdma.ap.gov.in/Nandyal/Basic_information_Municipality.html|website=Municipal Administration & Urban Development Department|publisher=Government of Andhra Pradesh|access-date=28 June 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141129034333/http://cdma.ap.gov.in/Nandyal/Basic_information_Municipality.html|archive-date=29 November 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>

== Transport ==
The [[Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation]] operates bus services from Nandyal bus station.<ref>{{cite web|title=Bus Stations in Districts |url=http://www.apsrtc.gov.in/Bus%20Stations.aspx |website=Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation |access-date=9 March 2016 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160322233330/http://apsrtc.gov.in/Bus%20Stations.aspx |archive-date=22 March 2016 }}</ref> 
{{rws|Nandyal}} Junction Railway Station is administered under [[Guntur railway division]] of [[South Central Railways]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Kolakalur railway station info|url=http://indiarailinfo.org/station/map/499?|publisher=India Rail Info|access-date=30 July 2014}}</ref>
[[Kurnool Airport]] is 50&nbsp;km from Nandyal.

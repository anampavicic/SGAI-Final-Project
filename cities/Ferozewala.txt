'''Firozwala''' ({{lang-pa|{{Nastaliq|فِيروزوالا}}}}), is a city of [[Sheikhupura District]] in the [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab]] province of [[Pakistan]].<ref>[https://archive.today/20120805110030/http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=30&dn=Sheikhupura Tehsils & Unions in the District of Sheikhupura - Government of Pakistan]</ref> The city is headquarters of [[Ferozewala Tehsil|Firozwala Tehsil]] and is located near the provincial capital [[Lahore]]. Situated on the Grand Trunk Road, it is the [[List of most populous cities in Pakistan|65th largest city of Pakistan]].

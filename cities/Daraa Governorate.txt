'''Daraa Governorate''' ({{langx|ar|مُحافظة درعا}} / [[ALA-LC]]: ''{{transl|ar|ALA|Muḥāfaẓat Dar‘ā}}'') is one of the fourteen [[Governorates of Syria|governorates]] (provinces) of [[Syria]]. It is situated in the south-west of the country and covers an area of 3,730&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>. It is bordered by [[Jordan]] to the south, [[Quneitra Governorate]] and [[Golan Heights]] to the west, [[Rif Dimashq Governorate]] to the north and [[As-Suwayda Governorate]] to the east. The governorate has a population of 998,000 (2010 estimate). The capital is the city of [[Daraa]].

[[Daraa Governorate campaign|Several clashes]] have occurred within the governorate throughout the [[Syrian civil war]].

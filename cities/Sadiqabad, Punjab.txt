'''Sadiqabad''' ([[Punjabi language|Punjabi]]/{{Langx|ur|{{nq|صادق آباد}}}}) is a city and capital of [[Sadiqabad Tehsil]] in [[Rahim Yar Khan District]], [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab]] province of [[Pakistan]].<ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/04/Sadiqabad.html Location of Sadiqabad - Falling Rain Genomics]</ref><ref>[http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=28&dn=Rahim%20Yar%20Khan Tehsils & Unions in the District of Rahim Yar Khan - National Reconstruction Bureau, Government of Pakistan website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120209043440/http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=28&dn=Rahim%20Yar%20Khan |date=2012-02-09 }}</ref> 

It is situated at the border of [[Sindh]] and [[Punjab, Pakistan|Punjab]]. According to the [[2023 Pakistani census]], it is the 32nd [[Largest cities of Pakistan| largest city of Pakistan]] with a population of 274,210.<ref name=city/>

== History ==
Sadiqabad, a [[Tehsil]] of [[Rahim Yar Khan District|Rahimyar Khan]] District, is named after [[Sadeq Mohammad Khan V]] of Bahawalpur state.<ref>{{Cite book|last=Wilcox, Wayne A.|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/1150950255|title=Pakistan : the consolidation of a nation|date=1969|publisher=Columbia Univ. Pr|isbn=9780231025898|edition=4. print|location=New York|oclc=1150950255}}</ref> The new city Tehsil was inaugurated in 1948.

'''Sassandra-Marahoué District''' ({{langx|fr|District du  Sassandra-Marahoué}}, {{IPA|fr|sa.sɑ̃d.ʁa ma.ʁa.we|}}) is one of fourteen [[Districts of Ivory Coast|administrative districts]] of [[Ivory Coast]]. The district is located in the central part of the country. The capital of the district is [[Daloa]].

==Creation==
Sassandra-Marahoué District was created in a 2011 administrative reorganisation of the [[subdivisions of Ivory Coast]].<ref>Décret n° 2011-263 du 28 septembre 2011 portant organisation du territoire national en Districts et en Régions.</ref> The territory of the district was composed by merging the regions of [[Haut-Sassandra]] and [[Marahoué]].<ref name=statoids>{{cite web|title=Districts of Côte d'Ivoire (Ivory Coast)|url=http://www.statoids.com/uci.html|website=Statoids.com|access-date=24 June 2015}}</ref>

==Administrative divisions==
Sassandra-Marahoué District is currently subdivided into two [[Regions of Ivory Coast|regions]] and the following [[departments of Ivory Coast|departments]]:
* '''[[Haut-Sassandra Region]]''' (region seat also in [[Daloa]])
** [[Daloa Department]]
** [[Issia Department]]
** [[Vavoua Department]]
** [[Zoukougbeu Department]]
* '''[[Marahoué Region]]''' (region seat in [[Bouaflé]])
** [[Bouaflé Department]]
** [[Sinfra Department]]
** [[Zuénoula Department]]

==Population==
According to the 2021 census, Sassandra-Marahoué District has a population of 2,720,876, making it the third most populous district in Ivory Coast, behind [[Abidjan Autonomous District]] and [[Montagnes District]].<ref name="pop"/>

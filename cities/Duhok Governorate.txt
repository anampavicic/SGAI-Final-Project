'''Duhok Governorate''' ({{langx|ku|پارێزگای دھۆک ,Parêzgeha Dihok}}, {{langx|syr|ܗܘܦܪܟܝܐ ܕܢܘܗܕܪܐ|Hoparkiya d’Nohadra}},<ref>{{cite web |title=Bahra Magazine |url=http://www.zowaa.co.uk/bahra/s145-2.pdf |website=www.zowaa.co.uk |access-date=27 April 2020 |language=syr}}</ref> {{langx|ar|محافظة دهوك|Muḥāfaẓat Dohūk}}) is a [[Governorates of Iraq|governorate]] in the autonomous [[Kurdistan Region]] of Iraq. Its capital is the city of [[Duhok]]. It includes [[Zakho]], near the [[Ibrahim Khalil border crossing]] with [[Şırnak Province]], [[Turkey]]. It borders the [[Al-Hasakah Governorate]] of [[Syria]]. It was established on 27 May 1969, previously part of [[Nineveh Governorate]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=About Duhok City |url=https://dhk-pti.com/website/DuhokCity.php |access-date=30 January 2024}}</ref> 

The population of the governorate was estimated to be 1,772,367  in 2023.<ref>{{Cite web |date=23 December 2023 |title=Kurdistan Population Exceeds Six Million in 2023 |url=https://www.basnews.com/en/babat/834275 |access-date=30 January 2024}}</ref>

== Demographics ==
Duhok Governorate is mainly populated by [[Kurds]] with an [[Arabs|Arab]], [[Assyrian people|Assyrian]], and [[Armenians|Armenian]] minority. Historically, there was a [[History of the Jews in Kurdistan|Jewish]] population in the region as well. The main religious groups are [[Muslims]], [[Yazidis]] and [[Christians]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=History|url=http://duhoktourism.org/service/history|url-status=live|access-date=3 July 2021|website=General Directorate of Tourism / Duhok|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130626203508/http://duhoktourism.org:80/service/history |archive-date=2013-06-26 }}</ref>

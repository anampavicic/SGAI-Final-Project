'''Paragominas''' is a [[Municipalities of Brazil|municipality]] in the [[states of Brazil|state]] of [[Pará]] in the [[Northern Region, Brazil|Northern region]] of [[Brazil]].<ref name="IBGE_DTB_2008">{{cite web |url=https://geoftp.ibge.gov.br/organizacao_do_territorio/estrutura_territorial/divisao_territorial/2008/dtb_2008.zip |title=Divisão Territorial do Brasil |date=July 1, 2008 |publisher=Divisão Territorial do Brasil e Limites Territoriais, Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatística (IBGE) |access-date=December 17, 2009|language=pt}}</ref><ref name="IBGE_Pop_2009">{{cite web | url = http://www.ibge.gov.br/home/estatistica/populacao/estimativa2009/POP2009_DOU.pdf | title = Estimativas da população para 1º de julho de 2009| publisher = Estimativas de População, Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatística (IBGE) | date = August 14, 2009 | access-date=December 17, 2009 |language=pt }}</ref><ref name="PNUD_IDH_2000">{{cite web |url=http://www.pnud.org.br/atlas/ranking/IDH-M%2091%2000%20Ranking%20decrescente%20(pelos%20dados%20de%202000).htm |title=Ranking decrescente do IDH-M dos municípios do Brasil |date=2000 |publisher=Atlas do Desenvolvimento Humano, Programa das Nações Unidas para o Desenvolvimento (PNUD) |access-date=December 17, 2009 |language=pt |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091003103229/http://www.pnud.org.br/atlas/ranking/IDH-M%2091%2000%20Ranking%20decrescente%20%28pelos%20dados%20de%202000%29.htm |archive-date=October 3, 2009 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref name="IBGE_PIB_2005">{{cite web |url=http://www.ibge.gov.br/home/estatistica/economia/pibmunicipios/2005/tab01.pdf |title=Produto Interno Bruto dos Municípios 2002-2005 |date=December 19, 2007 |publisher=Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatística (IBGE) |access-date=December 17, 2009|language=pt}}</ref> Paragominas mine, one of the largest bauxite mines in the world, is approximately 70&nbsp;km away. According to an estimate dated 1 July 2020,<ref>{{Cite web|title=Estimativas da população residente no Brasil e unidades da federação com data de referência em 1° de julho de 2020|url=ftp://ftp.ibge.gov.br/Estimativas_de_Populacao/Estimativas_2020/estimativa_dou_2020.pdf}}</ref> the city has a population of 111,764 and is at an altitude of 90 m above sea level. M. With a municipal area of approximately 19,342&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>, it has a population density of 5.7 inhabitants per km<sup>2</sup>, approximately the size of the state of Rhineland-Palatinate. Its distance from the capital [[Belém]] is 300&nbsp;km.

Since 2011 it has been allowed to call itself Município Verde (Green City or Eco-City).

== Toponimia ==
The name of the municipality originates from a series of abbreviations for the states of Pará, Goiás and Minas Gerais ("Para-Go-Minas").<ref>{{Cite book|last=Foweraker|first=J.|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=X2XHPpLiyQoC&pg=PA140|title=The Struggle for Land: A Political Economy of the Pioneer Frontier in Brazil from 1930 to the Present Day|date=2002-08-15|publisher=Cambridge University Press|isbn=978-0-521-52600-5|language=en}}</ref>

== History ==
The city was founded in [[1965]].

==Geography==

=== Climate ===
The city has a tropical climate according to Köppen-Geiger. The average temperature is 26.6&nbsp;°C. Average annual precipitation is 1805&nbsp;mm.<ref>[https://pt.climate-data.org/location/714961/ Climate data] retrieved March 31, 2018.</ref>

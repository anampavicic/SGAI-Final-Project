'''Gujranwala Cantonment''' ({{langx|ur|{{nq|گوجرانولہ کنٹونمنٹ}}}}) is a [[cantonment]] adjacent to [[Gujranwala]] in [[Punjab (Pakistani province)|Punjab province]], [[Pakistan]]. '''Gujranwala Cantonment''' has security checkpoints and barbed wire surrounding the cantonment.

==History==
It was established in 1967.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.globalsecurity.org/military/world/pakistan/cantt-gujranwala.htm|title=Gujranwala Cantonment|website=[[GlobalSecurity.org]]}}</ref><ref>[https://mlc.gov.pk/en/gujranwala-cantonment Overview of Cantonment Board Gujranwala] Military Lands & Cantonments, Ministry of Defence, Government of Pakistan website, Retrieved 23 July 2021</ref> It replaced the village of Cheena Thatha, the only remains of which exist in the form of Thatha Cheena Graveyard located in the cantonment.

'''Mambéré-Kadéï''' {{IPA|fr|mɑ̃.be.ʁe ka.de.i|}} is one of the 20 [[Prefectures of the Central African Republic|prefecture]]s of the [[Central African Republic]]. The prefecture covers 30,203&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup> and had a population of 197,535 as of the 2003 census, giving a population density of 6.5 inhabitants/km<sup>2</sup>. Its capital is [[Berbérati]]. It is named for the [[Mambéré River]] and [[Kadéï River]].

Until 1992, it was known as Haute-Sangha.

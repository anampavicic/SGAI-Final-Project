'''Aïn Oussera''' or '''Aïn Oussara''' ({{langx|ar|عين وسارة}}) is a city in [[Djelfa Province]], [[Algeria]], with an estimated (2005) population of 134,174. It is the district seat of the Aïn Oussera [[Districts of Algeria|District]]. This city, located 200&nbsp;km south of the capital Algiers, is crossed by the Trans-Saharan Highway main road number 1. The road that runs through the whole of Algeria from north to south over 2000&nbsp;km, connects Algiers on its southern border with Mali near Tamanrasset.  
Ain Oussera, has experienced, since 1990, rapid [[population growth]] due to its proximity to the 15 MW [[Es Salam nuclear reactor]]. Aïn Oussera is on the crossroads of the N1 ([[Trans-Sahara Highway|Trans-Saharan]]) and N40B highways. The nearest airport is the [[Algerian Air Force]]'s Aïn Oussera airfield (DAAQ).

'''Al Hawiyah''' is a town in [[Mecca Region|Makkah Province]], located {{convert|15|km|mi|frac=2|abbr=off}} from [[Ta'if]]. It was a small suburb in the past. Currently it has expanded to include more than 15 residential neighborhoods, with an area of {{convert|80|km2|sqmi|frac=2}} and is inhabited with many different classes of Saudi society. The population of Al Hawiya in 2010 was about 200,000 according to the last national census.<ref>{{Cite web|title=أهالي الحوية بانتظار إنشاء مستشفى يخدم المنطقة|url=http://www.alriyadh.com/464788|access-date=2020-08-10|website=[[Al Riyadh (newspaper)|Al Riyadh]] |language=ar}}</ref> Nowadays, Al Hawiyah is considered as an emerging vibrant area with many government facilities. And currently known as North of Ta'if city.

Al Hawiya is about {{convert|214|km|mi|0}} from [[Al Wahbah crater|Al-Waba Crater.]]<ref>[[National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency]]. [[GeoNames]] database entry. ([http://geonames.nga.mil/ggmagaz/ search]) Accessed 12 May 2011.</ref>

'''Almetyevsk''' ({{langx|ru|Альме́тьевск}}; {{IPA|ru|ɐlʲˈmʲetʲjɪfsk|IPA}}; {{langx|tt-Cyrl|Әлмәт}}) is a [[types of inhabited localities in Russia|city]] in [[Tatarstan]], [[Russia]], located on the left bank of the [[Zay River]] ([[Kama River|Kama]]'s [[tributary]]) {{convert|265|km|sp=us}} southeast of [[Kazan]]. Population: {{ru-census2010|146,393|;}} {{ru-census2002|140,437|;}} {{su-census1989|129,008|;}} 77,000 (1969); 49,000 (1959).

Almetyevsk is one of the youngest cities in the republic. Located along the federal highway ({{langx|ru|автотрасса федерального значения}}) R239 "Orenburgsky" [[Kazan]] - [[Orenburg]] (in Cyrillic [[:ru:Р239 (автодорога)|P239]] ({{langx|ru|"Оренбургский"}}),<ref>ШАНС-ON (22 June 2011). ==Administrative and municipal status==
Within the [[subdivisions of Russia#Administrative divisions|framework of administrative divisions]], Almetyevsk serves as the [[administrative center]] of [[Almetyevsky District]], even though it is not a part of it.<ref name="RTatarstan_adm">Order #01-02/9</ref> As an administrative division, it is incorporated separately as the '''[[city of federal subject significance|city of republic significance]] of Almetyevsk'''—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the [[administrative divisions of the Republic of Tatarstan|districts]].<ref name="RTatarstan_adm" /> As a [[subdivisions of Russia#Municipal divisions|municipal division]], the city of republic significance of Almetyevsk is incorporated within Almetyevsky Municipal District as '''Almetyevsk Urban Settlement'''.<ref name="RTatarstanAlmetyevskyD_mun">Law #9-ZRT</ref>

== Cycling capital of Russia ==
Almetyevsk has created highly developed [[cycling infrastructure]], considered the best in Russia, thanks to which the bicycle has become a full-fledged and popular type of street transport used year-round. Its creation began in 2016, as part of a set of measures to increase the attractiveness of the city.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Варламов |first=Илья |date=2021-07-01 |title=Российский город, которому можно позавидовать |url=https://varlamov.ru//2809615.html |access-date=2022-04-09 |website=Teletype |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Альметьевск: 50 км велодорожек построено — Let's bike it! |url=http://letsbikeit.ru/2016/10/almetyevsk/ |access-date=2022-04-09 |website=letsbikeit.ru |language=ru}}</ref> Thanks to its cycling infrastructure, Almetyevsk has received the unofficial title of the cycling capital of Russia.

==Industry==
Almetyevsk is an important center of [[Petroleum industry in Russia|Russia's]] [[oil industry]]. Since 1960, the [[Druzhba pipeline]] has started in Almetyevsk.<ref name=TatneftHistory>[https://tatneft.ru/o-kompanii/istoriya-gruppi-tatneft/?lang=ru История Группы «Татнефть» (History of TATNEFT Group)]. ==Sports==
Major sport enterprise in the city is an ice-hockey team "Neftyanik" presented in VHL league and sponsored by TatNeft. The team's main records are bronze medalists of Russian Championship Higher League (2002/2003 years ), gold medalists of Russian Championship Higher League (1997-1998 and 1999–2000), silver medalists of VHL 2010/2011 year, 2015/2016 Bratina Cup winners

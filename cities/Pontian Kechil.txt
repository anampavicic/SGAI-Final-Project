'''Pontian Kechil''' ([[Jawi script|Jawi]]: ڤونتين كچيل) also known as '''Pontian Town''' (Malay: ''Bandar Pontian'') is a town and the administrative centre of [[Pontian District]], [[Johor]], [[Malaysia]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Rancangan Tempatan Daerah Pontian (Penggantian) 2030|url=http://www.townplan.gov.my/epublisiti/RTDPontian2030.zip|language=ms|format=ZIP}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sinarharian.com.my/edisi/johor/iktiraf-pontian-bandar-nanas-1.772130|title = Edisi|date = 26 December 2018}}</ref>

==History==
The town was originally a fishing village. Now it has grown as a town.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tourism.johor.my/destination/pontian-3/|title=Pontian|website=Tourism Johor|access-date=21 February 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181224030648/http://tourism.johor.my/destination/pontian-3/|archive-date=24 December 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref>

==Geography==
[[File:Map of Pontian District, Johor.svg|thumb|Pontian Kechil in Pontian District]]
The town spans over an area of 6.6&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>.<ref>[http://jpbd.johor.gov.my/images/jpbd_DokumenTerbitan/Handbook.pdf Handbook] johor.gov.my</ref>

==Administration==
Pontian is administered by Pontian Municipal Council (MPPn). Pontian's postcode is 82000.

==Transportation==
===Buses===
[[File:Maju Route 96 Larkin - Pontian (230925) 01.jpg|thumb|Maju route 96 ]]
The town is served by MyBas Johor (T50) & Maju (96) public buses linking to [[Larkin Sentral|Larkin Sentral Terminal]] in [[Johor Bahru|Johor Bahru City]], Causeway Link (52T) to [[Gelang Patah]] and other towns within the district, such as [[Benut]] and [[Kukup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://paj.com.my/transjohor-route-schedules/|title=Bus Routes and Schedule|website=Pengangkutan Awam Johor|access-date=17 March 2018}}</ref>

===Car===
Being located along [[Malaysia Federal Route 5|Federal Route 5]], Pontian is relatively accessible for a town its size. Northward this highway goes to [[Melaka City]], [[Klang (city)|Klang]] and [[Teluk Intan]] before terminating at [[Jelapang, Perak|Jelapang]]. Southwards highway 5 turn right to [[Pekan Nanas]] and terminates at [[Johor Bahru]].

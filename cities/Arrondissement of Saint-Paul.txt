The '''arrondissement of Saint-Paul''' is an [[Arrondissements of France|arrondissement]] of France in the [[Réunion]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the Réunion [[Regions of France|region]]. It has five [[Communes of France|communes]].<ref name="insee2" /> Its population is 214,073 (2016), and its area is {{convert|537.2|km2|abbr=on}}.<ref name=insee>{{cite web|url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=ARR-9741+ARR-9742+ARR-9743+ARR-9744|title=Comparateur de territoire, géographie au 01/01/2019|accessdate=2019-10-06|publisher=[[INSEE]]}}</ref>

==History==

The arrondissement of Saint-Paul, containing four communes that were previously part of the [[arrondissement of Saint-Pierre, Réunion|arrondissement of Saint-Pierre]] and one commune that was previously part of the [[arrondissement of Saint-Denis, Réunion|arrondissement of Saint-Denis]], was created in 1969.<ref>[https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/affichTexte.do?cidTexte=JORFTEXT000000719357 Décret n°69-876 du 24 septembre 1969]</ref> In September 2006 it absorbed the two communes of [[Le Port, Réunion|Le Port]] and [[La Possession]] from the arrondissement of Saint-Denis, and it lost the two communes of [[Les Avirons]] and [[L'Étang-Salé]] to the arrondissement of Saint-Pierre.<ref name=arrete>{{cite web|url=http://administration.reunion.gouv.fr/raa/2006/juin/arrete2433.pdf|title=Arrêté préfectoral, 30 June 2006|accessdate=2019-10-29|archive-date=2019-10-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191029133016/http://administration.reunion.gouv.fr/raa/2006/juin/arrete2433.pdf|url-status=dead}}</ref>

As a result of the reorganisation of the [[cantons of France]] which came into effect in 2015, the borders of the cantons are no longer related to the borders of the arrondissements.

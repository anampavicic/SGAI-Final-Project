The '''Kubang Pasu District''' is a [[Districts of Malaysia|district]] in northern [[Kedah]], [[Malaysia]]. It contains the border town of [[Bukit Kayu Hitam]] as well as the educational hub of [[Changlun]], while [[Jitra]] is the largest town and administrative centre of the district. The district council had been upgraded into municipal council on 22 October 2018, becoming the fifth city or municipal in the state.

==History==
In the 19th century Kubang Pasu was a semi-independent kingdom, known as ''[[Kingdom of Kubang Pasu Darul Qiyam|Kubang Pasu Darul Qiyam]]'', ruled by Phaya or King Tunku Anom, Prince of Kedah. It was reintegrated into Kedah proper in the 1860s.

On 22 October 2018, Kubang Pasu was granted municipal status; the erstwhile district council was upgraded into a municipal council (''Majlis Perbandaran Kubang Pasu'').<ref>{{cite web |title=Latar Belakang |url=http://www.mpkubangpasu.gov.my/ms/mdkp/profil/latar-belakang |website=Portal Rasmi Majlis Perbandaran Kubang Pasu |access-date=7 December 2019}}</ref>

==Geography==
The district spans over an area of 948&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>. To the west it borders [[Perlis]], while its northern boundary forms part of the [[Malaysia-Thailand border]]. From east to south, in clockwise order, are the constituencies of [[Padang Terap]], [[Pokok Sena]] and [[Kuala Kedah (federal constituency)|Kuala Kedah]].

==Education==
The district is also known as ''Education Valley'' in Kedah because there is a concentration of educational institutions in Kubang Pasu. They are [[Universiti Utara Malaysia]], [http://www.kmk.matrik.edu.my/ Kolej Matrikulasi Kedah] (KMK), Polytechnic of Sultan Abdul Halim Mu'adzam Shah, Industrial Training Institute (ILP), Bandar Darulaman Community College (Kolej Komuniti Bandar Darulaman), Akademi Binaan Malaysia (ABM), Institut Kemahiran Belia Negara (IKBN) and others.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.utusan.com.my/berita/wilayah/150-anggota-polis-ahli-perkep-ipd-kubang-pasu-jayakan-ibadah-korban-1.140450|title=150 anggota polis, ahli Perkep IPD Kubang Pasu jayakan ibadah korban}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.didkedah.gov.my/index.php/galeri/arkib-sebutharga/92-kenyataan-kerja-undi-daerah-kubang-pasu.html|title=Kenyataan Kerja Undi - Daerah Kubang Pasu - Jabatan Pengairan dan Saliran Negeri Kedah Darul Aman|last=KPPT|website=www.didkedah.gov.my}}</ref>

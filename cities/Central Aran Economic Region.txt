'''Central Aran Economic Region''' ({{langx|az|Mərkəzi Aran iqtisadi rayonu}}) is one of the 14 [[Economic regions of Azerbaijan|economic regions]] of [[Azerbaijan]]. It borders the economic regions of [[Shaki-Zagatala Economic Region|Shaki-Zagatala]], [[Mountainous Shirvan Economic Region|Mountainous Shirvan]], [[Shirvan-Salyan Economic Region|Shirvan-Salyan]], [[Mil-Mughan Economic Region|Mil-Mughan]], [[Karabakh Economic Region|Karabakh]], and [[Ganja-Dashkasan Economic Region|Ganja-Dashkasan]]. The region consists of the districts of [[Agdash District|Agdash]], [[Goychay District|Goychay]], [[Kurdamir District|Kurdamir]], [[Ujar District|Ujar]], [[Yevlakh District|Yevlakh]], [[Zardab District|Zardab]], as well as the city of [[Mingachevir]].<ref>{{cite web |title=New economic regions of Azerbaijan – LIST |url=https://report.az/en/domestic-politics/new-economic-regions-of-azerbaijan-list/ |website=Report News Agency |date=7 July 2021 |access-date=24 April 2022 }}</ref> It has an area of {{convert|6,690|km2|sqmi}}. Its population was estimated to be at 740 thousand people in January 2021.<ref name="statgov" />

== History ==
Central Aran Economic Region was established on 7 July 2021 as part of a reform of the economic region system of Azerbaijan. Its territory was part of the larger [[Aran Economic Region]] prior to 2021.<ref>{{cite web |title=Azərbaycan Respublikasında iqtisadi rayonların yeni bölgüsü haqqında Azərbaycan Respublikası Prezidentinin Fərmanı |url=https://president.az/articles/52389 |website=president.az |language=az}}</ref>

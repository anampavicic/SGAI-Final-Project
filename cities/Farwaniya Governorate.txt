'''Farwaniya Governorate''' ({{langx|ar|محافظة الفروانية}} {{translit|ar|Muḥāfaẓat al-Farwānīyah}}) is the most populous of the six [[governorates of Kuwait]], and is the country's only [[landlocked]] governorate. It is [[Kuwait City]]'s main residential area and also forms an important part of Kuwait's commercial activities.

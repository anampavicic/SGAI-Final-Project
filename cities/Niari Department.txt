'''Niari''' (can also be written as ''Niadi''<ref>{{cite journal|url= https://www.persee.fr/doc/globe_0398-3412_1885_num_24_1_4668_t1_0057_0000_2 |language=fr|title= Proceedings of the Royal Geographical Society (de Londres) |author= M., A. |journal= Le Globe. Revue genevoise de géographie |year=1885 |volume=24 |issue=1 |pages=57–60 |access-date=5 January 2021}}</ref>) is a [[Departments of the Republic of the Congo|department]] of the [[Republic of the Congo]] in the western part of the country. It borders the departments of [[Bouenza Department|Bouenza]], [[Kouilou Department|Kouilou]], and [[Lékoumou Department|Lékoumou]], and internationally, [[Gabon]], the [[Democratic Republic of the Congo]], and the [[Cabinda (province)|Cabinda]] portion of [[Angola]]. The regional capital is [[Dolisie]].

[[Image:Niari districts.png|thumb|right|250px|Until 2012 Niari had only six districts]]

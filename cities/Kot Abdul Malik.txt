'''Kot Abdul Malik''' ({{lang-pa|{{nq|کوٹ عبد المالک}}}}) is a city in [[Sheikhupura District]], [[Punjab, Pakistan|Punjab]], [[Pakistan]] located near the [[M-2 motorway (Pakistan)|M-2 motorway]].<ref>[https://maps.google.com/maps?q=Kot+Abdul+Malik,+Punjab,+Pakistan&hl=en&ll=31.623841,74.237022&spn=0.009757,0.013797&sll=31.617135,74.2202&sspn=0.148813,0.220757&gl=pk&z=16 On google maps]</ref><ref>[http://wikimapia.org/#lat=31.6236037&lon=74.2348337&z=15&l=44&m=b On Wikimapia]</ref> It is the [[Largest cities of Pakistan|63rd largest city]] in [[Pakistan]]. Kot Abdul Malik is the part of [[Ferozewala Tehsil]] of [[Sheikhupura District]].

The city is situated on the Lahore-Sheikhupura road. <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mapquest.com/pakistan/punjab/kot-abdul-malik/546600/lahore-sheikhupura-faisalabad-road-31.631398,74.214085|title=Lahore - Sheikhupura Faisalabad Road - Map of Cities in Lahore - Sheikhupura Faisalabad Road - MapQuest|website=www.mapquest.com|language=en|access-date=2019-07-21}}</ref>

'''Miryalaguda''' is a city in [[Nalgonda district]] of the [[India]]n state of [[Telangana]].<ref name=census>{{cite web|title=District Census Handbook - Nalgonda|url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/dchb/2808_PART_B_DCHB_NALGONDA.pdf|website=Census of India|access-date=15 February 2016|page=13}}</ref> In 2015, the [[Government of India]] placed Miryalaguda under the [[Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation]] scheme.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eenaduindia.com/State/AndhraPradhesh/2015/06/24090313/AP-identifies-31-Telangana-15-cities-under-AMRUT-scheme.vpf|title=AP identifies 31, Telangana 15 cities under AMRUT scheme|work=Eenadu English Portal}}</ref>
<ref>{{cite news |url= https://www.thehindu.com/news/cities/Hyderabad/8-skoch-awards-for-municipal-administration-dept/article24204577.ece |title=8 SKOCH awards for Municipal Administration dept. |work= The Hindu English Portal|date=19 June 2018 }}</ref> It is located about {{convert|44|km|mi|0}} from the district headquarters [[Nalgonda]], {{convert|142|km|mi|0}} from the state capital [[Hyderabad]].

==Geography==
Miryalaguda is located at {{Coord|16.8667|N|79.5833|E|}}.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/02/Mirialguda.html|title=redirect to /world/IN/02/Mirialguda.html}}</ref> It has an average elevation of {{convert|105|m}}.

== Governance == 
=== Civic Administration ===
Miryalaguda Municipality was constituted as a municipality in 1984 and is governed by the provisions of the 
Telangana State Municipalities Act, 1965 (Act). MLGM is grade-I municipality, which manages the civic services in 
Miryalaguda town, located in the state of Telangana (TG)

The city also Serves as headquarters of Miryalaguda mandal in the Miryalaguda revenue division

== Transport ==
=== Road ===
State highway 2 passes through Miryalaguda as well as [[National Highway 167 (India)|National Highway 167]]

[[TSRTC]] operates buses from Miryalaguda to various destinations in the state.

[[File:Miryalaguda Street.jpg|thumb|A street in Miryalaguda]]

=== Railway ===
The [[Miryalaguda railway station|railway station]] has three platforms and is situated on the [[Pagidipalli–Nallapadu section]] in Guntur Division. The track is an electrified, single line; it was electrified in 2018–19. Pagidipalli-Nallapadu was electrified in 2019. The electric trains started service on 1 September 2019.{{citation needed|date=September 2019}}

=== Air ===

[[Rajiv Gandhi International Airport]] in Hyderabad is the nearest airport at a distance of 156&nbsp;km by road. The other airport nearer to Miryalaguda is [[Vijayawada International Airport]] which is at a distance of 151&nbsp;km by road.

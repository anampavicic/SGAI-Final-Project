'''Sambrial''' ({{langx|ur|{{Nastaliq|سمبڑيال}}}}) is a city in [[Sialkot District]] in the [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab]] province of [[Pakistan]]. It is the capital of [[Sambrial Tehsil]],<ref>[https://www.punjab.gov.pk/node/861 Sialkot - Tehsil Headquarters (shows Sambrial city as being the Tehsil Headquarters)] Punjab Portal, Government of Punjab website, Retrieved 25 July 2021</ref> an administrative subdivision of the district. [[Sialkot Dry Port]] is located in Sambrial. Sialkot international Airport is also located near Sambrial. It is the [[List of most populous cities in Pakistan|90th largest city of Pakistan]] by population.

==Location==
The city is situated on the bank of Upper [[Chenab River|Chenab]] Canal and lies to the west of the district capital [[Sialkot]]. The tehsil of [[Sambrial Tehsil|Sambrial]] is a tehsil of [[Sialkot District]] in [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab, Pakistan]] comprising 160 villages which are politically organized under 17 union councils in 2017.<ref>[https://www.punjab.gov.pk/node/1369 Ombudsman Punjab Took Notice of Irregularities in Sambrial Registry Branch] Punjab Portal, Government of Punjab website, Retrieved 25 July 2021</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://pmdfc.org.pk/gis-map/gis-maps-08/Sambrial/Sambrial%20SW.pdf |website=Punjab Municipal Development Fund Company website|archive-url=http://webarchive.loc.gov/all/20120609025537/http://pmdfc.org.pk/gis-map/gis-maps-08/Sambrial/Sambrial%20SW.pdf |archive-date=9 June 2012|title=Solid Waste Collection Map - Sambrial|access-date=25 July 2021}}</ref><ref>[https://en.climate-data.org/search/?q=Sambrial Sambrial weather on Climate-Data.org website] Retrieved 25 July 2021</ref>

== Sambrial motorway ==
In mid 2022, the NHA started constructing the 69&nbsp;km Kharian-Sambrial motorway, expanding Lahore-Sialkot motorway (M-11). The estimated cost of the project was Rs42 billion, and work had begun at Kharian.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Butt |first=Waseem Ashraf |date=2022-07-29 |title=Kharian-Sambrial motorway project’s construction work launched |url=https://www.dawn.com/news/1702017 |access-date=2023-03-15 |website=DAWN.COM |language=en}}</ref>

Officials stressed the need for quality roads and material, and for link roads to be built to connect cities. The government hopes to provide better travel facilities and reduce travel time. The first 35&nbsp;km phase has started from Kharian. Link roads to connect cities should also be built with quality materials. The government hopes to reduce travel time and provide better facilities.<ref>{{Cite web |author=News Desk |date=2022-11-10 |title=Gujrat-Sambrial-Kharian Motorway to be completed within two years |url=https://pakobserver.net/gujrat-sambrial-kharian-motorway-to-be-completed-within-two-years/ |access-date=2023-03-15 |website=Pakistan Observer |language=en-US}}</ref>

The National Highways Authority (NHA) earmarked 4,600 acres of land in three tehsils, and a sum of Rs13 billion allocated for compensation to the affected owners and utility services.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Butt |first=Waseem Ashraf |date=2022-01-21 |title=Sambrial-Kharian motorway: Rs13bn estimated for compensation |url=https://www.dawn.com/news/1670592 |access-date=2023-03-15 |website=DAWN.COM |language=en}}</ref>

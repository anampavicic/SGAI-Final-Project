'''Madina''' is a suburb of [[Accra]] and in the [[La Nkwantanang Madina Municipal District]], a [[Districts of Ghana|district]] in the [[Greater Accra Region]]<ref>{{Cite web|title=Six-year-old girl rescued from Alajo storm drain - MyJoyOnline.com|url=https://www.myjoyonline.com/six-year-old-girl-rescued-from-alajo-storm-drain/|access-date=2021-05-20|website=www.myjoyonline.com|language=en-US}}</ref> of southeastern [[Ghana]].<ref name="ouring Ghana - Greater Accra Region">[http://www.touringghana.com/regions/greateraccra_region.asp Touring Ghana - Greater Accra Region] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120411133930/http://www.touringghana.com/regions/greateraccra_region.asp |date=2012-04-11 }}</ref> Madina is next to the [[University of Ghana]] and houses the Institute of Local Government. Madina is the twelfth most populous [[Human settlement|settlement]] in Ghana, in terms of population, with a population of 137,162 people.<ref name="World Gazetteer"/> History has it that Madina was established by people from different ethnic backgrounds and some foreigners led by Alhaji Seidu Kardo.<ref>{{Cite web |title=The Real Story Behind The Town, Madina. |url=https://www.modernghana.com/lifestyle/12342/the-real-story-behind-the-town-madina.html |access-date=2022-09-05 |website=Modern Ghana |language=en}}</ref> Madina is contained in the [[Madina (Ghana parliament constituency)|Madina]] electoral constituency of the republic of Ghana. It shares borders with [[Adenta Municipal District|Adentan Municipal]] on the west, the [[Accra Metropolitan Assembly]] to the South and the [[Akuapim South District|Akwapim South District]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Education – LaNMMA |url=https://lanmma.gov.gh/departments/education/ |access-date=2022-09-05 |language=en-US}}</ref> to the north.

== Politics ==
The multiparty system in Ghana<ref>{{Cite web |date=2006-05-24 |title=Developing Multi-Party Politics: Stability and Change in Ghana and Mozambique - GSDRC |url=https://gsdrc.org/document-library/developing-multi-party-politics-stability-and-change-in-ghana-and-mozambique/,%20https://gsdrc.org/document-library/developing-multi-party-politics-stability-and-change-in-ghana-and-mozambique/ |access-date=2022-09-05 |website=gsdrc.org |language=en-US}}</ref>  permits citizens to freely join any political party of their choice.  Parliamentary aspirants from the two main political parties won the parliamentary elections between 2005 and 2020 to become members of parliament.  Members of parliament from 2005 to date are Alhaji Bukari Amadu Sorogho, who was a member of Parliament from 2005 to 2017,  Alhaji Abu-bakar, and Saddique Boniface 2017 – 2021. The incumbent member of parliament for the Madina Municipality 2021 – 2025 is Lawyer Francis Xavier Kojo Sosu<ref>{{Cite web |title=Madina |url=https://ghanamps.com/constituencies/madina/ |access-date=2022-09-05 |website=Ghana MPS |language=en-US}}</ref>

== Religion and Beliefs ==
The majority of the residents are Muslims and Christians, with few practicing traditional religions. Although residents practice different religions, they co-exist peacefully.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Madina |url=https://religiousmatters.nl/projects/madina |access-date=2022-09-05 |website=religiousmatters.nl |language=en-GB}}</ref>

== Madina Market ==

Madina market is one of the biggest and most vibrant markets in Accra.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Team |first=Editorial |date=2020-04-05 |title=Top Markets In Greater Accra » Ghana Insider |url=https://ghanainsider.com/markets-in-accra/ |access-date=2022-09-05 |language=en-US}}</ref> Located on the Chief Alhaji Seidu Street, popularly known as Old road.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Madina Market · Madina, Ghana |url=https://www.google.com.gh/maps/place/Madina+Market,+Madina/@5.6762422,-0.1700497,16.25z/data=!4m5!3m4!1s0xfdf9ce7efafc6cb:0xff231625802dfa11!8m2!3d5.6789707!4d-0.1691052 |access-date=2022-09-05 |website=Madina Market · Madina, Ghana |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Way: Madina Market (419698151) |url=https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/419698151 |access-date=2022-09-05 |website=OpenStreetMap |language=en}}</ref> It spans from Madina Zongo junction to Madina Polyclinic, Kekele, the main entrance can be found opposite to the Madina Divisional Police Station. Vendors, Sellers and providers of services at the market are active and competitive. Hawkers of various items mingle or display variety of items on tables and pavements along the street, thereby increases the visibility of the market to both travellers and visitors.

'''La Victoria''' ({{IPA|es|la βiɣˈtoɾja}}) is a [[city]] in the [[States of Venezuela|state]] of [[Aragua (state)|Aragua]] in [[Venezuela]].

It is famous for the independence battle of 12 February 1814, the [[Battle of La Victoria (1812)|Battle of La Victoria]], where [[José Félix Ribas]] led a young and inexperienced army that succeeded in halting the royalist troops of [[José Tomás Boves]] at La Victoria. Venezuela celebrates "Youth Day" every 12 February in La Victoria, with a ceremony usually presided over by the [[President of Venezuela|President of the Republic]].{{cn|date=February 2023}}

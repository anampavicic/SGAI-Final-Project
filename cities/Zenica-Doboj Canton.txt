[[File:Bih cantons en.png|thumb|250px|right|Cantons of the [[Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina]]]]
The '''Zenica-Doboj Canton''' ({{Langx|bs|Zeničko-dobojski kanton}}; {{langx|hr|Zeničko-dobojska županija}}; {{langx|sr|Зеничко-добојски кантон}})<ref name="Constitution" /> is one of 10 [[cantons of the Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina]] in [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]]. The cantonal seat is the City of [[Zenica]].

==History==
During the [[2014 unrest in Bosnia and Herzegovina|protests and riots in Bosnia and Herzegovina]] in February 2014, the entire government of the Zenica-Doboj Canton resigned.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sarajevotimes.com/government-of-zenica-doboj-canton-resigned/|title=Government of Zenica-Doboj Canton Resigned|publisher=Sarajevo Times|date=7 February 2014|access-date=1 April 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rtvbn.com/mobile/vijest/24399/vlada-zenicko-dobojskog-kantona-podnosi-ostavku|title=Vlada Zeničko-dobojskog kantona podnosi ostavku|publisher=RTVBN|date=7 February 2014|access-date=1 April 2015}}</ref>

==Geography==
This canton is situated in the central part of [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]]. The cantonal capital is [[Zenica]] and the other town mentioned in the name is [[Doboj]], which is in Republika Srpska, but part of the former Doboj municipality is in the Zenica-Doboj Canton. The canton has an area of 3,904&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>.

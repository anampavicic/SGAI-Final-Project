'''Sơn La''' is a [[Provinces of Vietnam|province]] nestled in the [[Northwest (Vietnam)|northwestern]] part of [[Vietnam]], renowned for its stunning landscapes and rich cultural heritage. The province is characterized by rolling hills, lush [[Valley|valleys]], and [[Terrace (earthworks)|terraced rice fields]]. The province is home to diverse [[Ethnicity|ethnic communities]], including the [[Thai people|Thai]], [[Hmong people|H'Mong]], and [[Muong people|Muong]] people.

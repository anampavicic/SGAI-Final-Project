'''Bukoba''' is a city with a population of 144,938 (2022 census),<ref>[https://www.citypopulation.de/en/tanzania/cities/ Citypopulation.de] Population of cities & urban localities in Tanzania</ref> situated in the northwest of [[Tanzania]] on the south-western shores of [[Lake Victoria]]. It is the capital of the [[Kagera Region|Kagera region]], and the administrative seat for [[Bukoba Urban District]].

The city is served by [[Bukoba Airport]] and regular ferry connections to and from [[Mwanza]], as well as roads to [[Uganda]]'s [[Rakai District]] for cross-border commuters. Plans are underway for a standard gauge railway <ref>http://www.mwtc.go.tz {{Bare URL inline|date=August 2024}}</ref> to [[Uganda]].

== Bukoba City ==

Situated on the south-western shores of Lake Victoria, Bukoba lies only [[1st parallel south|1 degree south]] of the Equator.

Bukoba is situated on the south-western shores of [[Lake Victoria]] in the north-western region of [[Tanzania]]. The regional capital and Kagera's biggest town is the gateway to the region.

It boasts a white sandy beach, a large market,<ref name="Jamhuri ya Muungano wa Tanzania"/> a port<ref name="Home"/> tennis courts<ref name=MICAS>{{Cite web|url=http://www.habari.go.tz/|title=Ministry of Information, Culture, Arts and Sports|website=www.habari.go.tz|access-date=2019-06-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190604134812/http://habari.go.tz/|archive-date=2019-06-04|url-status=live}}</ref> and a swimming pool.<ref name=MICAS/> It has 13 banks,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bot.go.tz/|title=Bank of Tanzania (BOT): Home Page|website=www.bot.go.tz|access-date=2019-06-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190502074052/https://www.bot.go.tz/|archive-date=2019-05-02|url-status=live}}</ref> 5 of which take VISA cards in the ATM.

Bukoba Town itself has the status of a municipal Council. It has a municipal Director and other local government officials like other district councils in the region.

==Ports==
Bukoba is currently Tanzania's second largest port on Lake Victoria after Mwanza.<ref name="Home">{{Cite web|url=https://www.ports.go.tz/index.php/en/|title=Home|website=www.ports.go.tz|access-date=2019-06-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190512223442/https://www.ports.go.tz/index.php/en/|archive-date=2019-05-12|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Utility==

Bukoba is served by the National Electric Utility Company [[TANESCO]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tanesco.co.tz/|title=TANESCO - Welcome to TANESCO website|website=www.tanesco.co.tz|access-date=2019-06-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190415173632/http://www.tanesco.co.tz/|archive-date=2019-04-15|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Economy==
Kagera's Regional Commissioner resides and has his head office in The Bukoba Central Business District.<ref name="Jamhuri ya Muungano wa Tanzania">{{Cite web|url=http://www.mit.go.tz/|title=Jamhuri ya Muungano wa Tanzania|website=www.mit.go.tz|access-date=2019-06-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190711021206/https://www.mit.go.tz/|archive-date=2019-07-11|url-status=live}}</ref>

Bukoba is the leading district in Tanzania with a high percentage of people with middle-class incomes. The percentage of people living under the poverty line is the lowest in the country. Bukoba has a new mall named Bakwata Complex that will start operation in September 2024. 

The city is flat, compact, and surrounded by hills. The town has a bus stand,<ref name="mwtc.go.tz">{{Cite web|url=http://www.mwtc.go.tz/|title=Home {{!}} Ministry of Works, Transport and Communications|website=www.mwtc.go.tz|access-date=2019-06-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190713095648/http://www.mwtc.go.tz/|archive-date=2019-07-13|url-status=live}}</ref> an airport,<ref name="Tanzania Airports Authority">{{Cite web|url=http://www.taa.go.tz/|title=Tanzania Airports Authority|website=www.taa.go.tz|access-date=2019-06-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170727221624/http://taa.go.tz/|archive-date=2017-07-27|url-status=live}}</ref> and a port.<ref>http://www.ports.go.t{{Dead link|date=June 2019 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> A ferry used to travel from Bukoba via Kemondo Bay Port to Mwanza on Monday, Wednesday and Friday nights, and soon a newly built ship, the second largest lake ship in Africa, will make ferry voyages to Bukoba daily.<ref name="mwtc.go.tz" />

===Bukoba Airport===
The Airport<ref name="Tanzania Airports Authority"/> is located south east of the city. The address on Sokoine Road.

[[Auric Air]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.auricair.com/|title=Auric Air Services Ltd|website=www.auricair.com|access-date=2019-06-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190524080519/https://www.auricair.com/|archive-date=2019-05-24|url-status=live}}</ref> flies three times a day to Mwanza. In 2010 the airport<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.taa.go.tz/ |title=Tanzania Airports Authority |access-date=2019-06-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170727221624/http://taa.go.tz/ |archive-date=2017-07-27 |url-status=live }}</ref> was extended as more flights were expected in line with capacity growth milestones of The Air Terminal reviewed after published reports every quarter of the year.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.mwtc.go.tz/ |title=Home {{pipe}} Ministry of Works, Transport and Communications |access-date=2019-06-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190713095648/http://www.mwtc.go.tz/ |archive-date=2019-07-13 |url-status=live }}</ref> [[List of Air Tanzania destinations|Air Tanzania]] (ATCL) also have scheduled flights to Bukoba throughout the week with connecting flights through Mwanza to Dar es Salaam.

==Transport==

===Bus===
A bus<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.sumatra.go.tz/ |title=Welcome to SUMATRA website |access-date=2019-10-06 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190323184027/http://www.sumatra.go.tz/ |archive-date=2019-03-23 |url-status=live }}</ref> leaves for Kampala at 7 am every day (about 6 hours) and from Kampala for Bukoba at 11 am every day.

==Sports==
Bukoba is represented in the [[Tanzanian Premier League]]<ref>https://www.fifa.com {{Bare URL inline|date=August 2024}}</ref> by football club [[Kagera Sugar]]. It is the home to several charities - including [[SHARE in Africa]] and [[Jambo Bukoba]]. Both are focused on the educational sector.

The Kaitaba Stadium<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.bukobasports.com/|title=bukobasports.com - Diese Website steht zum Verkauf! - Informationen zum Thema bukobasports.|website=www.bukobasports.com|access-date=2019-06-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190630143502/http://bukobasports.com/|archive-date=2019-06-30|url-status=live}}</ref> is also found in south east of Bukoba on Jamhuri Road. The stadium has the capacity of 15000 people and supports football games day and night.

== Climate ==
Bukoba has an upland [[tropical monsoon climate]] ([[Köppen climate classification|Köppen]] ''Amb'', because every month has a normal mean temperature of between 18&nbsp;°C and 22&nbsp;°C) although it is milder than most climates with that classification due to its altitude. It can sometimes get cool especially in the evenings during the two rainy seasons, but never as cold as the winter season in [[Europe]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://weather.com/|title=National and Local Weather Radar, Daily Forecast, Hurricane and information from The Weather Channel and weather.com|website=The Weather Channel|language=en-US|access-date=2019-06-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190828150844/https://weather.com/|archive-date=2019-08-28|url-status=live}}</ref>

'''Parnamirim''' is a city in the [[States of Brazil|state]] of [[Rio Grande do Norte]], [[Brazil]], next to the state capital city of [[Natal, Rio Grande do Norte|Natal]] and part of its [[metropolitan area]].

[[Natal Air Force Base|Natal Air Force Base - ALA10]], one of the most important bases of the [[Brazilian Air Force]], is located in Parnamirim at the former [[Augusto Severo International Airport]]. The site is also home to [[Barreira do Inferno Launch Center|Barreira do Inferno]] [[Spaceport|rocket launch base]].

== Geography ==
Parnamirim belongs to the Intermediate Geographic Region of Natal and the Metropolitan Region of Natal. It is located on the coast with [[Atlantic Ocean]], about 13 kilometers south of the urban center of [[Natal, Rio Grande do Norte|Natal]]. Its average elevation is 48 meters above the sea level.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Parnamirim topographic map, elevation, terrain |url=https://en-gb.topographic-map.com/map-mr7gz4/Parnamirim/?center=-5.91521,-35.268 |access-date=2023-04-27 |website=Topographic maps |language=en}}</ref>

== Climate ==
Parnamirim has a [[Tropical savanna climate|Tropical Savanna Climate]] (As). It receives the most amount of rainfall in April, with 138 mm of average precipitation; and the least amount of rainfall in October, with 4 mm of average precipitation.

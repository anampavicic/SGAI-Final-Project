'''El Mourouj''' ({{langx|ar|المروج|el-Murūj}}) is a town and commune in the southern suburbs of [[Tunis]] in the [[Ben Arous Governorate]], [[Tunisia]]. It became a commune in 1991. It has 120 732 inhabitants as of 2022,  making it the most populous commune in the Ben Arous Governorate.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://dataportal.ins.tn/fr/DataAnalysis?SLIyDNUqIUqE3bQE4PQj3Q |title=Portail de données de la Tunisie, Analyse de Données |publisher=Dataportal.ins.tn |access-date=2018-01-21}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.commune-elmourouj.gov.tn/en|title = مدينة المروج|website = www.commune-elmourouj.gov.tn|language = ar|access-date = 2017-02-28}}{{Dead link|date=December 2019 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

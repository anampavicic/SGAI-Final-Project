'''El-Eulma''' ({{langx|ar|العلمة}}) is a city in [[Algeria]], located 210 miles east of the capital [[Algiers]]. It is the second-largest city in [[Sétif Province]] with a population of 305,130 (1998 census). In the [[French colonial empires|French colonial period]] the city was known as ''Saint Arnaud'' after Marshal [[Jacques Leroy de Saint Arnaud]].

'''Primorje – Gorski Kotar County''' ({{lang-hr|Primorsko-goranska [[županija]]}}, {{IPA|sh|prǐːmorsko-ɡǒranskaː ʒupǎnija|pron}}) is a [[Counties of Croatia|county]] in western [[Croatia]], most of it based in the historical and cultural region called [[Croatia proper]] and some of it in [[Istria]], including the [[Bay of Kvarner]], the surrounding [[Northern Croatian Littoral]], and the mountainous region of [[Gorski Kotar]]. Its center is [[Rijeka]]. The county's population was 296,195 at the 2011 census.

The county includes the island territories of [[Krk (island)|Krk]], [[Cres (island)|Cres]], [[Lošinj]] and [[Rab (island)|Rab]]. It borders [[Slovenia]].

According to the 2011 census, Primorje – Gorski Kotar County has a population of 296,195.<ref name="census2011-counties">{{Croatian Census 2011|S|08}}</ref>

Ethnic [[Croats]] make up the majority with 86.3% of the population, followed by [[Serbs of Croatia|Serbs]] at 5.0% and [[Italians of Croatia|Italians]] 1,16%.<ref>{{Croatian Census 2011|E|4}}</ref>

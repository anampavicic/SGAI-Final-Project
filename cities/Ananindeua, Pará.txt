'''Ananindeua''' ({{IPA|pt|ananĩˈdewɐ}}) is a city in [[Pará]], northern [[Brazil]].  It is a part of the Metropolitan Region of [[Belém]], and is the second most populated city in the State of  Pará,<ref>{{cite web |url=http://portalamazonia.globo.com/new-structure/view/scripts/noticias/noticia.php?id=90499 |language=pt |title=Belém é a 10ª cidade mais populosa do Brasil |publisher=Portal Amazônia |url-status=dead |access-date=23 June 2013 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20130426080557/http://portalamazonia.globo.com/new-structure/view/scripts/noticias/noticia.php?id=90499 |archive-date=2013-04-26 }}</ref> and the third largest in the [[North Region, Brazil|Brazilian Amazon region]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.emsampa.com.br/xspxpa.htm |language=pt |title=Distâncias entre Cidades - Belém - Pará |publisher=Em Sampa.com |access-date=23 June 2013}}</ref> It has a population of 535,547 according to the last estimation of 2020, done by the Brazilian Statistic and Geography Institute ([[IBGE]]).

The municipality contains 1% of the [[Utinga State Park]], created in 1993 to protect the metropolitan area's water supply.<ref>{{citation|language=pt|date=August 2013|publisher=Secretaria de Estado de Meio Ambiente (SEMA)|title=Plano de Manejo do Parque Estadual do Utinga|last1=Veríssimo|first1=Adalberto|first2=Daniel Conceição dos|last2=Santos|first3=Jakeline Ramos|last3=Pereira|page=30|url=http://ideflorbio.pa.gov.br/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/PMUtinga_26out2013.pdf|access-date=2016-12-05|archive-date=2018-10-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181020212214/http://ideflorbio.pa.gov.br/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/PMUtinga_26out2013.pdf|url-status=dead}}</ref>

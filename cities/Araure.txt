'''Araure''' ({{IPA|es|aˈɾawɾe}}) is a town in the [[Venezuela]]n [[States of Venezuela|state]] of [[Portuguesa (state)|Portuguesa]].  This town is the seat of the Araure Municipality and, according to the 2001 Venezuelan [[census]], the municipality has a population of 111,908.<ref name=autogenerated1>{{Cite web |url=http://www.ine.gov.ve/censo/fichascenso/tiro.asp?cod_entidad=18 |title=Primeros Resultados XIII Censo General de Población y Vivienda<!-- Bot generated title --> |access-date=2007-10-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070825040625/http://www.ine.gov.ve/censo/fichascenso/tiro.asp?cod_entidad=18 |archive-date=2007-08-25 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

==History==
Araure was founded on July 6, 1694, as Nuestra Señora del Pilar de Araure.<ref>[http://occidente.com.ve/phpj/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=59&Itemid=44 Portal de Occidente – Municipio Araure<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080101103441/http://occidente.com.ve/phpj/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=59&Itemid=44 |date=January 1, 2008 }}</ref>

==Demographics==
The Araure Municipality, according to the 2001 Venezuelan census, has a population of 111,908 (up from 75,811 in 1990).  This amounts to 15.4% of Portuguesa's population.  The municipality's population density is {{convert|452.9|PD/sqmi|PD/km2}}.<ref name=autogenerated1 />

==Government==
Araure is the seat of the Araure Municipality in Portuguesa.  The mayor is José Rafael Vásquez, elected in 2004 with 44% of the vote.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Alcalde de Municipio |url=http://www.cne.gob.ve/regionales2004/16/alcaldedis=0&edo=16&mun=1&par=0&cen=0&mesa=0&cua=0.html |access-date=October 1, 2023 |website=CNE}}</ref>  He replaced Armando Rodriguez shortly after the last municipal elections in October 2004.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cne.gob.ve/estadisticas/e016.pdf |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2008-03-15 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20061002063906/http://www.cne.gob.ve/estadisticas/e016.pdf |archivedate=2006-10-02 }}</ref>

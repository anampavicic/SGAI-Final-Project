'''Hoseo''' ({{Korean|hangul=호서|lit=west of the lake}}; {{IPA|ko|ho.sʌ}}) is a region coinciding with the former [[Chungcheong Province]] in what is now [[South Korea]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.yonhapnews.co.kr/bulletin/2015/02/27/0200000000AKR20150227162400064.HTML|title=Where is Ho (Lake) in Honam / Hoseo? Byeolgogje? Seungrimji?|date=2015-03-01|work=yonhapnews|access-date=2018-04-28|language=ko-KR}}</ref> Today, the term refers to [[Daejeon]], [[Sejong City]], [[South Chungcheong Province|South Chungcheong]] and [[North Chungcheong Province]]s. Hoseo people use [[Chungcheong dialect]].<ref name=":0" /> The name is often used to refer to people residing in the region. Nowadays [[Chungcheong]] is more frequently used instead of '''Hoseo'''.<ref name=":0" /> The region will host the [[2027 World University Summer Games|World University Summer Games]] in 2027.

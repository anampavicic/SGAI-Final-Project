'''Montero''' is a city and a municipality in [[Santa Cruz Department (Bolivia)|Santa Cruz]], [[Bolivia]], about 50&nbsp;km north of [[Santa Cruz de la Sierra]]. Montero had a population of 137,931 as of 2020 and has experienced growth in recent decades, becoming an important city in the region.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.world-gazetteer.com/wg.php?x=1&men=gcis&lng=en&des=gamelan&geo=-38&col=abcdefghinoq&msz=1500&pt=c&va=&srt=pnao|title=World Gazetteer|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://archive.today/20130111125234/http://www.world-gazetteer.com/wg.php?x=1&men=gcis&lng=en&des=gamelan&geo=-38&col=abcdefghinoq&msz=1500&pt=c&va=&srt=pnao|archivedate=2013-01-11}}</ref>

Montero has an elevation of about 300 meters above sea level and an average temperature of 23 [[Celsius|°C]] (73.4 [[Fahrenheit|°F]]). The city is predominantly agricultural, producing [[soybean]]s, [[cotton]], [[maize|corn]], and [[rice]].

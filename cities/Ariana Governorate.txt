'''Ariana Governorate''' ({{Langx|ar|ولاية أريانة}} ''{{transl|ar|Wilāyat Aryānah}}'' {{IPA|ar|ærˈjæːnæ|pron}}; {{langx|fr|Gouvernorat de l'Ariana}}) is one of the twenty-four [[governorate]]s (provinces) of [[Tunisia]]. It is in northern Tunisia, approximately triangular, having as one of its sides part of the [[Gulf of Tunis]], it covers an area of 482&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup><ref name="tun" /> and has a population of 576,088 (2014 census).<ref name="census">{{cite web|url=http://tunisia.opendataforafrica.org/TNCD2014/tunisia-census-data-population-2014?region=1000000-tunisia |title=2014 Tunisian census data |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150518094332/http://tunisia.opendataforafrica.org/TNCD2014/tunisia-census-data-population-2014?region=1000000-tunisia |archive-date=2015-05-18 }}</ref> The capital is [[Ariana (tunisian city)|Ariana]].

== Geography ==
The governorate borders the governorates of [[Tunis Governorate|Tunis]], [[Bizerte Governorate|Bizerte]] and [[Manouba Governorate|Manouba]].

The average [[temperature]] is 18.7&nbsp;°C, and annual [[rainfall]] is 450 millimeters.<ref name="tun" />

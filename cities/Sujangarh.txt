'''Sujangarh''' is a city and [[List of tehsils of Rajasthan|Sub District]]<ref>{{Cite web |title=Sub Districts of churu district |url=https://igod.gov.in/district/ezg3tXQBW7DqAzx4Vryt/sub_districts}}</ref> in  [[Churu District]] in the [[Shekhawati]] region of [[Rajasthan]], India. Sujangarh lies on the [[Churu, Rajasthan|Churu city]]-[[Pali, Rajasthan|Pali]] Highway ([[National Highway 65 (India)|NH 65]]) and Hanumangarh-Kishangarh Mega Highway. The town is well known for a second Tirupati Balaji Temple.The first one being in Tirumala – the [[Venkateswara Temple, Tirumala|Tirupati Balaji Venkateshwar Mandir]]. It is the first of its kind built in South Indian style, in Rajasthan.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://devasthan.rajasthan.gov.in/images/Churu/venketashwarji.htm|title=Temple Profile}}</ref> Lord Shiva Temple on Sujangarh Road in Thardaa and Dungar Balaji on Sujangarh - Dungar Balaji Road in Gopalpura are other two nearby Hindu temples that are popular. Sri Devsagar Singhee Jain Mandir at Sujangarh is a century-old Jain Tirth that is located within the town.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.singhijainmandir.com/ |title=Home |website=singhijainmandir.com}}</ref>

==Demographics==
{{As of|2011}}, India [[census]],<ref name="2011 table 2">{{cite web |title=Cities having population 1 lakh and above |url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011-prov-results/paper2/data_files/India2/Table_2_PR_Cities_1Lakh_and_Above.pdf |publisher=Census of India 2011 |access-date=8 September 2019}}</ref> Sujangarh had a population of 101,528; 52,078 males and 49,450 females, giving a sex ratio of 950. The average literacy rate was 74%,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ndtv.com/elections/rajasthan/sujangarh-mla-results|title=Sujangarh City Population Census 2011–2020 {{!}} Rajasthan|website=www.census2011.co.in|access-date=2020-02-21}}</ref>  male literacy was 85%, and female literacy was 63%. 14,723 (14.5%) of the population was under 6 years of age.

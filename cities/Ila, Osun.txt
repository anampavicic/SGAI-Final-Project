'''Ila''' is a [[Local Government Areas of Nigeria|local government area]] in [[Osun State]], [[Nigeria]]. Its headquarters are in the town of [[Ila Orangun]].

It has an area of  303&nbsp;km{{sup|2}} and a population of 62,049 at the 2006 census.

The [[postal code]] of the area is 234.<ref>{{cite web|title=Post Offices- with map of LGA |publisher=NIPOST |url=http://www.nipost.gov.ng/PostCode.aspx |accessdate=2009-10-20 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091007011423/http://www.nipost.gov.ng/PostCode.aspx |archivedate=2009-10-07 }}</ref>

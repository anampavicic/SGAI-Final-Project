'''Nyeri County''' is a [[Counties of Kenya|county]] located in the central region of [[Kenya]]. Its capital and largest town is [[Nyeri]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://kenya.usaid.gov/sites/default/files/profiles/Nyeri%20County%2023%20Jan%202012.pdf |title=Archived copy |access-date=2013-07-27 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130224021452/http://kenya.usaid.gov/sites/default/files/profiles/Nyeri%20County%2023%20Jan%202012.pdf |archive-date=24 February 2013 }}</ref> It has a population of 759,164 and an area of 2361&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>. Mutahi Kahiga was governor {{As of|2022|lc=y}}.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Nyawira |first=Lydia |title=Nyeri: Governor Kahiga picks David Kinarire as running mate |url=https://www.standardmedia.co.ke/central/article/2001445824/nyeri-governor-kahiga-picks-david-kinarire-as-running-mate |date=2022-05-19 |website=The Standard |language=en}}</ref> Previous governors include Nderitu Gachagua, who died while receiving treatment in London,<ref>{{Cite web |last=Okumu |first=Patel |title=Nyeri Governor Nderitu Gachagua is dead |url=https://www.standardmedia.co.ke/counties/article/2001230544/nyeri-governor-nderitu-gachagua-is-dead |access-date=2022-10-11 |website=The Standard |language=en}}</ref> Wamathai Samuel Githaiga, and Wahome Gakuru, who died in a road accident at Kabati near Kenol heading towards Thika Super Highway.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Gichure |first=Nderitu |title=Revealed: Former Nyeri Governor Gakuru's driver did not apply brakes during tragic crash |url=https://www.standardmedia.co.ke/entertainment/news/article/2001343936/revealed-former-nyeri-governor-gakurus-driver-did-not-apply-brakes-during-tragic-crash |access-date=2022-10-11 |website=Standard Entertainment |language=en}}</ref> It is also the home county of the third president of Kenya, the late President Mwai Kibaki.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Komu |first=Nicholas |date=2022-04-27 |title=Othaya town gets a makeover ahead of Kibaki burial |url=https://nation.africa/kenya/news/politics/othaya-town-gets-a-makeover-ahead-of-kibaki-burial-3796052 |access-date=2023-06-12 |website=Nation |language=en}}</ref>

==People==
The county is located on the southwest flank of [[Mount Kenya]]. Local people are predominantly of the [[Kikuyu people|Kikuyu]] ethnicity.

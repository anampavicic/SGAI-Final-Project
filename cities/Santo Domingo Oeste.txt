'''Santo Domingo Oeste''' is a [[Municipalities of the Dominican Republic|municipality]] of the [[Santo Domingo Province|Santo Domingo province]] in the [[Dominican Republic]]. It is part of the greater Santo Domingo metropolitan area, which is the [[de facto]] [[List of countries with multiple capitals|co-capital]] of the Dominican Republic as seat of the Constitutional Court and the Central Electoral Commission.<ref>{{Cite web |title=¿Cómo se pobló Santo Domingo Oeste? |url=https://acento.com.do/opinion/como-se-poblo-santo-domingo-oeste-210415.html |access-date=2024-04-21 |website=Acento |language=es}}</ref> 

For comparison with other municipalities and municipal districts see the [[list of municipalities and municipal districts of the Dominican Republic]].

Santo Domingo Oeste was created as municipality in 2001 by law 163-01 splitting the Santo Domingo province from the [[Distrito Nacional]] including parts of metropolitan [[Santo Domingo]] west of the [[DR-1]] (''Autopista Duarte'').<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.informejudicial.com/leyes/Division%20Territorial/Ley%20163-01,%20crea%20Provincia%20Santo%20Domingo%20y%20Modifica%20Articulos%201%20y%202%20Ley%205220.htm|title=Ley No. 163-01 que crea la provincia de Santo Domingo, y modifica los Artículos 1 y 2 de la Ley No. 5220, sobre División Territorial de la República Dominicana.|author=EL CONGRESO NACIONAL|language=es|access-date=2007-03-08 |archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20070518042944/http://www.informejudicial.com/leyes/Division+Territorial/Ley+163-01,+crea+Provincia+Santo+Domingo+y+Modifica+Articulos+1+y+2+Ley+5220.htm <!-- Bot retrieved archive --> |archive-date = 2007-05-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Historia : Ayuntamiento Municipal de Santo Domingo Este |url=https://ayuntamientosde.gob.do/historia/ |access-date=2024-04-13 |website=ayuntamientosde.gob.do}}</ref>

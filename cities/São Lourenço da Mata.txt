'''São Lourenço da Mata''' is a city located in the greater [[Recife metropolitan area]] in the state of [[Pernambuco]], with a population of 114,079 inhabitants (2020 estimate by [[Brazilian Institute of Geography and Statistics|IBGE]]). The city was one of the hosts of the [[2014 FIFA World Cup]]. The new [[Arena Pernambuco]] is set to boost the local economy with the construction of a new stadium, flats, car parks, hospital, technical school, shopping center, integrated metro-bus station, and road improvements.

* Distance to [[Recife]] - 18&nbsp;km

==Economy==

{{See also|Largest Cities of Northeast Region, Brazil}}
The main economic activities in São Lourenço da Mata are based in general industry, especially the electrical and transportation sectors.

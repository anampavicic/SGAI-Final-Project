'''Mohammadshahr''' ({{langx|fa|محمدشهر}}){{efn|Also [[romanize]]d as '''Moḩammadshahr'''; formerly '''Moḩammadīyeh'''<ref>{{GEOnet3|-3075531|Mohammadshahr}}</ref>}} is a city in the [[Central District (Khusf County)|Central District]] of [[Khusf County]], [[South Khorasan province|South Khorasan]] province, [[Iran]].

==Demographics==
===Population===

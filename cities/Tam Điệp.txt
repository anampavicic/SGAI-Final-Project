'''Tam Điệp''' is a [[Provincial city (Vietnam)|city]] of [[Ninh Bình Province]] in the [[Red River Delta]] region of [[Vietnam]]. It was established on 11 May 2015. As of 2015 the city had a population of 104,175.<ref name="Statoids">{{cite web|url=http://www.statoids.com/yvn.html|title=Districts of Vietnam|publisher=Statoids|access-date=March 20, 2009}}</ref> The city covers an area of 105&nbsp;km².<ref name="Statoids"/>

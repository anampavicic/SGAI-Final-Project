'''Muridke''' (Urdu: muridke), is a city and headquarters of [[Muridke Tehsil]] of [[Sheikhupura District]] in [[Punjab Pakistan|Punjab]], [[Pakistan]]. It is the [[List of most populous cities in Pakistan|53rd largest city of Pakistan]] by population. Muridke is situated near the city of [[Lahore]], at an elevation of 205&nbsp;m (675&nbsp;ft).<ref>[https://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/04/Muridke2.html Location of Muridke - Falling Rain Genomics]</ref> It situated on the [[Grand Trunk Road]].

In 2005, Muridke became the headquarters of the newly created [[Muridke Tehsil]] of [[Sheikhupura District]].

== Schools ==
[[Chand Bagh School]] is situated on the Muridke-Sheikhupura Road.

Jinnah Ideal Higher Secondary School and Al noor group of schools and colleges are other famous schools of Muridke.

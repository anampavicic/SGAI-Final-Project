'''Alor Gajah''' is a town and district seat of [[Alor Gajah District]] in the [[Malaysia]]n state of [[Malacca]]. It is governed by '''Alor Gajah Municipal Council''' ({{lang-ms|Majlis Perbandaran Alor Gajah}}), which was formerly known as '''Alor Gajah District Council''' ({{lang-ms|Majlis Daerah Alor Gajah}}) from 1 July 1978 until 1 May 2003.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mpag.gov.my/mpag/profil/latar-belakang/|title=Latar Belakang|date=14 September 2021 }}</ref>

==Etymology==
Alor Gajah used to be one of the forest areas believed to be the routes ([[Malay language|Malay]]: ''alor'') for wild elephants ([[Malay language|Malay]]: ''gajah'').<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.mpag.gov.my/pelancongan/info-alor-gajah/sejarah/|title=Sejarah|date=17 November 2020 }}</ref>

==Economy==

Alor Gajah is home to Honda Malaysia's car manufacturing plant,<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.honda.com.my/nhc/contact_us.php |title=Honda |access-date=12 September 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180912131137/https://www.honda.com.my/nhc/contact_us.php |archive-date=12 September 2018 |url-status=dead }}</ref> which assembling cars for the local market and also selected automotive parts for Honda's overall [[ASEAN]] market and also local biscuit producer - [[Julie's]] manufacturing plant.

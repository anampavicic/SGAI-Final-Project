'''Franco da Rocha''' is a municipality in the state of [[São Paulo (state)|São Paulo]]. It is part of the [[Metropolitan Region of São Paulo]].<ref>[http://governo-sp.jusbrasil.com.br/legislacao/1028148/lei-complementar-1139-11 Lei Complementar nº 1.139, de 16 de junho de 2011]</ref> The population is 156,492 (2020 est.) in an area of 132.78&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>.<ref name=ibge>[http://cod.ibge.gov.br/23BOP Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatística]</ref> The suburban city is served by [[CPTM]] [[Line 7 (CPTM)|Line 7 (Ruby)]].

The municipality contains the {{convert|2058|ha}} [[Juqueri State Park]], created in 1993.<ref>{{citation|language=pt|ref={{harvid|Parque Estadual do Juqueri – SAP}}
 |title=Parque Estadual do Juqueri|publisher=SAP: Sistema Ambeintal Paulista
 |url=http://www.ambiente.sp.gov.br/parque-estadual-do-juquery/|access-date=2016-01-10}}</ref>

==History==
The municipality was created by state law in 1944.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.al.sp.gov.br/documentacao/municipios-paulistas|title=Municípios Paulistas|access-date=2024-06-26|website=www.al.sp.gov.br|language=Portuguese}}</ref>

In February 2016, the municipality signed a Friendship Declaration with [[Stepanakert]], the capital of the partially independent [[Republic of Artsakh]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=International Cooperation &#124; NKR |url=http://www.nkr.am/en/international-cooperation |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210301004114/http://www.nkr.am/en/international-cooperation |archive-date=2021-03-01 |access-date=2019-10-09}}</ref>

== Media ==
In telecommunications, the city was served by [[:pt:Companhia_Telefônica_Brasileira|Companhia Telefônica Brasileira]] until 1973, when it began to be served by [[:pt:Telecomunicações_de_São_Paulo|Telecomunicações de São Paulo]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.imprensaoficial.com.br/DO/BuscaDO2001Documento_11_4.aspx?link=/1973/ineditoriais/abril/24/p1/pag_0054_DUI6O3SN7V5CNe7E7ANSRTTG04F.pdf&pagina=54&data=24/04/1973&caderno=Ineditoriais&paginaordenacao=100054|title=Creation of Telesp - April 12, 1973|access-date=2024-05-26|website=www.imprensaoficial.com.br|language=Portuguese}}</ref> In July 1998, this company was acquired by Telefónica, which adopted the [[Vivo (telecommunications company)|Vivo]] brand in 2012.<ref name=":vivo" />

The company is currently an operator of cell phones, fixed lines, internet (fiber optics/4G) and television (satellite and cable).<ref name=":vivo">{{Cite web|url=https://ri.telefonica.com.br/en/the-company/our-history/|title=Our History - Telefônica RI|access-date=2024-05-26|website=Telefônica}}</ref>

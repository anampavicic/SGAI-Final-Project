'''Erbil Governorate''' ({{langx|ku|پارێزگای ھەولێر|translit=Parêzgeha Hewlêr}};<ref>{{cite news |title=Tekoşerekî kevnar yê PDKÎ li parêzgeha Hewlêr wefat kir |url=https://kurdistanmedia.com/ku/news/tekosereki-kevnar-ye-pdki-li-parezgeha-hewler-wefat-kir |access-date=21 December 2019 |work=kurdistanmedia.com |language=ku}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=پارێزگای ھەولێر |url=http://momt.gov.krd/kur/?tag=%D9%BE%D8%A7%D8%B1%DB%8E%D8%B2%DA%AF%D8%A7%DB%8C-%DA%BE%DB%95%D9%88%D9%84%DB%8E%D8%B1 |access-date=21 December 2019 |language=ku }}{{Dead link|date=March 2024 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> {{langx|ar|محافظة أربيل|Muḥāfaẓat Arbīl}}<ref>{{cite news |title=قوة هولندية تتجه إلى إقليم كردستان العراق لحماية مطار أربيل |url=https://www.alaraby.co.uk/politics/قوة-هولندية-تتجه-إلى-إقليم-كردستان-العراق-لحماية-مطار-أربيل |access-date=21 November 2020 |work=[[The New Arab]] |language=ar}}</ref>) is a [[Governorates of Iraq|governorate]] in the [[Kurdistan Region]]. It is the capital and economic hub of the semi-autonomous [[Kurdistan Region]] of [[Iraq]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://krg.org/articles/detail.asp?lngnr=12&smap=03010300&rnr=140&anr=23911 |title=The Kurdistan Region in brief |publisher=Krg.org |access-date=2013-10-03 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141006170845/http://www.krg.org/articles/detail.asp?lngnr=12&smap=03010300&rnr=140&anr=23911 |archive-date=2014-10-06 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

==Academic establishments==
*[[Salahaddin University]]: Founded in 1958 in Sulaymaniya and transferred to Erbil in 1981.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.suh-edu.com |title=Salahaddin University - Hawler |publisher=Suh-edu.com |access-date=2013-10-03}}</ref>
*[[Hawler Medical University]]: Founded in 2005.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hmu.edu.krd/ |title=Hawler Medical University (HMU) |publisher=hmu.edu.krd |access-date=2013-10-03}}</ref>
*University of Kurdistan Hewlêr: Public English-medium university.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ukh.ac/ |title=University of Kurdistan |publisher=Ukh.ac |access-date=2013-10-03}}</ref>
*Tishk International University (Previously called Ishik University): Private university that was established in 2008 in Erbil.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tiu.edu.iq/ |title=> Home |publisher=Tishk University |access-date=2021-03-09}}</ref>
*Cihan University: Private university, founded in 2007.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cihanuniversity.org/ |title=Cihan University |publisher=Cihan University |access-date=2013-10-03 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130527042727/http://cihanuniversity.org/ |archive-date=2013-05-27 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
*SABIS University: Private university.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sabisuniversity.net |title=SABIS® University |publisher=Sabisuniversity.net |access-date=2013-10-03}}</ref>
*[[Soran University]], KRG-Iraq governmental university founded in 2009.<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.soran.edu.iq |title=Soran University |access-date=2015-05-15}}</ref>
*[[Knowledge University]], KRG-Iraq Private university founded in 2009.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://knu.edu.iq/ |title=Knowledge University |access-date=2015-05-15}}</ref>
*[[Catholic University in Erbil]], Private non-profit university founded in 2015.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Catholic University in Erbil |url=https://cue.edu.krd/}}</ref>

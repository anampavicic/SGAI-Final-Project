'''Bordj Bou Arréridj''' ({{langx|ar|برج بوعريريج}}) is the capital city of [[Bordj Bou Arréridj Province]], [[Algeria]]. According to the 2008 estimation, its population was 168,346. It is situated 148 miles by road east of [[Algiers]], near the [[Hodna Mountains|Hodna Massif]] in the southern [[Kabylie Mountains]], at an elevation of 916 metres.

The economy is largely based upon [[agriculture]] and [[forestry]], with some small-scale [[Industrial sector|industries]]. Bordj Bou Arréridj is well-connected to other urban centres by [[road]] and [[Rail transport|rail]]. The cities of [[Sétif]] and [[Bouira]] are 70&nbsp;km east and 115&nbsp;km northwest, respectively. [[CA Bordj Bou Arreridj|CA Bordj Bou Arréridj]] (CABBA) is a 1st Level Algerian professional football club based in town; its home grounds are [[20 August 1955 Stadium]] in [[Mohamed Belouizdad]].

== Economy ==
The city is considered highly innovative for presence of many [[high-tech]]. The city is home to a number of technology companies. The city is home to the headquarters of [[Condor Electronics]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.anandtech.com/show/14049/condor-allure-x-premium-smartphone-with-amoled-for-300eur|title = The Condor Allure X: A Premium Smartphone with a 6.39-Inch AMOLED Display for €300}}</ref>

== Sports ==
Bordj Bou Arréridj has a team in the [[Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 1]]: [[CA Bordj Bou Arreridj|CA Bordj Bou Arréridj]], founded in 1931.
They also have two teams in the [[Algerian Basketball Championship]]: IRB Bordj Bou Arréridj and OS Bordj Bou Arréridj.

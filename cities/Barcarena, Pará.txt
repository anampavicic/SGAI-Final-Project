'''Barcarena''' is a Brazilian [[Municipalities of Brazil|municipality]] in the state of [[Pará]] adjacent the [[Tocantins River]].  It has a population of 127,027 and is part of the [[Belém]] metropolitan area.<ref>Browder, Brian John O. [https://books.google.com/books?id=gR4yUgC1_HIC&pg=PA136&dq=%22barcarena%22+history+para&lr=lang_en&sig=88axrIGhLFTtX_58DVedLg95ltg Rainforest Cities] ''books.google.com''</ref> The city's economy relies largely on agriculture and [[bauxite]]. There are also facilities for [[pig iron]] nearby.<ref>http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_m3MKT/is_24-1_112/ai_n6106309{{dead link|date=April 2022}}</ref>  They city and port is developing as a major transshipping port that takes goods for international export linked by rail from nearby [[inland port]]s, from all over Brazil, speeding up shipment and reducing time and costs as opposed to traditional megaports in the Southeast.

== History ==
The town was originally inhabited by the [[Aroã language|Aruá Indians]]. In 1709 Jesuits came.{{citation needed|date=April 2022}}

On October 6, 2015, the livestock vessel "MV Haidar" capsized in the Vila do Conde port due to an unsuccessful turning maneuver. It had 4,900 live cattle and almost 700 metric tonnes of oil on board.<ref>[https://www.motherjones.com/blue-marble/2015/10/ship-carrying-5000-cows-sank-brazil/ - 4,400 Dead Cows Are Decomposing in a Sunken Ship in a Brazilian River] on MotherJones.com</ref><ref>[http://g1.globo.com/pa/para/noticia/2016/07/mpf-convoca-audiencia-com-familias-afetadas-por-naufragio-em-barcarena.html - MPF convoca audiência com famílias afetadas por naufrágio em Barcarena] on Globo.com (Portuguese)</ref> 4,400 cattle drowned while locked in the ship's cargo area. About 500 animals could free themselves, but only about 100 survived the accident. Fuel and oil seeped from the wreck into the water and contaminated the Pará river and surrounding beaches, which deprived many local fisher families of their livelihood.<ref>[https://www.vesselfinder.com/news/4436-Update-Local-residents-and-authorities-angry-after-sinking-of-cattle-carrier-Haidar-in-Barcarena-Brazil-video - Update: Local residents and authorities angry after sinking of cattle carrier Haidar in Barcarena, Brazil] on Vesselfinder.com</ref> Since the removal of the oil slick proved to be complicated, and no consensus regarding responsibility and suitable cleanup efforts could be reached, hundreds of carcasses rotted at the beach and inside the shipwreck for months – a huge burden on the fishery and tourism of the region. The competent Federal Ministry estimated the damage to the residents to be 71 million reais (about 21 million US$). By July 2016 nothing had been paid out yet.<ref>[http://g1.globo.com/pa/para/noticia/2016/04/apos-6-meses-de-naufragio-navio-e-bois-ainda-estao-submersos-no-pa.html - Após 6 meses de naufrágio, navio e bois ainda estão submersos no PA] (Portuguese) on Globo.com</ref><ref>[http://g1.globo.com/pa/para/noticia/2016/07/mpf-convoca-audiencia-com-familias-afetadas-por-naufragio-em-barcarena.html - MPF convoca audiência com famílias afetadas por naufrágio em Barcarena] on Globo.com (portuguese)</ref><ref>[http://www.mpf.mp.br/pa/sala-de-imprensa/noticias-pa/afetados-pelo-naufragio-em-barcarena-pa-decidem-que-debate-sobre-indenizacao-deve-continuar - Afetados pelo naufrágio em Barcarena (PA) decidem que debate sobre indenização deve continuar] on Mpf.mp.br (Portuguese)</ref>
